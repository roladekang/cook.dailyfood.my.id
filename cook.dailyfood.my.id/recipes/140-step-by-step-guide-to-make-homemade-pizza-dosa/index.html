The ingredients needed to make Pizza Dosa:
  1. Get For Dosa Batter-
  2. Prepare 11/2 cups rice (soaked for 6hrs)
  3. Take 1/2 cup Splitted white lentil (udad dal)(soaked for 6 hrs)
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp flatten rice (Poha) (Soaked it and put while grinding)
  5. Get 1/2 tsp Fenugreek seeds(soaked with rice)
  6. Take 2 tbsp curd
  7. Prepare as per taste Salt
  8. Prepare as required Water
  9. Make ready For Pizza Dosa-
  10. Prepare 2 cups Dosa Batter
  11. Take 1 cup Pizza Sauce
  12. Make ready 1 cup Mozzarella cheese grated
  13. Prepare 2 Onions diced
  14. Get 1 Capsicum diced
  15. Make ready 2 Tomatoes, deseed and diced
  16. Get 1/2 Carrot finely chopped
  17. Prepare 5-6 Black Olives sliced
  18. Take as required Ketchup
  19. Prepare as required Pizza Mix Masala
  20. Make ready as required Butter

Instructions to make Pizza Dosa:
  1. Drain all water from the Soaked ingredients. Take a grinding jar add all ingredients with curd. Crush it. To get a smooth sticky batter if required add water. Take it out in the bowl. Whisk it. Cover it and keep it for fermentation in a hot place for 2-3 hrs.
  2. After 3 hours of fermentation add salt to the batter and water mix it well. Mix it vigorously so will get batter lighter and flowing consistency.
  3. Get all ready Dosa Batter, diced Veggies, grated cheese, ketchup, pizza mix masala.
  4. Put the dosa tava on medium flame to get hot. Sprinkle some water so the tava get little cool. Wipe it with cloth. Pour the batter on hot tava and spread with the ladle. Here for pizza dosa spread the batter little thicker.
  5. Once the dosa ready from one side. Put the put butter, pizza sauce and ketchup. Spread it on whole dosa.
  6. Now spread all diced veggies. Spread grated cheese. Put sliced olives.
  7. Sprinkle pizza masala. Cover it and let the cheese get melts.
  8. Serve.
