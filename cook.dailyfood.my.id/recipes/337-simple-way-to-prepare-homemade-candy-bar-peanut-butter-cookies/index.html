The ingredients needed to make Candy bar peanut butter cookies:
  1. Make ready 1 cup Softened butter
  2. Take 1 cup Granulated sugar
  3. Take 1 cup Creamy peanut butter
  4. Take 1 cup Packed brown sugar
  5. Make ready 2 large Eggs
  6. Take 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  7. Make ready 2 cup All purpose flour
  8. Prepare 1 tsp Baking soda
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp Salt
  10. Take 36 each Bite size snickers bars
Instructions to make Candy bar peanut butter cookies:
  1. Beat butter, sugars, and peanut butter at medium speed until smooth. Add eggs and vanilla, beat until blended
  2. Stir together flour, baking soda and salt in small bowl
  3. Add to butter mixture, cover and chill 30 minutes
  4. Preheat oven 350¬∞
  5. Shape 2 tablespoon around each snicker bar and roll into balls
  6. Place 3 inches apart on an ungreased cookie sheet
  7. Cook 13 to 14 minutes…cool 5 minutes
