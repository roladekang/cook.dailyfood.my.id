The ingredients needed to make Time pass chana dal namkin:
  1. Take 100 gr chanadal namkin
  2. Get 1 Pyaz
  3. Get 1 tomato
  4. Prepare 1 hari mirch
  5. Make ready Aamchur powder for sprinkle
  6. Take 1 lemon juice
  7. Take fresh dhaniya for garnish
  8. Take 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  9. Take salt
  10. Take red chilli powder
  11. Take Chat masala
Instructions to make Time pass chana dal namkin:
  1. Take all veg and cut in small pees
  2. Now in mixing bowl add namkin chana dal
  3. Add mixer of veg and add salt chat masala aamchur powder hari mirch lemon juice. Lalmirchi powder and all mix very well then garnish fresh Coriander
  4. Transfer to plats and serve with potato weffers
