Recipe of Tasty Freddo Cappuccino

by Harriet Hart

The ingredients needed to make Freddo Cappuccino:
  1. Get 2 shots espresso
  2. Make ready 100 ml full fat milk, cold from the fridge
  3. Prepare Sugar (according to your liking)
  4. Make ready cubes Ice
  5. Get Grounded cinnamon or chocolate powder
Instructions to make Freddo Cappuccino:
  1. Prepare the coffee in the espresso machine. Get two shots of espresso.
  2. If you want to put sugar in your coffee now is the time, that the coffee is still hot and the sugar will melt.
  3. Put the sugar in the coffee and give it a good stir.
  4. Put the coffee in a mixing jar with two ice cubes and give it a good stir with a hand mixer.
  5. Place your coffee in a glass half filled with ice.
  6. Prepare your froth milk. The milk needs to be very cold, so take it out from the fridge the very last moment.
  7. Put your milk in the mixing jar and stir it until you get a rich froth and the milk thickens.
  8. You can top it up with cinnamon or chocolate powder.
  9. Enjoy your coffee like a Greek!
