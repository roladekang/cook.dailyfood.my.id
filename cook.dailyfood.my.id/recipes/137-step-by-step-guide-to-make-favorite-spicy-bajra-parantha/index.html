The ingredients needed to make Spicy Bajra parantha:
  1. Prepare 1 cup Bajra flour
  2. Get 1 cup Potato boiled and mashed
  3. Take 1/2 tsp Salt
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp Red chilli powder
  5. Take 1/4 tsp Asafoetida
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp Ajwain (Carom Seeds)
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp Coriander leaves (chopped)
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp. Desi ghee
Steps to make Spicy Bajra parantha:
  1. Mix Bajra flour, potato, salt,red chilli powder, ajwain and water to make soft dough.
  2. Divide the dough into 4 portions.
  3. Take 1 portion, make a ball and roll on like a chapati.
  4. Heat a tawa,cook the rolled parantha from both the sides with the help of desi ghee.
  5. Your spicy and tasty parantha is ready to serve.
  6. Serve it with curd, butter and pickle.
