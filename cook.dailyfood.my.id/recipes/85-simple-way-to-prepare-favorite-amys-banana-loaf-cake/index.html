The ingredients needed to make Amy’s Banana Loaf Cake:
  1. Get 2 Ripe Bananas
  2. Get 225 g Self-raising flour
  3. Make ready 2 Eggs
  4. Make ready 175 g Caster Sugar
  5. Get 1 tsp Baking Powder
  6. Get 2 tbs Milk
  7. Prepare Few drops of Banana flavouring
  8. Make ready 100 g Softened Butter
  9. Prepare Decorate as you wish

Instructions to make Amy’s Banana Loaf Cake:
  1. Lightly grease the loaf tin and line it with non-stick baking parchment. Pre-heat the oven to 180°C/350°F/Gas Mark 4.
  2. Measure all the ingredients into a mixing bowl and beat for about 2 minutes, until well blended; an electric mixer is best for this but of course you can also beat by hand with a wooden spoon.
  3. Spoon the mixture into the prepared tin and level the surface.
  4. Bake for about 1 hour, until well risen and golden brown. A fine skewer inserted in the centre of the cake should come out clean.
  5. Leave the cake to cool in the tin for a few minutes, then loosen with a small palette knife and turn the cake out. Remove the lining paper and leave on a wire rack to cool completely.
  6. Decorate as you wish, I chose to make some white icing and using a piping bag drizzle it all over the cake and then add almond flakes for decoration.
  7. When icing has set slice thickly to serve.
