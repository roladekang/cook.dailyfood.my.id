Instructions to make Korma paratha:
  1. Take moong daal and coarsely ground it.
  2. Wash it 2 to 3 times and soak it for 2 hours.
  3. Take daal in bowl and add wheat flour.
  4. Add onion and ginger-garlic paste.
  5. Add green chilli and coriander leaves.
  6. Add salt, red chilli powder and coriander powder.
  7. Add asafoetida, turmeric powder and cumin seeds.
  8. Add oil and knead medium soft dough. Give rest for 15 minutes.
  9. Take medium size ball from dough, flatten it and cut with bowl in round.
  10. Heat tawa and apply oil. Put paratha on it and roast it from both side.
  11. Our korma paratha is ready… enjoy…
