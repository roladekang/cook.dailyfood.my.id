The ingredients needed to make Chana dal pulau:
  1. Get 1 cup basmati rice
  2. Take 1/2 cup chana dal
  3. Get 2 onions sliced
  4. Prepare Red chili powder
  5. Get 2 tbs oil
  6. Prepare Salt as per the taste
  7. Take 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
Instructions to make Chana dal pulau:
  1. Wash and soak dal and rice min 30 minutes
  2. Heat oil in a pressure cooker add cumin seeds and onions site it till pink in color
  3. Add water dry spices red chili powder salt turmeric powder
  4. Add dal and rice cook it till two whistles cool down or release pressure
  5. Serve with raita
