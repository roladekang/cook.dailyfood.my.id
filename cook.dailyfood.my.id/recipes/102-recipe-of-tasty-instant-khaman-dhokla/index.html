The ingredients needed to make Instant khaman dhokla:
  1. Make ready 1/1/2 cup besan (gram flour)
  2. Make ready 1 cup water
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp oil
  4. Get 1/2 tsp turmeric
  5. Prepare 1 tsp green chilli paste
  6. Prepare 2 tsp ginger paste
  7. Take 1 tsp each salt,sugar
  8. Make ready 1/4 tsp baking soda
  9. Make ready 1/1/2 tsp eno fruit salt
  10. Make ready 2 tsp lemon juice
  11. Get Tempering
  12. Get 2 tbs oil
  13. Make ready 1 tsp mustard seed
  14. Make ready 2-3 green chilli cut in to long pieces
  15. Prepare 1/4 cup or less as per ur taste white vinegar or lemon juice
  16. Get 3/4 cup water

Steps to make Instant khaman dhokla:
  1. Grease flat dish with oil and keep aside mix gram flour, water, oil, turmeric salt sugar, and chilli ginger paste and water to make lump free smooth batter
  2. Add eno and baking soda in to the batter and pour lemon juice and immediately mix well and pour in a flat dish and microwave uncovered for 6 minutes. (I use half measurement of this recipe and microwave for 3 minutes)
  3. Temper :heat oil in a pan add mustard seeds when crackle add green chilli saute add water sugar and vinegar instead of vinegar I used half lemon juice pour on ready dhokla and keep it for half an hour to absorb it garnish with coriander leaves and enjoy yummy easy dokhla as a light snacks.
