The ingredients needed to make Microwave Khaman Dhokla in Mug !!:
  1. Make ready 200 gms Gram Flour or Besan
  2. Take 1 and 1/2 tsp Grated Ginger or Ginger Paste
  3. Get 1-2 Chopped Green Chillies
  4. Get Pinch Hing
  5. Make ready to Taste Salt
  6. Get 1/2 cup Curd
  7. Prepare as required Water (1/2 cup of water, I required)
  8. Get 2 tsp Oil
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp Sugar
  10. Make ready 1 tsp Fruit Salt or Eno
  11. Get Tempering :
  12. Make ready 1-1/2 to 2 tbsp Oil
  13. Make ready 1 tsp Mustard Seeds
  14. Prepare as needed Few Curry Leaves
  15. Get 1 tbsp Sugar
  16. Make ready 3 tbsp Water
  17. Take as needed Chopped Green Coriander Leaves for garnishing

Instructions to make Microwave Khaman Dhokla in Mug !!:
  1. In a mixing bowl, take besan, grated ginger or ginger paste, chopped green chillies, hing, salt, curd, oil and sugar. Mix all these well. Then add water (half of the required water). Whisk all well with a whisk or spoon. Then add remaining amount of water and again whisk it well. Add more water if required. Batter should be of smooth flowing consistency. Make sure no lumps are there in batter.
  2. Then add fruit salt. Add little water on fruit salt. Bubbles will come. Gently mix batter. Don't over mix.
  3. Pour batter in mug. Put mug in microwave for 2 mins on high temperature. In microwave it will look like batter is over flowing from mug, but don't worry it won't come out and don't open the door of the microwave.
  4. Tempting - Heat oil. Add mustard seeds to oil. Add Curry leaves. Let it crackle. Add sugar to oil. Mix well. Add water and mix well. Pour this tempting immediately on each mug and garnish with chopped coriander leaves. Serve with green chutney.
