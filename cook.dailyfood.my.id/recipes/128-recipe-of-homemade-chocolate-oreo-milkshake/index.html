The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Oreo MilkShake:
  1. Make ready 2 glass Milk
  2. Get 9 Oreo chocolate & vanila Biscuits crushed
  3. Get 1 cup chocolate ice cream
  4. Prepare 1 scoop vanilla ice cream to decorate
  5. Take 1 tbsp chocolate syrup
  6. Make ready pack Cocoa Crunch crushed small
  7. Get cone for decorate
  8. Take donut for decorate
Instructions to make Chocolate Oreo MilkShake:
  1. Add milk ice cream scoop crushed Oreo, crushed cocoa pops and chocolate syrup in a blender.
  2. Blend it well. and pour in the jar.
  3. Now decorate with cone, ice cream, donut & chocolate syrup. Serve and enjoy
