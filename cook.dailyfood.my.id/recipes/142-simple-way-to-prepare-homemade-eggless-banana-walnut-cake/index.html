The ingredients needed to make Eggless Banana Walnut Cake:
  1. Prepare For dry
  2. Take 1 1/2 cup plain flour
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp baking powder
  4. Make ready 1 pinch salt
  5. Take For wet :
  6. Make ready 2 banana
  7. Take 2 tbsp crushed walnuts
  8. Get 1/2 cup melted butter
  9. Take 2 tbsp powder sugar or as needed
  10. Get 2 tbsp condensed milk

Steps to make Eggless Banana Walnut Cake:
  1. Combine banana in a bowl and mash with a masher. Add powder sugar, melted butter. Mix well
  2. Sieve plain flour, baking powder and salt. Combined with wet ingredients. Mix well. Then add crushed walnuts again mix well.
  3. Mean while preheat otg for 180 degree C for 5 minutes. Brush oil in and sprinkle plain flour. Pour the cake batter and spread evenly and tap it. Bake for 200 degree C for 10 minutes. Check in between. If it is done. After 2 minutes take out from the oven. Cut into pieces.
  4. Transfer to a serving plate. Garnish with leftover crushed walnut. Banana walnut is Ready to serve with evening tea or as a dessert
