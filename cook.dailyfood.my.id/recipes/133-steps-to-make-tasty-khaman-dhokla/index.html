The ingredients needed to make Khaman dhokla:
  1. Prepare 150 gm besan
  2. Take 2 tbsp sooji
  3. Take 1 tbsp sugar
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp lemon juice
  5. Take 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp oil
  7. Prepare Salt to taste
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp eno
  9. Prepare 2-3 green chilli (tempering)
  10. Get 1 tsp mustard
  11. Get 1 pinch hing
  12. Take 1 tbsp curry leaves
  13. Take 2 tbsp coriander leaves
  14. Make ready 1 tbsp sugar
  15. Make ready 2 tbsp oil
  16. Take Warm water

Steps to make Khaman dhokla:
  1. Take a bowl add besan & sooji, sugar, turmeric powder mix it well
  2. Add warm water to the mixture.make sure that te batter should be of meduim thickness.
  3. Take a steamer add water & keep it to the boilfor 1 min
  4. Add lemon juice,oil, salt to the batter & mix it well.let the batter rest for 10 min after that add eno to the batter & mix it clockwise.
  5. Take a greased bowl pour the batter immediately into the bowl after that adding eno.keep the batter bowl inside the steamer.
  6. Steam it for 20 min
  7. For the tempering :- take a tadka pan pure oil & add hing, mustard seeds, green chillies,curry leaves, water, sugar, once it done add coriander leaves.
  8. Once the dhoklas are done pour the tadka over them.
  9. Serve with green chutney or tomato ketchup.
