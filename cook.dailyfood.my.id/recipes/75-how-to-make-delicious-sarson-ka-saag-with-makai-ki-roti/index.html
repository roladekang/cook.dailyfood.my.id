The ingredients needed to make Sarson ka saag with Makai ki roti:
  1. Take Makai ki roti:
  2. Make ready 3 cups Makai ka aata (Maize flour)
  3. Take 1 tsp Salt
  4. Prepare Water lukewarm
  5. Get Thick plastic sheets 2
  6. Prepare Desi ghee
  7. Prepare Sarson ka Saag:
  8. Get 3-4 cups Water
  9. Make ready 1 kg Sarson ka saag
  10. Prepare 1 bunch Palak
  11. Prepare 1 small bunch Methi
  12. Make ready 10-12 cloves lehsan
  13. Make ready 10 Green chilli chopped
  14. Take 2 tsp Salt
  15. Make ready 2-3 tsp Makai aata
  16. Prepare Tarka:
  17. Prepare 2-3 tsp Butter
  18. Take 2-3 tsp Ginger
  19. Prepare 1 tsp Garlic slices
  20. Make ready 1 tsp Sabut lal mirch
Instructions to make Sarson ka saag with Makai ki roti:
  1. Here's, all ingredients.
  2. Wash sarso ka saag, methi, palak properly and cut it.Add water in pot.
  3. Add sarso ka saag, palak, methi.
  4. Add garlic cloves, green chilli, salt.
  5. Mix well and bring it to boil.
  6. In bowl add Maize flour, salt and luke water and knead it.
  7. Knead until it becomes a ball.
  8. Take plastic bags, place one ball dough and press it between plastic sheets.
  9. Make a round shape and fry makai ki roti with ghee. Makai roti is ready.
  10. Cook on low flame for 1-2 hours. After cooking blend whole mixture add maize flour until saag thickens.
  11. For tarka add in fry pan butter, garlic, ginger, red chilli and saute until brown. Now add this tarka in to saag.
  12. Sarso da saag is ready with makai de roti.
