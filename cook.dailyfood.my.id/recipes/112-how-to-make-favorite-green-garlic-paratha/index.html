How to Make Favorite Green Garlic Paratha

by Bryan Jennings

The ingredients needed to make Green Garlic Paratha:
  1. Get 2 cup wheat flour
  2. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  3. Get 2 tsp oil
  4. Make ready as needed Water
  5. Get 1 cup Clarified butter to shallow fry
  6. Prepare For garlic spread
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup garlic semi crushed (green garlic & garlic cloves, mix)
  8. Get 2 tbsp clarified butter
  9. Prepare 2 hot spicy green chillies
  10. Take Handful coriander leaves
  11. Get 1/4 tsp salt
Instructions to make Green Garlic Paratha:
  1. 100gm of green garlic chopped into small pieces. Mix with green chili, coriander leaves, and crushed into hand chopper. Then mix with clarified butter or homemade white butter, and salt. Mix well and keep aside.
  2. Bind smooth dugh, semi soft, with wheat flour, salt and water. Give rest for 15min or more then add oil and knead it for 2min. Make slightly bigger balls then usual paratha.
  3. Role it half and spread garlic spread into half. Turn half side and seal it. Again spread garlic spread in that half, turn it into half.
  4. Now you have triangular garlic flavored mixture stuffed paratha. Keep into fridge for 10-15min minutes. Then roll it with light pressure.
  5. Heat tawa and add into it. Wait for 15-20sec and flip it. Add some butter to shallow fry, and flip it. Again add some clarified butter and flip again. Roast on both side till light brown. Keep flame medium to low.
  6. Transfer into plate and serve with any of sabji, or pickle or snacks with curd or lassi or buttermilk or tea or coffee. - (Even you can make it like Lachchha paratha but it would be little tough. But taste very nice even you can eat it without anything.)
