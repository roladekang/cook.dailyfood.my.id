The ingredients needed to make Kaddu Chana dal:
  1. Get Half cup chanay ki dal(soaked for 4 to 5 hrs before)
  2. Prepare Aik Kaddu medium sized (cut in small cubes)
  3. Make ready 1 large pyaz thinly sliced
  4. Make ready Aik large sized tomato thinly sliced
  5. Get 1 tblspn ginger garlic paste
  6. Prepare 2-3 tspn dahi
  7. Get 1/2 tspn pisi lal mirch
  8. Make ready 1/2 tspn nmk
  9. Prepare 1/4 rth tspn kutti lal mirch
  10. Make ready 2 pinch haldi
  11. Take 1 tspn kutta dhania
  12. Get 1/2 tspn kutta zeera
  13. Take Half tspn mix sabat grm masala
  14. Prepare 1/4 rth tspn Pisa grm masala
Instructions to make Kaddu Chana dal:
  1. In a pan add oil and pyaz and fry them when translucent add mix grm masala and fry untill lite goldn now add ginger garlic paste add 3 tblspn water and cvr the pan
  2. After a minute add nmk mirch kuti and pisi both haldi zeera dhania and mix and fry the masala Pani ke cheetay dy ke masala fry krty rhain then add dahi and tomato and cvr it so that tomatoes soft ho Jain after two min mix and fry achay se masala bhoon lain
