The ingredients needed to make One Pot Chicken Arrabbiata::
  1. Make ready 1 Tbsp Olive Oil
  2. Get 1 Onion peeled and chopped
  3. Make ready 3 Garlic Cloves peeled and chopped
  4. Take 3 Chicken Breasts cut into bitesize pieces
  5. Get 1/2 tsp Red Pepper Flakes
  6. Get 1/4 tsp Chilli Flakes
  7. Make ready 1 tsp Oregano
  8. Get 1/2 tsp Worcestershire Sauce
  9. Make ready 600 g Passata / canned tomato / tomato purée (choice)
  10. Prepare 600 ml Chicken Stock
  11. Take 300 g Penne Pasta
  12. Take 125 g Mozzarella
  13. Get 100 g Parmesan grated
  14. Get Small handful of Basil leaves
Steps to make One Pot Chicken Arrabbiata::
  1. Heat the oil in the shallow casserole and fry the onion and garlic for a few minutes until soft.  - Add the chicken breast pieces and cook for another couple of minutes until they lose their pinkness (they don;t need to be completely cooked through)
  2. Add in all the other ingredients except for the basil, mozzarella and Parmesan and simmer on a low heat for 25 minutes. Stir occasionally.  - Serve with the fresh basil leaves, mozzarella and grated Parmesan. Season with salt and pepper.
