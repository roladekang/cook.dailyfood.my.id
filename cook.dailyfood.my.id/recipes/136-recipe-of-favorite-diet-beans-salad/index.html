The ingredients needed to make Diet beans salad 🥗:
  1. Take 2 1/2 cups boiled chickpea mix with 1/4 cup of black white kidney beans
  2. Take 1 tsp salt to taste
  3. Get 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  4. Get 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp lemon juice
  6. Get 3,4 tbsp imli ki chutney
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp chaat masala
  8. Get 1 tbsp olive oil
  9. Make ready 2 cucumber 🥒 chopped
  10. Get 1 medium onion chopped
  11. Prepare 1 large tomato 🍅 chopped
  12. Take 1,2 large Apple
  13. Make ready 2 medium 🍑 peach chopped
  14. Take 2 tbsp chopped almonds
  15. Get 1 tsp soda
  16. Get 1/2 cabbage/ lettuce 🥬
Steps to make Diet beans salad 🥗:
  1. Wash and soak the white chickpeas and kidney beans overnight with 1 tsp of soda.
  2. Next day wash the chickpea and kidney beans and boiled it till it becomes soft and tender than strain it.
  3. Now cool the chickpea and kidney beans.
  4. Cut the vegetables according to yr choice you can add capsicum/ lettuce 🥬 and broccoli 🥦.
  5. Now mix the spices and olive oil in the salad 🥗.
  6. Now chill the diet beans salad and eat it according to yr desires bowl/ plate.
