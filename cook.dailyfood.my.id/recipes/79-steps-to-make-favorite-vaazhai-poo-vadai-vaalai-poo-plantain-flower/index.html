The ingredients needed to make Vaazhai poo vadai (Vaalai poo/ Plantain flower):
  1. Get 100 grams Vaazhai poo stem removed and cut finely
  2. Prepare 100 grams Besan/ Kadalai maavu
  3. Get 2 teaspoons Chilli powder
  4. Take 1 teaspoon salt
  5. Make ready 2 medium sized onion grinded to rough paste
  6. Prepare 1 spring of curry leaves chopped
  7. Prepare 1 handful chopped coriander
  8. Make ready 2 green chillies ,chopped finely
Instructions to make Vaazhai poo vadai (Vaalai poo/ Plantain flower):
  1. Fine chop and get ingredients ready.
  2. Mix ingredients together without water. If it is too watery add some more besan to adjust consistency
  3. Take small gooseberry sized balls and flat in against inner palm and fry in medium heated oil
