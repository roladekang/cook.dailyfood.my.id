The ingredients needed to make Jain Bhel:
  1. Make ready 1 cup puffed rice
  2. Prepare 1/2 chopped cucumber
  3. Get 1/2 chopped tomato
  4. Make ready 1 green chilli
  5. Get 1 tsp coriander 🌿
  6. Take 1/4 tbsp tamarind chutney
  7. Make ready 1/2 tbsp coriander/green chutney
  8. Take 1/2 tsp chat masala
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp fried chana dal
  10. Take 1/2 tbsp roasted chana
  11. Get 1 tbsp nylon sev
Instructions to make Jain Bhel:
  1. First in a bowl put cucumber, tomato, chana dal, chana & sev.
  2. Then add chat masala & chutney… mix well.
  3. Add kurmura & mix.
  4. Serve with the garnishing of coriander leaves & nylon sev.
