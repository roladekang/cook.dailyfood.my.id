The ingredients needed to make Roti wrap:
  1. Make ready 4-5 leftover chapati
  2. Prepare 1 cup leftover vegetable (of your choice)
  3. Make ready 2 boiled and mashed potatoes
  4. Prepare 1 cup grated capsicum
  5. Make ready 1 cup grated cabbage
  6. Take 1 cup peas
  7. Take 1 cup grated carrot
  8. Take 1 tsp oregano salt
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  10. Get as per taste Salt
  11. Get 1 green chilli
  12. Take 4-5 pods garlic
  13. Prepare 1 tbsp oil
Steps to make Roti wrap:
  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Heat oil in kadhai. Add vegetables and salt. Mix them nicely.
  3. Take a roti, spread mayonnaise, then vegetables and then warp roti and roast for 2 minutes on tawa.
  4. Roast from both sides. Ready serve with ketchup.
