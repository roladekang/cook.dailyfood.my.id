Steps to Make Tasty Chocolate Dahi Lassi

The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Dahi Lassi:
  1. Make ready 1 cup curd
  2. Prepare 1-2 piece grated chocolate
  3. Take 1/2 tbsp sugar
  4. Take 4-5 ice cubes
  5. Prepare 1 tsp chocolate syrup
  6. Prepare As needed chunks of chocolate for garnishing
Steps to make Chocolate Dahi Lassi:
  1. In a blender put curd,sugar,ice cubes & grated chocolate.churn them.
  2. Decorate the sides of the serving glass with chocolate syrup & pour the drink.
  3. Put some chunks & enjoy.
