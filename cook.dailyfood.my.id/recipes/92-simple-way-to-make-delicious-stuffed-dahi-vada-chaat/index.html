Simple Way to Make Delicious Stuffed Dahi Vada Chaat

The ingredients needed to make Stuffed Dahi Vada Chaat:
  1. Get 3/4 cup split black lentils (urad dal)
  2. Get 1 tsp ginger- garlic- green chilli paste
  3. Prepare Pinch asafoetida (hing)
  4. Make ready (Stuffing):-
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp chopped cashew nuts
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp almond chopped
  7. Take 1 tbsp raisins
  8. Prepare Handful pomegranate (anaar)
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp red apple chopped
  10. Take (For curd mixture):-
  11. Make ready 1 cup chilled curd (dahi)
  12. Make ready 1 tsp sugar
  13. Take Pinch black salt
  14. Get 1 tsp Cumin powder (sahi jeera)
  15. Prepare 1 tsp Red chilli powder
  16. Make ready to taste Salt
  17. Take (For garnishing):-
  18. Prepare 1 tbsp coriander leaves (daniya patti)
  19. Make ready 1 tbsp Aloo bhujia
  20. Prepare 1 tsp chaat masala
  21. Take Oil for deep frying vadas
Instructions to make Stuffed Dahi Vada Chaat:
  1. First wash and soak urad dal in a bowl for 3 hours. After drain well and blend with greenchilles add very little water in a mixer.
  2. Transfer the dal mixture into a bowl add ginger-garlic paste, hing, salt mix well.
  3. Wet a deep ladle with water pour 1 tbsp batter in it add stuffing in the centre and pour 1 more tbsp batter over it, drop it in hot oil for deep fry,till golden brown in colour.
  4. Note:-(Or u can make your hand wet with water and take 1 tbsp batter and make small pit and our stuffing in between close it with a tbsp batter and deep fry them)
  5. Drain off in a kitchen paper after this soak the vadas in a water for 15 minutes. Squeeze out and drain all the excess water. Keep aside.
  6. Now Take a serving dish or bowl arrange stuffed vadas. Spread beaten curd. Sprinkel a little red chilli Powder, chaat masala, Cumin powder, fresh Coriander leaves, Aloo bhujia.
  7. Serve immediately.
