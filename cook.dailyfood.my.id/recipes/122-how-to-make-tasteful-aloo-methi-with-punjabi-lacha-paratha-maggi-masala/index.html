The ingredients needed to make Aloo methi with punjabi lacha paratha maggi masala:
  1. Prepare 2 bunch fresh methi( fenugreek) leaves
  2. Prepare 4 potatoes
  3. Make ready 2 tsp Maggi masala
  4. Get 1 tsp cumin seeds
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  6. Prepare 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  7. Take 2 teaspoon Mustard oil
  8. Get 100 gram wheat flour
  9. Take 10 gram oats powder
  10. Make ready 2 tsp besan/Gram flour
  11. Make ready 4 tbsp Pure ghee or fresh butter
  12. Get as needed Hung curd
  13. Make ready as needed Warm water
  14. Prepare to taste Salt
