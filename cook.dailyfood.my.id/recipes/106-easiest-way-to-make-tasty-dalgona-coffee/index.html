The ingredients needed to make Dalgona Coffee !!:
  1. Make ready 4 tsp Instant Coffee Powder
  2. Prepare 4 tsp Sugar
  3. Get 5 tsp Hot Water
  4. Get 1 cup Milk
  5. Make ready 3-4 Ice Cubes
  6. Take as required Cinnamon Powder to sprinkle on top
Steps to make Dalgona Coffee !!:
  1. Take instant coffee powder and sugar in big mug. Mix well and add hot water. Mix well.
  2. Whisk together well till the mixture becomes creamy and frothy.
  3. Add ice cubes to the glass. Then add milk.
  4. Scoop the prepared creamy frothy coffee on top of the milk. Sprinkle some cinnamon powder over it.
  5. Chilled creamy frothy Dalgona Coffee is ready to serve. Enjoy.
