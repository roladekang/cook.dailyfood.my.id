The ingredients needed to make Oreo ice cream cake:
  1. Make ready Amul royal ice cream
  2. Prepare Chocolate spring roll cake
  3. Get Gems
  4. Make ready Waffers
  5. Make ready Oreo biscuits
  6. Take Cocao powder
  7. Get Milk
  8. Make ready Condensed milk
  9. Get Butter
  10. Take Dark fantasy biscuits
Steps to make Oreo ice cream cake:
  1. Chocolate sauce.. - Mix cocao powder, milk, condensed milk, butter and little corn starch well in a bowl.boil it to a sauce like consistency. Let cool in fridge
  2. Add ice cream in a jar along with oreo biscuits and dark fantasy and whisk well untill its mixed. Put this in freezer.
  3. Take a bowl spread oreo ice cream even in it. Then place spring rolls slices on ice cream. Then add ice cream then some gems then ice cream. Set in Freezer.
  4. Till its set then unmould ice cream cake and pour chocolate sauce on it.again freeze till it set.
  5. Take it out and decorate with gems white chocolate and waffer as you like.
  6. Serve chill..
