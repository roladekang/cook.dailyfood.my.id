Step-by-Step Guide to Prepare Homemade Biscuit pops

The ingredients needed to make Biscuit pops:
  1. Get 10 nos marie biscuits
  2. Get 10 nos parle g biscuits
  3. Make ready 1 cup fruity muesli
  4. Prepare as needed sugar
  5. Get 80 ml fresh thick cream
  6. Get as needed white melted chocolate
  7. Get as needed dark chocolate melted
  8. Take 2 tsp coconut oil
  9. Take sprinklers,sweet fennel, dessicated coconut
Instructions to make Biscuit pops:
  1. Take biscuits and muesli and grind with sugar if needed..as mine was sweet muesli…
  2. Take out this powder in a bowl and add the thick cream till it binds well.knead it like a dough and make small balls out of it.
  3. Take white and dark chocolate melted in different bowls…add a tsp oil to both and mix well.
  4. Take sticks and dip in the chocolate and stick in the centre of pops… refrigerate to set..
  5. Hold the stick amd dip in chocolate and sprinkle the sprinklers or sweet fennel or dessicated coconut…tadaa biscuit pops are ready…
