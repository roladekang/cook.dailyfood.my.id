The ingredients needed to make Peaches and cream overnight oats:
  1. Take 1 cup peaches chopped
  2. Prepare 3/4 cup vanilla yoghurt light and fit
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup quick oats
  4. Prepare 1 tbs honey
Instructions to make Peaches and cream overnight oats:
  1. Mix peaches, yoghurt and oats together
  2. Drizzle honey overtop and put in fridge overnight
  3. Get out of fridge in morning and enjoy.
