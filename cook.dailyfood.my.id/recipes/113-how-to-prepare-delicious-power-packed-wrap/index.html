How to Prepare Delicious Power packed wrap

The ingredients needed to make Power packed wrap:
  1. Prepare 2 big size whole wheat roti
  2. Take 200 gms fine chicken mince
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp finely chopped onion
  4. Get 1 tsp finely chopped garlic
  5. Prepare 1 tsp finely chopped ginger
  6. Prepare 1 green chilli finely chopped
  7. Take to taste Salt and pepper
  8. Take 1 large carrot cut in long strip
  9. Get 1 large potato cut in long strip
  10. Prepare 1/2 red capsicum cut in long strip
  11. Prepare 1 onion thinly sliced
  12. Make ready 2 tbsp oil
  13. Get to taste Salt and pepper
  14. Prepare leaves Few lettuce
  15. Prepare For dressing
  16. Make ready 1 tbsp lemon juice
  17. Get 1 tbsp olive oil
  18. Prepare 1 tbsp honey
  19. Take 1 tsp mustard sauce
  20. Make ready to taste Salt and pepper
  21. Prepare 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  22. Take Few spring onion green for tying rolls
Instructions to make Power packed wrap:
  1. First in a.bowl.mix chicken mince,onion,ginger,garlic,green chilli,salt and pepper
  2. Mix well and keep aside.
  3. Cut carrot and potato in strips.
  4. Cut onion and red capsicum.
  5. Boil carrot and potatoes in salted water.
  6. Now saute veggies with salt and pepper.
  7. Spread chicken mince on foil.
  8. Place lettuce leaves over it.
  9. For dressing first mix all.ingredients except mayonnaise. Then add mayonnaise and.mix well
  10. Put sauted veggies on chicken mince and lettuce bed.Drizzle some prepared dressing.
  11. Now fold it and chill.in fridge for 30 mins.
  12. Meanwhile prepare Roti for roll.
  13. Now heat a pan. Add some oil and cook chicken mince roll.
  14. Cook from all sides on low flame.
  15. Now spread some dressing on Roti.
  16. Also add some lettuce leaves and onion rings.
  17. Now place cooked chicken mince roll.
  18. Fold it and cut in halves. wrap in butter paper and secure tightly with spring onion strip.
