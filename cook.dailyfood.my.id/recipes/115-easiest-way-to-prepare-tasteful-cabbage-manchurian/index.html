The ingredients needed to make Cabbage manchurian:
  1. Take Ching's secret veg manchurian masala
  2. Get chopped ginger
  3. Prepare Oil for deep frying
  4. Make ready Chopped spring onion for garnishing
  5. Take grated cabbage
  6. Prepare Salt
  7. Prepare diced vegetables (onion. Coloured capsicum)
  8. Make ready finely chopped green chilli
  9. Make ready besan
  10. Prepare water
  11. Make ready chopped baby corn
  12. Take chopped green garlic
Instructions to make Cabbage manchurian:
  1. In cabbage add salt and keep aside for 5-10 mins. Then squeezed the water out of it. Add ginger. Chilli. Besan. Mix well and make a Dough
  2. Make small sized ball. Deep frying and drain access oil and keep a side
  3. Heat the oil in pan. Add chopped veggies. Saute for 1-2 min. Dont over cook
  4. In bowl add manchurian masala and 3-4 spoon water and mix well
  5. Add manchurian ball. Masala mix and water. Bring it boil for 5-10 min. Turn off the gas once the gravy thickens
  6. Garnish with chopped green onion. Green garlic and serve hot
