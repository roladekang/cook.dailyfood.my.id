Recipe of Delicious Veg Manchurian

by Cole Manning

The ingredients needed to make Veg Manchurian:
  1. Prepare grated or grinded Cabbage
  2. Get chopped capsicum
  3. Prepare chopped onion
  4. Take chopped garlic
  5. Take soya sauce
  6. Take tomato sauce
  7. Take Schezwan chutney
  8. Take red chilli sauce
  9. Take Cornflour
  10. Make ready green chilli
  11. Make ready Black pepper powder
  12. Get Maida
  13. Get Oil
  14. Make ready Spring onion
Steps to make Veg Manchurian:
  1. Take 1 cup grinded Cabbage add salt and some pepper mix it then add 2 tbsp cornflour and 2 tbsp Maida sprinkle some water and make a dough.
  2. Make balls of the cabbage dough and fry till golden and crispy.
  3. Take a pan heat some oil and put chopped green chilli and garlic then add onion capsicum and pepper saute till the veggies cooked.
  4. Then add some salt all the sauces and mix it and saute for 2 mins.
  5. Then add 1 cup water and bring to a boil.
  6. Take 1 tbsp cornflour and add water make a liquid batter.
  7. Then pour in a gravy for thickness.add little as per desired consistency.donot add too much.
  8. Add Manchurian balls mix it add some spring onion.
  9. Serve the delicious Chinese Manchurian
