Instructions to make Whole Wheat banana & choco chip cupcakes:
  1. First of all measure all the ingredients.
  2. Sift the Maida and aata together.
  3. Grind sugar.
  4. Prepare buttermilk by adding 1tbsp vinegar in 1cup milk.
  5. Mix all the dry ingredients together in a bowl.
  6. Mash bananas in a separate bowl.and keep aside.
  7. Take eggs in another bowl..beat…then add oil.
  8. Add vanilla essence in the eggs mixture.
  9. Then add mashed bananas in this batter and mix.
  10. Then fold the dry ingredients in this dry batter.
  11. Also add buttermilk side by side…mix well until homogenized mixture is formed.
  12. Cupcake mixture is ready.
  13. Take the cupcake baking tray and set the cupcake liners.
  14. Pour the cupcake mixture in liners. Add chocolate chip and almonds according to choice.
  15. Put in oven and bake at 170 c for 25 to 30 mins until they e cooked.
  16. Take out of the oven…yummilicious whole wheat banana cupcakes are ready..
