The ingredients needed to make Fried Green Tomato:
  1. Take 1 cup flour
  2. Take 1 cup seasoned corn meal
  3. Take 1 egg whipped
  4. Get 1 tbsp salt
  5. Take 1 large Green Tomato
  6. Prepare 1 cup canola oil
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp butter
Instructions to make Fried Green Tomato:
  1. Cut tomato in slices.
  2. Lay slices flat and sprinkle with salt.
  3. Coat with flour.
  4. Dip in beaten egg.
  5. Then coat in seasoned cornmeal.
  6. Heat oil over meadium heat in frying pan. Place butter in oil.
  7. When the butter has melted and begins to sizzle the oil is hot enough. Brown each side til golden brown.
