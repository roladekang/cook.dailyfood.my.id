The ingredients needed to make Chana dal chutney (Koul chutney):
  1. Take 3 spoons Chana dhal
  2. Make ready 4 red chillies
  3. Prepare 1 Tomato
  4. Take 5 spoons Coconut grated
  5. Take 1 spoon mustard seeds
Steps to make Chana dal chutney (Koul chutney):
  1. Fry 3 red chilli and Chana dhal in a kadhai.keep it aside.
  2. Fry tomato in the same Kadai. Let it cool down.
  3. Grind coconut, Chana dhal, red chilli, tomato into a smooth paste.
  4. Fry in oil mustard, red chilli. Serve it hot with idli and dosa
