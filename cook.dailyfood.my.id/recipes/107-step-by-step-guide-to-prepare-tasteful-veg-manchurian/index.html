Step-by-Step Guide to Prepare Tasteful Veg Manchurian

by Sally Phelps

To begin with this recipe, we have to first prepare a few ingredients. You can cook veg manchurian using 21 ingredients and 14 steps. Here is how you can achieve that.

The ingredients needed to make Veg Manchurian:
  1. Get For Veg Manchurian Balls:
  2. Make ready maida/ all purpose flour
  3. Make ready Corn flour
  4. Get shredded Carrot
  5. Make ready shredded Cabbage
  6. Make ready finely chopped Capsicum
  7. Take finely chopped Green Chilli
  8. Take Pepper Powder
  9. Get salt
  10. Take For Gravy:
  11. Prepare Ginger
  12. Prepare Green Chillies
  13. Get Garlic
  14. Prepare finely chopped Spring Onion
  15. Take Oil
  16. Prepare Soy Sauce
  17. Get Chilli Sauce
  18. Prepare Tomato Ketchup
  19. Take Pepper Powder
  20. Take Cornflour
  21. Make ready Salt
Instructions to make Veg Manchurian:
  1. Preparing Manchurian: Take a bowl, add shredded carrot, shredded cabbage, chopped capsicum and chopped green chilli, oil, pepper powder, maida, cornflour and salt.
  2. Mix  all  ingredients  properly.  Prepare  small  round  shaped  balls  from  the  mix.  Do  not  add water.
  3. Heat oil in a kadhai (pan). Deep fry prepared balls on medium flame until they turn golden brown.
  4. Drain excess oil and transfer them to plate.
  5. Preparing gravy: Dissolve cornflour in 1/2 cup of water.
  6. Heat oil in a pan. Add minced ginger, minced garlic, chopped green chilli and chopped spring onion.
  7. Sauté for a minute.
  8. Add soy sauce, chilli sauce and tomato ketchup; sauté for a minute.
  9. Pour 1 cup water, pepper powder and salt. Bring mixture to boil over medium flame.
  10. Add dissolved cornflour to the gravy.
  11. Mix well and cook it on low flame for 2 minutes.
  12. Add deep fried balls and cook for few mins over medium flame.
  13. Turn off flame and garnish it with chopped spring onion.
  14. Enjoy hot and spicy veg manchurian with fried rice or noodles or simply as appetizer.
