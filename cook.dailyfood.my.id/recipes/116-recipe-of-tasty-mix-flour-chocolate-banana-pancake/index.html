The ingredients needed to make Mix Flour Chocolate Banana Pancake:
  1. Make ready 1 cup wheat flour
  2. Get 2 tsp semolina
  3. Make ready 2 tsp rice flour
  4. Make ready 4 tsp sugar
  5. Get 2 tsp custard powder
  6. Take 2 tsp cocoa powder
  7. Take As needed milk with fresh cream for batter
  8. Get 4-5 drops vanilla essence
  9. Prepare 1 ripe mashed banana
  10. Take as required Oil for frying
  11. Take as required Grated coconut for garnishing

Steps to make Mix Flour Chocolate Banana Pancake:
  1. In a bowl take wheat flour,semolina and rice flour.
  2. Now add sugar,custard powder and mashed banana.
  3. Add vanilla essence,milk and mix all the ingredients nicely and prepare a smooth batter of pouring consistency.
  4. Finally add cocoa powder and mix batter well.
  5. Heat oil in a pan,pour laddle full of batter and spread it by moving the pan in circular motion and cook it on low flame for 2 minutes.
  6. Flip the pancake,pour 1 tsp more oil on edges and coot it for more 2 minutes on low flame.
  7. Cut into pieces,garnish with grated coconut,Pancake is ready to serve.
