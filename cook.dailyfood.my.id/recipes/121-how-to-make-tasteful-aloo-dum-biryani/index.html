The ingredients needed to make Aloo Dum Biryani:
  1. Get 200 gm Basmati Rice (washed and soaked for 20 mins)
  2. Get 2 medium size Potatoes (peeled Washed and cutted into half)
  3. Make ready as needed Oil For frying Potatoes
  4. Get as needed water For cooking Rice
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp Oil / Ghee
  6. Prepare to taste Salt
  7. Take 3 Green Cardamom
  8. Take 1 Black Cardamom
  9. Prepare 1 Bay leaf
  10. Get 1 inch Cinnamon Stick
  11. Make ready 2 tsp Mint leaves
  12. Take 2 tsp lemon juice
  13. Make ready For Biryani Gravy
  14. Get 3 tbsp ghee
  15. Get 1 tsp cumin seeds
  16. Prepare 1 Bay leaf
  17. Prepare 1 inch Cinnamon stick
  18. Make ready 1 tbsp Ginger Garlic paste
  19. Get 2 medium size Chopped tomato
  20. Get handful Salt
  21. Make ready 1 tsp Red chilli Powder
  22. Prepare 1/2 tsp kashmiri Red chilli powder
  23. Prepare 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
  24. Prepare 2 tsp Coriander powder
  25. Get 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  26. Get 1/2 cup Barista(fried onion)
  27. Prepare 1/2 cup Yoghurt
  28. Take 4 gm Mix powder of Mace, Nutmeg, Black pepper, Green Cardamom
  29. Prepare 3 tsp Chopped Coriander leaves for Garnish
  30. Prepare 2 tsp mint leaves for Garnish
  31. Get 1 1/2 pinch saffron in 2 1/2 luckwarm milk
Steps to make Aloo Dum Biryani:
  1. Wash and soak Basmati Rice for 20 mins.
  2. Wash, peeled & cut the potatoes into half and fry in ghee (Not boil the potatoes because it will cook 15% while frying, 60 % in gravy & 15% in dum). When potatoes are done with frying then put it side.
  3. In 1 ltr. Water add bay leaf, mint leaves, Green Cardamom, Black Cardamom, Cinnamon Stick, Ghee & oil, Lemon Juice, and salt. Then boil the water and put soaked rice and cook it on slow flame. Cook it 80 to 85%.
  4. For Gravy:-
  5. In Hot ghee temper Cumin seeds, Cinnamon stick, Bay leaf then add Ginger&Garlic paste cook it, after that add chopped tomatoes and salt, red chilli powder, kashmiri red chilli powder, Turmeric powder, Coriander powder, Gharam Masala. When the tomatoes melt add Barista and yoghurt and then add fried potatoes and cook until potatoes cooked until 85% and oil comes on top.
  6. Once gravy become thick and cooked now start layer your biriyani.
  7. For layering:-Take out your half gravy and potatoes.
  8. Now put first 50% of your gravy & 50% of Rice and coriander leaves, mint leaves, Mix powder, Barista, saffron..
  9. Again repeat 2nd layer of it then cover it and seal with dough and put on slow flame for 15 - 20 mins.
  10. Once it done serve it hot.
  11. And become cool
