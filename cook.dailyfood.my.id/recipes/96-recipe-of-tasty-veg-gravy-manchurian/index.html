Recipe of Tasty Veg gravy Manchurian

The ingredients needed to make Veg gravy Manchurian:
  1. Get For Manchurian ball-
  2. Prepare 4 tbsp Carrot grated
  3. Make ready 4 tbsp Cabbage finely chopped
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp Capsicum finely chopped
  5. Take 1 Green chilli finely chopped
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp Maida
  7. Take 2 tbsp Cornflour
  8. Get 1/2 tsp Salt
  9. Take 1/4 tsp Black pepper
  10. Prepare 1 tsp Ginger garlic paste
  11. Take 1 tsp Soya sauce
  12. Get For gravy-
  13. Prepare 1 tsp Crushed ginger
  14. Take 1 tsp Crushed garlic
  15. Make ready 1 Chopped green chilli
  16. Prepare 1 Chopped Onion medium
  17. Take 2 tbsp Chopped spring onion
  18. Get 1 tbsp Oil
  19. Get 2 tsp Soya sauce
  20. Make ready 1 tsp Chilli sauce
  21. Make ready 1 tsp Tomato sauce
  22. Take 1/2 tbsp Vinegar
  23. Get 1/2 tsp Black pepper
  24. Prepare 1/4 tsp Salt
  25. Make ready 2 cups Water
Steps to make Veg gravy Manchurian:
  1. Take a bowl, add chopped capsicum, cabbage, carrots, green chilli, ginger-garlic paste, salt, black pepper, maida, cornflour and soya sauce. Mix properly. Prepare small round shaped balls from the mixture. Heat oil in a wok, deep fry balls on medium flame till golden brown.Transfer fried balls on kitchen paper. Keep aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add crushed ginger, garlic, chopped onion, green chilli and spring onion saute for a minute, add soya sauce, chilli sauce, tomato sauce and vinegar, saute these for a minute. Now add 2 cups of water, salt, and black pepper. Bring mixture to boil, boil it for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Dissolve cornflour in some water and mixed to the gravy, cook it for 3-4 minutes on low flame. Add deep-fried Manchurian balls and cook for 5 minutes on slow flame. Off the flame, and transfer the dish to a serving platter, and garnish with spring onions.
  4. Ready to serve veg gravy Manchurian with nan or fried rice.
