The ingredients needed to make Medhu Vadai:
  1. Make ready 1 cup urad dhal
  2. Take 2 green chillies ,chopped
  3. Prepare 1 tsp pepper
  4. Make ready Few curry leaves
  5. Get 1 onion (optional)
  6. Take Pinch asafoetida
  7. Make ready as required Oil to fry
  8. Get to taste Salt
Steps to make Medhu Vadai:
  1. Soak urad dhal in water for 2 hours. Add little water and grind it as a thick batter.
  2. To check the correct consistency take a small portion of batter and drop it in water, it should float.
  3. To the batter add all the rest of the ingredients.
  4. Scoop the batter and make a hole in the centre and drop it in the pan.
  5. Tip - If the batter is watery add rice flour to adjust.
