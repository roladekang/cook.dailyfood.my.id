The ingredients needed to make Paneer wrap:
  1. Get 100 gms paneer
  2. Prepare 1 chopped capsicum
  3. Get 1 cup boiled corn
  4. Prepare 1/2 chopped cabbage
  5. Make ready 3 green chillies
  6. Take 1 tbsp butter
  7. Take 8-10 Chapati
  8. Make ready For puree:
  9. Take 3 tomatoes
  10. Prepare 1 pc ginger
  11. Take For Gravy:
  12. Take 1/4 tbsp pizza pasta sauce
  13. Make ready 1 tsp red chilli powder
  14. Get as per taste salt
  15. Make ready 1/2 tsp garam massla
  16. Take as required butter for cooking

Steps to make Paneer wrap:
  1. Heat butter in a pan, add chillies and all the veggies. Saute it for 2 mins.
  2. Add puree and all the spices and sauce to it.
  3. Add paneer and cook well.
  4. Let it cool.
  5. Take one chapati. Spread paneer gravy. Roll and toast it on tawa with butter.
