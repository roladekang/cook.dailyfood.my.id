The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Veggie Egg Wrap:
  1. Prepare 5 rotis normal
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 chopped capsicum
  3. Make ready 1 chopped carrot
  4. Get 1 chopped onion
  5. Take 2 tsp Chopped green chillies
  6. Get 3 tsp fresh coriander
  7. Prepare 5 tsp mint coriander chutney
  8. Get 5 tsp Veeba spread
  9. Get 5 tsp grated cheese
  10. Get 5 eggs
  11. Get to taste Salt
  12. Take to taste Black pepper
  13. Make ready 1 tsp chaat masala
Instructions to make Cheesy Veggie Egg Wrap:
  1. Beat veggies, egg, salt and black pepper
  2. Make normal rotiis.
  3. Take out egg mixture for 1 serving and cook from both sides
  4. Spread green chutney and veeba spread on roti
  5. Keep a cooked veggie egg on top. Sprinkle some cheese and chaat masala. Seal the edges and roast it with butter on all sides.
  6. Serve hot!
