Instructions to make Biscuit Cups With Yoghurt #kids #Post1:
  1. Crush the biscuits in blender. - Add melted butter & mix. - I divided the crushed biscuits into 4 portions & mixed the colour with 1 tbsp melted butter & added.
  2. Press the mixture evenly to bottom & sides of your mould. - Freeze them for 30- 45 minutes.
  3. Remove them & gently remove from the mould. - Fill them with yoghurt of your choice, garnish & let them rest in freezer for sometime & serve chilled.
