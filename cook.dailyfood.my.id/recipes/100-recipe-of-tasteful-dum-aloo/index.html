The ingredients needed to make Dum Aloo-:
  1. Get 8-10 boiled small potatoes
  2. Get 2-3 tomato
  3. Take 2 green chilli
  4. Get as needed oil for frying
  5. Take as per taste salt
  6. Get 1 tsp dhania powder
  7. Prepare 2 tsp red chilli powder
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp black salt
  9. Prepare 1 tsp raw mango powder
  10. Take 1 tsp kasoori methi
  11. Get 1/4 tsp haldi
  12. Prepare 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  13. Take 1/2 tsp hing
  14. Get 1/2 cup curd
  15. Take 1 tsp sugar
  16. Take white paste
  17. Take 7-8 cashew nuts
  18. Take 4-5 cloves garlic
  19. Take 1 small pc ginger
  20. Get 1 small stick cinnamon
  21. Take 4-5 black pepper
Steps to make Dum Aloo-:
  1. Peel the boiled potatoes
  2. Pork with Fork & deep fry them
  3. Drain out the extra oil…
  4. Make white paste
  5. Make tomato puree
  6. Mix red chilli powder with 1 tbsp of water
  7. Whisk curd and mix little water.
  8. Heat 2tbsp oil in a kadhai..add cumin seeds hing & red chilli powder paste….cook well on low flame.. immediately add white paste and cook
  9. Then add tomato purée and all the spices…. mix well then add curd and sugar and stir continuously
  10. Then add potatoes and cook
  11. At last add kasoori methi and cook for a minute.. garnish with coriander leaves… enjoy with chapati naan kulcha or rice
