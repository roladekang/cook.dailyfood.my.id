The ingredients needed to make Chocotruffle Biscuit cake:

Steps to make Chocotruffle Biscuit cake:

  2. Now pour dark chocolate over it.
  3. Swtich off gas, add butter.
  4. Let it sit till chocolate melts.
  5. Give it a good stir and your ganache is ready
  6. Let it cool a little and assemble.
  7. Line a cake tin with parchment.
  8. Spread late today biscuits and pour ganache over it.
  9. Spread walnuts and biscuits and again a top layer of ganache.
  10. Decorate with walnuts.
