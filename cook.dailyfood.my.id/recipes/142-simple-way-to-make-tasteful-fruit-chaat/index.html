The ingredients needed to make Fruit chaat:
  1. Get 2 apples
  2. Make ready 1 cup grapes
  3. Take 2 bananas
  4. Take 2 peaches
  5. Get 1/2 cup strawberries
  6. Take 1 cup melon cubed
  7. Prepare Use any fruits of choice
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp sugar
  9. Get 2 tbsp or more tang powder
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup water
  11. Get 1/4 tsp Black salt
  12. Get 1 tsp fruit chat masala
  13. Prepare 1 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions to make Fruit chaat:
  1. Cut all fruits
  2. In a big bowl put water, sugar and lemon juice and mix
  3. Add fruits and mix
  4. Add tang, chat masala and black salt and toss every thing well.
  5. Keep covered for sometime to infuse flavour of each other and release their juices.
  6. Juicy, tasty chaat is ready
