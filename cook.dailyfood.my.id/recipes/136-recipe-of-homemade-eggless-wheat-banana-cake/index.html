The ingredients needed to make Eggless wheat banana cake:
  1. Make ready 2 cup Wheat flour
  2. Take 1 1/2 cup sugar
  3. Take 2 tsp Vanilla essence
  4. Get 2 tsp baking powder
  5. Take 1 tsp baking soda
  6. Take 1 1/2 tsp Milk
  7. Take 4 tbsp Butter
  8. Prepare 3-4 sweet Banana

Instructions to make Eggless wheat banana cake:
  1. To a cooker, add a layer of salt. Close the lid without casket. Preheat it for 10 to 15 min.
  2. In a mixing bowl. Add butter, vanilla essence and mix it well. Add powdered sugar.
  3. To a mixer, add chopped banana and grind it. Add that banana puree to the sugar butter mixture.
  4. Mix it well. Add baking soda and baking powder. Mix it well using cut & fold method.
  5. Add wheat flour. Mix it well. Then add milk.
  6. Grease butter in a vessel and dust wheat flour. Pour the cake batter and tap the vessel.
  7. Remove the cooker lid and place a stand or unused plate. Then place the cake batter vessel. Close the lid and cook for 10 min in high flame then 45 min in medium flame.
  8. After 45 min check using knife or tooth pick it should not stick to it. Then the egg less wheat banana cake is ready. Decorate as you wish.
