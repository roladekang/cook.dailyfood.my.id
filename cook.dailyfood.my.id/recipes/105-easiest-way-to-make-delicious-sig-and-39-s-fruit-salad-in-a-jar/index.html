The ingredients needed to make sig's Fruit Salad in a Jar:
  1. Get summer salad
  2. Make ready 400 grams fresh strawberries, hulled and rinsed
  3. Take 1/2 cucumber seeded and peeled
  4. Take 2 semi crisp red apples
  5. Get 2 peaches stoned and sliced
  6. Take 1/2 juice of a lemon
  7. Prepare 2 oranges
  8. Make ready 1 small bunch of fresh mint
  9. Make ready Pimm's syrup
  10. Make ready 70 grams sugar
  11. Prepare 75 ml each brandy and sherry or 200 ml Pimm's or Campari
  12. Make ready 50 ml fresh squeezed orange juice if using brandy and sherry.
Instructions to make sig's Fruit Salad in a Jar:
  1. to make syrup ,heat the sugar and Pimms or Campari,reduce to about half the amount .Set aside to chill.
  2. Quarter the strawberries,peel and decede cucumber ,cut into small chunks,core apples and remove stones from peaches ,cut into small chunks, peel orange and cut segments into bitesize pieces collect all the juices .add mint leaves.Drizzle the lemon juice over top ,mix all gently.Add the syrup.I serve this salad in small jars that I collected ,chill for a few hours
