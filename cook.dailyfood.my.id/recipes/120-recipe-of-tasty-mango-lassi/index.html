The ingredients needed to make Mango Lassi:
  1. Make ready Mango pulp (3 big mangoes)
  2. Prepare Yoghurt (2 cups)
  3. Take cubes Ice
  4. Take Sugar(optional)
  5. Get Chocolate balls for topping
Instructions to make Mango Lassi:
  1. Transfer the mango pulp into blender cup pour in yoghurt, ice cubes and sugar.
  2. Blend till smooth and pour in your cup/container.
  3. Top with chocolate balls or anything of choice and serve. - Hmmmmm am telling you even if you no like mango u go love this.
