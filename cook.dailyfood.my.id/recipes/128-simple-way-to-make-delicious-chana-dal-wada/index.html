The ingredients needed to make Chana dal wada:
  1. Make ready 250 gm chana dal
  2. Prepare 2 medium size onion
  3. Make ready 5-7 curry leaves
  4. Take To taste Salt
  5. Get 2-3 Green chilli
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  7. Make ready As needed Oil for fry
Instructions to make Chana dal wada:
  1. Take dal and soak for overnight..
  2. At morning rinse the dal and grind it in mixer..do not make smooth paste. Some of the dal is don't grind.
  3. Add Chopped Onion and all dry spices in the dal mixture.
  4. Take pan and heat the oil..take small portion of dal mixture at your palm and make a tikki style and deep fry it. Fry till then comes golden brown colour…
  5. Take out and serve hot with red tomato ketchup and green chutney as your wish..
