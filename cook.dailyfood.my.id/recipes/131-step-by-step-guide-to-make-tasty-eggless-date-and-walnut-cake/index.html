The ingredients needed to make Eggless Date and Walnut Cake:
  1. Make ready 1 cup whole wheat multigrain atta
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  3. Prepare 1/2 tbsp chocolate powder
  4. Make ready 1 tsp baking powder
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking soda
  6. Get 1/4 tsp vanilla essence
  7. Make ready 14 dates finely chopped
  8. Make ready 12 walnuts chopped
  9. Take 6 whole walnuts
  10. Make ready 1/4 cup olive oil
  11. Take 3/4 cup cow milk
  12. Make ready 1 banana
  13. Get 2 tbsp fresh curds

Instructions to make Eggless Date and Walnut Cake:
  1. Chop dates remove seeds,you can take seedless dates too.Heat milk,soak chopped dates for 30 min.
  2. After 30 min puree dates and milk mixture in mixie.Puree to a smooth paste.
  3. Add a banana and puree in mixie.Add oil in this and blend,add curds too.Chop walnuts and keep few chopped dates to add in cake.
  4. Pureed mixture.Now sieve dry ingredients such as whole wheat flour,baking powder, baking soda,cocoa powder, chocolate powder.Sieve all together.
  5. Add dry ingredients slowly in wet ingredients,blend slowly together till a smooth consistency.
  6. Add little more milk if required.
  8. Add vanilla essence and mix nicely.Grease a baking tin.Sprinkle maida.Remove excess maida.
  9. Add chopped dates and chopped walnuts in the batter.Add in cake batter.Preheat oven for 7 minutes.Decorate with chopped dates and whole walnuts.
  10. Bake in preheated oven at 180degrees.Keep cake in oven bake at 180degrees for 50 minutes.
  11. Serve hot.When warm cut into pieces.
