Simple Way to Prepare Homemade Tandoori chicken cheese wrap

The ingredients needed to make Tandoori chicken cheese wrap:
  1. Take 150 gram Chicken
  2. Prepare 2 spoon Cabbage grated
  3. Make ready 1 Onion medium size chopped lengthwise
  4. Take 1/2 spoon Chat masala
  5. Make ready 20 gram Cheese
  6. Get 1/4 spoon black pepper powder
  7. Make ready 1/2 Lime
  8. Get 1 Roti/Chapathi
  9. Take Marinade for chicken
  10. Make ready 1 spoon red Chilli powder
  11. Make ready 1 spoon Kashmiri chilli powder
  12. Prepare 1/2 spoon Ginger garlic paste
  13. Get 1/2 spoon Mint paste
  14. Get 3 spoon Curd
  15. Get 1/2 spoon Salt
  16. Take 1 spoon Dried methi(fenugreek) leaves
Instructions to make Tandoori chicken cheese wrap:
  1. Marinate the chicken with the ingredients mentioned under the head, marinade for atleast 1 hour. Grill them in oven or tandoor and separate the bones if any and make it into small pieces by hand.
  2. Take a roti or chapathi and spread the chicken pieces followed by onions and grated cabbage
  3. Add finely chopped coriander leaves, some chat masala and lime juice
  4. Grate your favourite cheese on top and add some pepper powder. You can also add mayonnise if you want to.
  5. Gently wrap and it put it on tawa for few minutes
  6. Serve hot with mint chutney or mayonnaise and onions. You can also alternatively add a gallop of mayonnaise instead of cheese.
