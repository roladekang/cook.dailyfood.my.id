The ingredients needed to make Leftover khaman/dhokla Upma:
  1. Get 3-4 pieces leftover dhokla
  2. Prepare 1 small onion (sliced)
  3. Get 1 small tomato finely chopped
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup green peas
  5. Take 1/4 cup carrot finely chopped
  6. Make ready 1 green chilli finely chopped
  7. Take Some fresh coriander leaves chopped
  8. Get 1-2 strings of curry leaves
  9. Get 1 tsp moong/urad dal
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp peanuts
  11. Get 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  12. Take 1-2 tbsp oil
  13. Get Salt and red chilli powder as per taste

Steps to make Leftover khaman/dhokla Upma:
  1. Crumble the leftover dhokla in a bowl.
  2. Heat oil in a pan,add peanuts,urad/moong dal,stir for a min on low flame, add curry leaves,all chopped vegetables,stir well add salt and cook covered for few minutes until vegetables gets soft.
  3. Add red chilli powder and crumbled dhokla mix until well combined with veggies.
  4. Drizzle little water and cook covered for a min.
  5. Add fresh coriander and serve hot.
