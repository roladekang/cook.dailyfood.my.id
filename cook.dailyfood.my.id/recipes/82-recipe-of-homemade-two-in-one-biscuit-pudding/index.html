Recipe of Homemade Two in one biscuit pudding

The ingredients needed to make Two in one biscuit pudding:
  1. Prepare marie biscuits
  2. Get bourbon biscuit
  3. Make ready milk
  4. Take custard powder
  5. Prepare corn flour
  6. Take sugar
  7. Get butter
  8. Prepare choco powder
  9. Get choco sauce
Instructions to make Two in one biscuit pudding:
  1. Boil the milk and keep it cool.
  2. Make both type of biscuit crumbs by rolling pin
  3. Mix sugar and butter in marie biscuit crumbs
  4. Take some normal milk with custard powder and mix.
  5. Take some more milk with corn flour and mix
  6. Boil some milk in pan with sugar and butter
  7. Then add custard milk and stir with cornflour milk.
  8. Keep it cool and take another milk in pan
  9. Add sugar and choco powder in milk
  10. Then,add choco sauce in it and mix
  11. Stir and make a thick mixture
  12. Take glass and put biscuit crumbs layer
  13. Put custard milk on it
  14. Then,add choco biscuit crumbs on it
  15. Next,put another biscuit crumbs on it
  16. Then,add choco milk on it
  17. Add biscuit crumbs layer on it
  18. Keep them in freeze for 10 hours.
  19. After that,serve it as dessert
