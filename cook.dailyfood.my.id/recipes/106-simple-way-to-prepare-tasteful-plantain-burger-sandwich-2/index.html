The ingredients needed to make Plantain burger /sandwich 2:
  1. Make ready 6 Plantain
  2. Take Flour
  3. Make ready 1/2 tsp Salt
  4. Get Egg 2 egg
  5. Make ready Breadcrumbs
  6. Make ready Oil
  7. Prepare Chop onion
  8. Take Seasoning
  9. Prepare Spices
  10. Take Cook meat
  11. Make ready Salad
  12. Make ready Fried potato
Steps to make Plantain burger /sandwich 2:
  1. Peel and Cook plantain for 10 min,drain it and let it cook,mash it…add little salt
  2. Apply flour to ur hand,take a portion and make ur desired shape…do for the rest
  3. Whisk eggs(spices,seasoning), place breadcrumbs in a plate or bowl
  4. Put it in egg and in breadcrumbs
  5. Like diz
  6. Put little oil in pan and shallow fry,turn the other side
  7. For the sauce,add chop onion stir for 3min add cooked and slice meat and liver stir for 4 min add spice and seasoning keep stirring for to 3 min
  8. Add water and let it cook until it soak all the water
  9. Put the bread,put salad,add the meat
  10. Put the potato and add the bread
  11. Enjoy
