The ingredients needed to make Bombay street style Bhel:
  1. Take 4 tbsp Tomato ketchup
  2. Get 1 tomatoes medium sized finely chopped
  3. Get 2 Green chillies finely chopped
  4. Take 1 tsp Chaat masala
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup Sev
  6. Get 1 cup puffed rice
  7. Make ready 1 cup Mix bhujia
  8. Take 1 onions big size finely chopped
  9. Make ready 1 cup Salted dal( chana dal)
