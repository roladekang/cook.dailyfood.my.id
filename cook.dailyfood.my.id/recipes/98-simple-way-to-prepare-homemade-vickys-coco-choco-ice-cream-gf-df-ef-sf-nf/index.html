The ingredients needed to make Vickys Coco-Choco Ice Cream, GF DF EF SF NF:
  1. Make ready 240 ml coconut cream
  2. Make ready 240 ml full fat coconut milk (or oat milk for a milder taste)
  3. Prepare 80 grams granulated sugar
  4. Make ready 60 grams icing/powdered sugar
  5. Get 1 tsp ground cardamom
  6. Take 1 tsp xanthan gum
  7. Prepare 100 grams chocolate chips (I use Moo-Free or Enjoy Life brand)
Instructions to make Vickys Coco-Choco Ice Cream, GF DF EF SF NF:
  1. Add all ingredients into an ice cream maker, churn for 15 - 30 minutes then freeze as normal in suitable container
  2. Makes 1 litre, great served right out of the maker!
  3. You can exchange the chocolate chips for dessicated coconut and the cardamom for vanilla extract if preferred
  4. If you don't have an ice cream maker, blitz the ingredients apart from the choc chips together in a blender, then pour into a suitable lidded container
  5. Stir through the choc chips, put in the freezer, then stir every half hour until frozen to your preferred consistency
