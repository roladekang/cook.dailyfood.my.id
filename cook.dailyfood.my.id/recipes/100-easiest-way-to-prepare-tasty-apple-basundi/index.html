The ingredients needed to make Apple basundi:
  1. Take 1 ltr Milk
  2. Prepare 2 Grated apple
  3. Prepare 3 tbsp Sugar
  4. Take 1/2 tsp Elaichi powder
  5. Make ready Pista badam for garnishing

Steps to make Apple basundi:
  1. Take a heavy bottom pan.. add milk and sugar.. kaado (cook) for 15-20 min at simmer gas..
  2. Now add grated apple and elaichi powder.. simmer for 10-15 min.
  3. Keep stiring.. when the consistency becomes thick.. switch off the gas. Garnish with pista and bdam.
