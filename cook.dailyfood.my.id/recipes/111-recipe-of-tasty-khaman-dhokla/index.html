The ingredients needed to make Khaman Dhokla:
  1. Prepare 1 packet MTR khaman dokhla
  2. Prepare For chilli tangy dokhla
  3. Take 1 onion chopped with 1 green chilli
  4. Prepare 1 tomato chopped
  5. Take 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  6. Make ready 1 tsp tomato sauce
  7. Prepare Few curry leaves
  8. Get 2 tbsp Oil
  9. Make ready Few coriander leaves chopped
  10. Take to taste Salt
