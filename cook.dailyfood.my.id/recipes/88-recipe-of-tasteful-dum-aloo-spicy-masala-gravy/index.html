The ingredients needed to make Dum aloo spicy masala gravy:
  1. Take 1/2 kg small potatoes half boil
  2. Make ready 1/2 small bowl curd
  3. Make ready 1 tsp cumin seeds
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp red chilli powder
  5. Prepare 1 TSP turmeric powder
  6. Prepare 1 dry coconut
  7. Make ready 2 tsp Coriander powder
  8. Prepare 3 bay leaf
  9. Make ready 1 star phool
  10. Make ready 4 cloves
  11. Prepare 3 green cardamon
  12. Get 1 cinnamon stick
  13. Make ready 1 tbsp garam masala
  14. Take as needed Coriander leaves
  15. Make ready As needed Ginger garlic paste
  16. Get 4 tomatoes
  17. Prepare 2 onions
  18. Make ready Oil for frying
  19. Take 4 tbsp Red chilli powder
  20. Take as per taste Salt
  21. Take 2 tsp Anchur powder
Steps to make Dum aloo spicy masala gravy:
  1. First take a small potatoes in a pressure cooker pressure for 2 whistle
  2. After that take dry coconut, ginger garlic, onion, Coriander leaves chopped in to small pieces and grind make a fine paste keep a side, make a 4 tomatoes puree, take a pan add oil deep fry half boil potatoes and keep a side
