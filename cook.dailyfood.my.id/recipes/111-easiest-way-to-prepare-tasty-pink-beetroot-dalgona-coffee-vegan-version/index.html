The ingredients needed to make Pink (Beetroot) Dalgona Coffee: VEGAN VERSION:
  1. Make ready 1/2 Cup Fresh Milk (Boiled & Chilled): I’ve used herein the store bought & readymade SOYMILK, to keep it pure Vegan
  2. Make ready 3 tbsps Beetroot Juice (Freshly Cooked & Juice extracted)/ Alternatively, Pink Raspberry/Strawberry Powders can also be used
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp Castor Sugar (Any-White or Brown)
  4. Prepare 1 Instant Coffee Pouch or 1 tbsp
Steps to make Pink (Beetroot) Dalgona Coffee: VEGAN VERSION:
  1. In a mixing bowl: Add in all the aforementioned ingredients altogether
  2. With the help of an electric hand blender or even hand whisk- blend it for good 10-15 mins time (whisking takes more time than the electric hand blender)
  3. When the frothy stage comes in & starts showing its soft peaks- stop therein (that’s how I do like it to have it)
  4. Enjoy…Your unique VEGAN PINK DALGONA COFFEE
