The ingredients needed to make Carrot and walnut cake:
  1. Get Rice bran oil, to grease
  2. Get 1/2 c brown sugar
  3. Make ready 3/4 c rice bran oil
  4. Take 130 ml golden syrup
  5. Make ready 3 eggs
  6. Get 1 tsp vanilla essence
  7. Take 1/2 c plain flour
  8. Get 1 c self-raising flour
  9. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  10. Make ready 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  11. Make ready 350 g carrots, peeled and grated
  12. Prepare 1/2 c walnuts, chopped
  13. Prepare Icing sugar to spinkle
Instructions to make Carrot and walnut cake:
  1. Warm oven at 150C fan-forced or 170C. Grease a 20cm round cake pan lightly with oil, and line with non stick baking paper
  2. Add the brown sugar, oil, golden syrup, egg and vanilla in a separate bowl. Use a balloon whisk to mox until combined
  3. Sift the flours, baking soda, and cinnamon into a large bowl
  4. Pour the oil mixture into the dry ingredients. Use a wooden spoon to stir gently until just combined. Stir in the grated carrot and walnut
  5. Pour the mixture into mould and bake for 1 hour. Set aside for 5 minutes, before turning out onto a wire rack to cool completely
  6. Spingle with icing and serve
