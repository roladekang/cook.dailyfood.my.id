The ingredients needed to make Bacon, herb and vegetable pie:
  1. Make ready 30 g butter
  2. Get 1 clove garlic, crushed
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp plain flour
  4. Get 100 g button mushrooms, halved
  5. Make ready 6 slices bacon, chopped
  6. Prepare 1 large carrot, chopped
  7. Get 1 parsnip, chopped
  8. Prepare 2 leeks, thinly sliced
  9. Get 1 1/4 cups cream
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp rosemary
  11. Take Puff pastry
  12. Make ready 1 egg, beaten
Instructions to make Bacon, herb and vegetable pie:
  1. Heat butter, add garlic and onion. Cook over medium heat for 3 minutes. Add flour, stir for 1 min.
  2. Add mushrooms and bacon, cook for 5 minutes. Add carrot, parsnip and leek. Gradually stir in cream and rosemary. Bring to boil, reduce heat, cover, simmer 15 mins or until veg are tender.
  3. Spoon mixture into pie dish and cover with pastry. Use a knife/fork to peirce holes in pastry. Brush with egg and cook in oven until golden.
