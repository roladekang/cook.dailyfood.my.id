Instructions to make Fried plantain with fried egg and pap:
  1. Cut the plantain sprinkle salt on it and fry in vegetable oil.
  2. Cut onion,green pepper,red pepper.crack and beat the eggs add salt and curry fry onion and the peppers pour in the egg to fry.
  3. Dissolve the pap in cool water pour hot water over it till is thick.
