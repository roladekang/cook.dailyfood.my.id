The ingredients needed to make No onion Garlic Dum aloo:
  1. Take 250 gms baby potatoes
  2. Prepare 4 medium sized tomato (finely chopped)
  3. Prepare 2 green chillies
  4. Prepare 1 cup thick curd
  5. Take 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
  8. Take 1 tsp Chilli powder
  9. Take 1 tsp coriander powder
  10. Get 1 tsp kasuri methi
  11. Prepare 1 tsp sugar
  12. Make ready as per test Salt
  13. Make ready As required Oil for frying
Instructions to make No onion Garlic Dum aloo:
  1. Peel the potatoes and put it in pressure cooker with water and take 2 whistles on medium flame. Take them out and prick with fork. Deep fry them in oil on medium flame.
  2. Take a bowl of curd and mix salt, red chilli powder and coriander powder in it. Keep it aside.
  3. Now put a wok on flame heat some oil and add cumin and mustard seeds. Let them crackle. Now put tomatoes in it. Cover them for 3-4 mins. They will become soft and meshy. Switch off the flame and add pour curd mixture in it. Stir it well and again switch on the flame.
  4. Keep stirring it for some time and than Cover with lid. Let it cook for 5-7 mins. Check it out you will get oil floats on top now adjust consistency by adding water in it. Add kasuri methi, give it a mix. Now at last add fried potatoes in the greavy and cover it for 2-3 mins. So that potatoes will absorb the masala.
  5. Dum aloo is ready to serve now. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve with Poori or paratha.
