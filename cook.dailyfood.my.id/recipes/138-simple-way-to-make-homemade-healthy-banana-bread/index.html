The ingredients needed to make Healthy banana bread:
  1. Get Half a cup wholemeal flour
  2. Get Half a cup wheat flour
  3. Get Quarter a cup all-purpose flour
  4. Get Quarter a cup rolled oats
  5. Take 1 teaspoon baking soda
  6. Prepare Quarter teaspoon salt
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon cinnamon powder
  8. Get Quarter teaspoon nutmeg powder
  9. Make ready 2 tbsp hemp protein powder
  10. Take 2 eggs
  11. Prepare Quarter a cup dark brown sugar
  12. Prepare 3 tbsp honey
  13. Prepare 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  14. Get 1 mashed banana
Instructions to make Healthy banana bread:
  1. Preheat the oven 180 degrees Celsius
  2. In a bowl mix all dry ingredients set aside.
  3. In a separate bowl beat eggs sugar honey and oil add the vanilla extract and finally the mashed banana. Mix well
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture in batches and incorporate gently. - Pour the mixture in a baking tin. Sprinkle with some rolled oats. - Bake in the oven for 30 to 40 minutes
