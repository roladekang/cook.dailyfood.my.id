The ingredients needed to make Garlic Lachha Paratha:
  1. Get 2 Cup Wheat Flour/ Atta
  2. Get 1/2 Cup Maida/All-purpose Flour
  3. Prepare 1 Tsp Baking Soda
  4. Take to taste Salt
  5. Make ready 1 Tsp Sugar
  6. Make ready as needed Oil
  7. Get 4 Tbsp Butter
  8. Take 1 Tsp Crushed Garlic
  9. Prepare 1 Tbsp Chopped Coriander leaves
  10. Make ready as needed Ghee /Oil /Butter To make parathas
Steps to make Garlic Lachha Paratha:
  1. Cover and keep for 1/2 hour.
  2. Take a big ball of the dough, roll to make a big round, cut into strips.
  3. Apply the garlic, butter and coriander mixture on the same.
  4. Get all the strips together, roll and roll it.
  5. Now make an oval or round shape paratha and cook on hot tawa on medium flame on both sides till its cooked and crisp.
