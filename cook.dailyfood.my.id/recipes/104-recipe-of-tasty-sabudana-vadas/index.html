The ingredients needed to make Sabudana Vadas:
  1. Prepare 1 cup Sago Pearls or Sabudana
  2. Get 2 Potatoes parboiled
  3. Take 1 tsp Red chilli powder
  4. Prepare 1 tsp Salt
  5. Take 3 Green Chillies finely chopped
  6. Make ready 1/4 tsp Turmeric powder
  7. Prepare 1 tsp Cumin powder roasted
  8. Prepare 1 tsp Coriander powder roasted
  9. Take 1/4 cup Peanuts roasted
  10. Take 1/4 cup Coriander Leaves finely chopped
  11. Get 1/8 tsp Asafoetida
  12. Prepare 1 tsp Dry Mango powder or Amchur
Instructions to make Sabudana Vadas:
  1. Soak Sabudana overnight. Boil Potatoes before you proceed to make the Vadas. They should be firm yet soft. Mash them well and keep aside.
  2. Roast Peanuts and powder them coarsely in a dry jar grinder attachment. In a large bowl, mix all the ingredients listed. There is no need of any water at all. Shape them into circular or oblong or both. Flatten them a bit with your palm if making round tikkis or patties. If making oblong or cylindrical, shape them accordingly. Deep fry them on medium to high flame in hot oil.
  3. They get done very easily and does not take much time either. Serve them hot with green coconut chutney like me.
