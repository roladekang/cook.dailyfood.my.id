The ingredients needed to make Banana maple upsidedown cake:
  1. Make ready 50 g unsalted butter
  2. Get 100 g unsalted butter, softened,extra
  3. Get 55 g brown sugar
  4. Take 60 ml maple syrup
  5. Take 3-4 banana sliced
  6. Take 230 g caster sugar
  7. Get 4 egg
  8. Make ready 1 TSP vanilla extract
  9. Get 155 g plain(all purpose flour
  10. Prepare 1 TSP baking powder
  11. Prepare Pinch salt

Instructions to make Banana maple upsidedown cake:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180°c, to make the topping place the butter,brown sugar,and maple syrup in a small pan,cook on medium heat for 10 minutes or until the sugar melted and the syrup is rich and golden
  2. Pour the syrup into non stick springform cake tin and arrange the sliced bananas over the base of the tin
  3. To make a cake place extra butter and caster sugar in a bowl and beat until pale and creamy,add the eggs one at a time, beating after each addition then add the vanilla. Sift the flour,baking powder and salt and gently fold through the mixture. Spoon the batter evenly over the bananas caramel and smoth the top with spatula
  4. Place the cake in the oven baking tray to catch any escaping caramel and bake for 35 min or until a skewer inserted into the centre of the cake comes out clean
  5. Remove from the oven n leave in the tin for 5 min to cool slightly. Transfer to large serving plate
  6. Enjoy
