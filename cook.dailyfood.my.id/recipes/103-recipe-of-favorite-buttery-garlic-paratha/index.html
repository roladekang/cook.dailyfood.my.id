Recipe of Favorite Buttery Garlic Paratha

The ingredients needed to make Buttery Garlic Paratha:
  1. Make ready 1 lemon sized ball of dough.
  2. Get 1 tablespoon butter
  3. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon garlic paste
  4. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon finely chopped garlic cloves
  5. Take 1 teaspoon finely chopped fresh coriander
  6. Get 1 pinch red chilli powder
  7. Take to taste Black salt
  8. Get as needed Butter for frying the parathas
Steps to make Buttery Garlic Paratha:
  1. Take the butter in a bowl.
  2. Add the garlic paste and chopped garlic to it.
  3. Then add the salt and red chilli to it and mix well.
  4. Flatten the ball of the dough and roll it.
  5. Then apply the prepared garlic butter mixture on the chapati.
  6. Add some chopped coriander.
  7. And fold it in any shape you like.
  8. Then roll it again.
  9. Heat the tawa and put the paratha on it.
  10. After 2 mins flip the paratha and spread some butter.flip it again and spread the butter on this side also.
  11. Cook from both the sides. - And your buttery garlic Paratha is ready.
  12. Serve hot with curd or chutney.
