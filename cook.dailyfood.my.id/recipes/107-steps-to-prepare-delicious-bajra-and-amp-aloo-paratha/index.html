The ingredients needed to make Bajra & Aloo Paratha:
  1. Get 1 cup Bajra flour
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup wheat flour
  3. Prepare 1 cup boiled and mashed potatoes
  4. Take As per taste Salt
  5. Take 1 tsp chaat masala
  6. Prepare 11/2 tsp jeera powder
  7. Prepare 1 tsp ajwain
  8. Take 1 tsp garam masala
  9. Prepare As per taste Red chilli powder
  10. Take 3 finely chopped green chillies
  11. Get Handful finely chopped coriander
  12. Prepare As Required Oil for roasting
  13. Get as required Water
Instructions to make Bajra & Aloo Paratha:
  1. Take the boiled potatoes and cool completely. Mash it and keep it aside.
  2. Take 1cup bajra flour.
  3. Take 1/2 cup wheat flour.
  4. In a big bowl add mashed potatoes and bajra flour and wheat flour. Add salt, chaat masala, chopped green chillies, chopped coriander, ajwain, red chilli powder, garam masala.
  5. Mix everything well. Then add little water and make a tight dough. Cover and keep aside for 5 mins.
  6. Grease your palm and take a ball of dough and dust it with dry flour and roll little thick chapati.
  7. Take a pan and grease it and cook the paratha on medium flame by adding little oil or ghee from both the sides.
  8. Serve hot with curd.
