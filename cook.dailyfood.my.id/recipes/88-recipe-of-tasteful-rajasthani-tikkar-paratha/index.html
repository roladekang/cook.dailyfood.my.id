The ingredients needed to make Rajasthani Tikkar Paratha:
  1. Take 1/4 cup makki atta
  2. Prepare 1/4cup wheat flour
  3. Get 1/4 cup besan
  4. Make ready 1 small onion chopped
  5. Make ready 1 big tomato pureed
  6. Prepare 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  7. Take 2 tsp desi ghee
  8. Prepare 4 tsp fresh coriander
  9. Prepare 1/4 tsp haldi
  10. Prepare 1/4 tsp red chilli powder
  11. Make ready to taste Salt
  12. Get as per need Desi Ghee for cooking
Instructions to make Rajasthani Tikkar Paratha:
  1. Knead a dough with all the ingredients. No need to add water.
  2. Put 2-3tomato chunks and fresh coriander leaves on roti. Keep that side up. Coon and Apply ghee on both sides.
  3. Serve hot with curd/green chutney/aloo pyaz sabzi
