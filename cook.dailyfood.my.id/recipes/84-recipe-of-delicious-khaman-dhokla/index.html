Dhokla is a vegetarian culinary dish that is found mainly in the Indian state of Gujarat and parts of adjacent states. It is made with a fermented batter derived from rice and split urad dal.

Instructions to make Khaman dhokla:
  1. FOR GARNISHING: - chopped coriander leaves, some mustard seeds, whole red chillies and some black pepper along with oil.
  2. Wash the rice and urad dal properly until the water runs clear. Now put the rice and dal mix in a bowl and cover with warm water and soak for 3 hrs
  3. Tempering: Add oil in a kadai. Upon heating, add mustard seeds, red chillies, black pepper, a pinch of salt and sugar along with water. Bring to a boil and with a spoon add the tempering to the dhokla.
  4. The Khaman Dhokla is ready to be served.
