Steps to make Delicious Banana Crumble:
  1. Pre heat oven to gas mark 5.
  2. Peel 2 bananas & place into a blender & blend until it resembles a creamy mix. Chop remaining 4 bananas into half inch circles & place into a 20cm glass dishe with the banana puree & mix.
  3. Add milk, brown sugar & cinnamon/cake spice mix & mix it in well with the cut bananas.
  4. Once everything has been mixed, combine porridge oats & granola cereal & evenly spread onto bananas until completely covered.
  5. Place into the middle of the oven for 40 minutes or until the crumble mixture is lightly brown & golden
