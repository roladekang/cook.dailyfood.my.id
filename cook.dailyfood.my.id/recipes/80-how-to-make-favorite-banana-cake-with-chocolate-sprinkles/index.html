Steps to make Banana Cake with Chocolate Sprinkles:
  1. Combine ingredients in Part A in a bowl and beat until a little pale.
  2. Add part B into part A (beaten). Mix well. Add mashed bananas and chocolate sprinkles.
  3. Pour batter into baking dish and bake at 160°C for 45 minutes. Sprinkle some chocs on top.
