The ingredients needed to make KFC Chicken Wrap:
  1. Take 1 KFC Spicy Chicken Drumstick
  2. Prepare 1 Cucumber
  3. Make ready 2 slices cheese
  4. Get 2 pieces roti wrap
  5. Get Some chilli sauce
Instructions to make KFC Chicken Wrap:
  1. Cut the cucumber into small pieces. Any shape you like, how thin is the cucumber depends on your patience.
  2. Slice the chicken meat. The thinner the chicken meat, the more wraps you can produce.
  3. The skillful part is over. Now, let's put all together. Btw, the gardenia packaging is resealable, so you may keep the remaining wraps. Remember don't cut the packaging like I did.
  4. La la la~ wrap + cheese + chicken meat + cucumber + chilli sauce
  5. Ta da~ Done! Repeat the steps for second wrap. If you are curious why the wrap looks so nice, you may refer to the back of the packaging.
