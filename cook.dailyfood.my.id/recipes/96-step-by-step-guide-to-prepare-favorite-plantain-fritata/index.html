The ingredients needed to make Plantain fritata:
  1. Get Plantain
  2. Make ready Know
  3. Prepare Black pepper
  4. Make ready Egg
  5. Prepare Yellow pepper
  6. Make ready Green pepper
  7. Prepare Salt
  8. Prepare Onion
  9. Prepare Red pepper
  10. Get Carrot
  11. Make ready Green beans
  12. Take Oil
Instructions to make Plantain fritata:
  1. Kifere pawon plantain dinki kiyanka round round sai kidaura mai awuta idan yayi zafi saiki zuba plantain dinki kisoya idan yasoyu sai kikwashe kisa aside.
  2. Ki wanke su onion, green pepper, red pepper, carrot,green beans, yellow pepper bayan kin wanke saiki yankasu round.
  3. Kidaura pan awuta sai kisa mai Dan dadai idan yayi zafi sai kizuba su tattasai dinki da kika yanka kiyi frying dinsu sama sama sai ki kwashe.
  4. Kisa aroba kibari yayi sanyi sai kifasa kwai aciki kisa salt,know, black pepper, curry powder sai kikada sosai idan yakadu sai kidauko baking pan dinki kishafa mai ajikin pan sai kijuye.
  5. Bayan kin juye sai kidauko plantain dinki kisassaka asama kidan danna sai kisa a oven kiyi baking.
