The ingredients needed to make Halwa paratha platter:
  1. Take For halwa
  2. Make ready 1 cup semolina
  3. Get 1 cup milk
  4. Prepare 1 cup sugar
  5. Make ready 3 cup water
  6. Take 1/2 tsp green cardamom powder
  7. Prepare handful raisins
  8. Prepare as needed Chopped dry fruit
  9. Make ready For paratha
  10. Take 1 cup whole wheat flour
  11. Prepare to taste Salt
  12. Take 2 tbsp any vegetable oil for cooking
  13. Make ready 1 cup moong dal
  14. Get 1/4 cup urad dal
  15. Get 1/2 tbs red chilli powder
  16. Get 1 tbsp coriander powder
  17. Get 1 green chilli chopped
  18. Take 1 inch ginger
  19. Take 3 tsp coriander leaves
  20. Make ready For gravy aloo
  21. Take 2 boiled potatoes
  22. Get 1 cup tomato puree
  23. Get 1 green chilli chopped
  24. Take 1 tbsp ginger paste
  25. Take 2 tbsp desi ghee
  26. Take 1/2 tbs cumin seeds
  27. Prepare 1/4 tsp Asafoetida
  28. Prepare 1/2 tbsp red chilli powder
  29. Get 1 tbsp coriander powder
  30. Get 1/2 tbs turmeric powder
  31. Take 1/2 tbs garam masala powder
  32. Take 2 tbsp curd
  33. Make ready 1 tbsp kasoori methi
  34. Take 1 tbsp laungi masala
  35. Take 2 tsp Fresh coriander for garnish
Steps to make Halwa paratha platter:
  1. For halwa - In a bowl sieve sammolina and mix milk and make smooth paste and rest for about 10 minute.
  2. In a nonstick voke add ghee when hot add paste of milk and sammolina and stir continuously otherwise stick on bottom.
  3. On other flame in a pan take 3 cup water and sugar and boil it add green cardamom powder and washed raisins and keep a side.
  4. When sammolina properly roasted and remove from sides of kadai add sugar syrup and stir continuously add chopped dry fruit and cover it for 5 minute on medium flame.
  5. For paratha - wash and soak both dal about 1 hour. Now in a blender jar add green chilli,ginger chopped and spices and make smooth paste.
  6. For dough in a parat wheat flour and salt and make dough with help of water and rest for about 10 minutes.
  7. In the meantime in a kadai add desi ghee when hot add Asafoetida and cumin seeds when cracked add chopped green chilli and ginger paste stir it now add tomato puree stir it now add spices and laungi masala.
  8. Add 2 tbsp curd and kasoori methi stir it now add 1 glass of water and boiled mashed potatoes. Cook about 5 minutes.
  9. Add garam masala powder and fresh coriander.
  10. For paratha - take small size dough roll it and place on hot tawa. Now apply dal paste with help of ladle or spoon like thin layer. Now apply oil or butter and cook from both sides till golden brown.
  11. Remove on plate and serve with gravy aloo and halwa.
  12. This halwa is made from sammolina but its texture is like moong dal halwa and preparation is like it.If we want to make halwa in hurry so we can try it in place of moong dal halwa.
