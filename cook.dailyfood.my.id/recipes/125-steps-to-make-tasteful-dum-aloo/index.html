Steps to Make Tasteful Dum Aloo

The ingredients needed to make Dum Aloo:
  1. Prepare 250 gms Baby potatoes
  2. Make ready 2 onion chopped
  3. Prepare 1 cup Curd
  4. Prepare 1 tsp Cumin seeds
  5. Get 2 Tomato puree
  6. Make ready 2 tsp Ginger garlic paste
  7. Prepare 1 tsp Kashmiri red chilii powder
  8. Make ready 1 tsp Coriander seeds
  9. Prepare 1 cup Water
  10. Make ready 1 tsp Kasuri methi
  11. Prepare 3/4 tsp Salt
  12. Get As required Oil sufficient for frying
  13. Prepare 1 Bay leaf
  14. Take Handful Fresh Coriander leaves for Garnishing
Steps to make Dum Aloo:
  1. Wash the potatoes, Boil them and peel them. Now prick the boil potatoes with fork or toothpick and fry them in hot oil on medium high flame until crisp and turns golden brown and Drain them in a plate on tissue paper to remove excess oil and keep aside.
  2. In same oil, add cumin seeds, bay leaf and chopped onions /Onion paste and ginger garlic paste and saute all well and cook till they turn translucent.
  3. Now add salt, red chilli powder and coriander seeds and tomato puree and stir well. Now add curd and cook until oil starts separating.
  4. Cook the masala well and then add fried potatoes, add water and kasuri methi and mix all well and bring it to a boil on medium flame.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and Serve hot with roti and rice.
