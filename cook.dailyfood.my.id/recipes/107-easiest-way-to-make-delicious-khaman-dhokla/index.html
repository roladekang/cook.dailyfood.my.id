The ingredients needed to make Khaman dhokla:
  1. Prepare 1 cup besan
  2. Get 2 ¹/² cups water
  3. Prepare 1 tsp sugar
  4. Make ready 1 ³/⁴ tsps lemon juice

  8. Take 15 curry leaves
  9. Get 1 tsp mustard seeds
  10. Take 1 tsp coconut powder
  11. Make ready garnishing
  12. Make ready 4 sliced green chillies

Instructions to make Khaman dhokla:
  1. Take a bowl & add besan, water, lemon juice & baking soda. Mix well.Allow it to ferment for 1-2 hrs.
  2. In the meantime,pour boil water in the steamer & grease the utensil with oil.
  3. Pour the batter in the utensil & steam in low flame for 15-20 mins.
  4. Check with knife after 15 mins by inserting it inside the dhokla.If the knife comes out clean,it means the dhokla is done.
  5. Allow the dish to cool & the cut it into pieces.
  6. FOR TEMPERING:Heat oil in a pan.Once it is hot enough, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, chopped chillies & allow it to boil.On it squeeze lemon, add sugar & green coriander leaves.
  7. Once done,pour the tempering on the dhokla & enjoy pairing with fafda & jalebi.
