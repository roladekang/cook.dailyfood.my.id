The ingredients needed to make Plantain pancake:
  1. Get plantain
  2. Take flour
  3. Prepare eggs
  4. Make ready small onions
  5. Make ready fresh Pepper
  6. Make ready milk
  7. Take salt
  8. Make ready Sugar (optional)
  9. Get butter
  10. Get Water
Steps to make Plantain pancake:
  1. Wash, peel and blend your plantain to smooth
  2. Diced your onions and peppers melt your butter now add the pepper, onions, melted butter, milk and egg to the batter and blend together for some minutes
  3. Now add salt and sugar to the flour mix
  4. In a clean bow pour your batter and add in the flour little by little to your desire thickness, oil your pan and fry little by little on low heat
