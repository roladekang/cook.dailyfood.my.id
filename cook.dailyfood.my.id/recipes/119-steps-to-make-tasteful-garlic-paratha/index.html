Steps to Make Tasteful Garlic Paratha

The ingredients needed to make Garlic Paratha:
  1. Get 1 bowl fresh garlic
  2. Take 1 bowl wheat flour
  3. Prepare 1 spoon red chilli powder
  4. Get to taste Salt
  5. Take 1/2 spoon dry Coriander powder
  6. Make ready as needed Cooking oil
  7. Take as needed Drinking water
  8. Get 1 cup wheat flour
Steps to make Garlic Paratha:
  1. Take clen wash drain or wipe fresh garlic chopped little transfer in mixi jar blend in pulses don't make paste. or puree.. In big bowl take wheat flour add 1-2 spoon oil and knhed dough in medium cover for 5 min.
  2. Make small size balls dust in dry wheat flour and roll thick chapati add salt red chilli powder dry Coriander powder add 1/2 spoon oil n spread well now add fresh garlic all over spices press with fingers
  3. Fold carefully dust with dry flour roll a paratha
  4. Heat up 1 spoon oil on graddle transfer paratha on it roast both sides till golden in colour spread little oil both sides.
  5. Your tasty paratha is ready Enjoy. Thank-you.
