Instructions to make Valentine Fruit Pudding:
  1. Make a custard with milk, sugar and whipped cream.
  2. Add fruits and nuts.Keep it in fridge to cool down.
  3. Decorate a glass with chocolate syrup.
  4. Start Layering - 1st Sponge cake - 2nd Custard - 3rd Fruits and nuts - Repeat the process till the glass is full.
  5. Garnish it with strawberry and chocolate syrup. - Put it in the fridge to cool. - Serve cool. - Enjoy :)
