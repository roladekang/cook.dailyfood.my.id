The ingredients needed to make Dum aloo biryani:
  1. Prepare For biryani
  2. Get 200 gm biryani rice
  3. Make ready 2 green cardamom
  4. Get 2 black cardamom
  5. Get 1 bay leave
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp ghee
  7. Prepare as per taste Salt
  8. Get as required Water
  9. Make ready For marination
  10. Get 2 tbsp hung curd
  11. Get 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  12. Take 1/4 tps haldi
  13. Make ready 1/2 tsp Coriander powder
  14. Prepare 1 tsp kashmiri chilli
  15. Make ready to taste Salt
  16. Take 1 tsp Pudina,
  17. Prepare 1 tsp Fresh Coriander
  18. Take For dum aloo
  19. Take 5 big potatoes cut into two pieces
  20. Take 2 big sliced onion
  21. Make ready 2 green chilli
  22. Get 1/2 tbsp cumin seeds
  23. Prepare as required Some whole garam masala
  24. Take 1 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
  25. Prepare As per requirement Biryani essence
  26. Take as required Water
  27. Take 2 tomato finely chopped
  28. Take 3 medium onion for fried
  29. Take 2 tbsp ghee
  30. Take 2 tbsp mustard oil
Instructions to make Dum aloo biryani:
  1. Take a pan add water,black and green cardamom,bay leaves,and salt, stir it now add biryani rice in it put some ghee cooked it 1/3, then take a strainer and strain the rice keep it aside
  2. Take a pan add little more oil for potato frying,keep it aside and we can fried our onion in this oil
  3. Take another bowl add hung curd and marination item like red chilli, Coriander,salt, haldi little pudina,some fried onions,put our fried potatoes in it and mix it well and keep aside for 15 min
  4. Take a another pan heat it and pour mustard oil along with ghee, add cumin and tadka like green chilli, whole garam masala and saute it for some tym now add sliced onion and saute it for 5 min now add our marinated potato into it and saute well for 5,7 minth in medium flame, now add tomato and rest of dry spices add some water salt and cook for 2,3 min
  5. Now it's time to layer,in this dam aloo mixture now we,ll add our semi cooked rice in it, sprinkle some fried onions,bunch of pudina and fresh Coriander put some ghee and little water as required, now covered it properly on vry slow flame for 10 min and add biryani essence on the top
  6. Now off the flame, and ready to plate we,ll garnish with some fried onions and mint leaves, and serve with cucumber raita
