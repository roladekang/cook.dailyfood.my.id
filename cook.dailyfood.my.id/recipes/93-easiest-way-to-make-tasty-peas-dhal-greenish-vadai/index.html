Easiest Way to Make Tasty Peas dhal greenish vadai

To get started with this recipe, we must first prepare a few ingredients. You can have peas dhal greenish vadai using 10 ingredients and 3 steps. Here is how you can achieve it.

The ingredients needed to make Peas dhal greenish vadai:
  1. Get 400 g peas dhal
  2. Get 1 cup Greenish (keerai)
  3. Get Ginger garlic paste
  4. Take 3 Big Onion
  5. Make ready 13 green chilli
  6. Take 1 spoon Fennel seed
  7. Get 1/2 spoon Asafoetida
  8. Get leaves Curry
  9. Take leaves Coriander
  10. Take leaf Mint
Steps to make Peas dhal greenish vadai:
  1. Step 1: 45 mints peas dhal has to soak it, chop small size all onion,tomato, curry leaf, coriander leaf,mint leaf,greenish, green chilli
  2. Step 2: grind peas dhal transfer to bowl and add all chopped ingredients add salt to taste, asafoetida,fennel seed mix it well and make it small balls and 2 time press it
  3. Step 3: Take a pan heat the oil and put vadai into the pan once brown colour both the side transfer into plate and serve it yummmmy peas dhal greenish vadai.
