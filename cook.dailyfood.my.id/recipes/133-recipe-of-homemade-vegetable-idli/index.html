The ingredients needed to make Vegetable idli:
  1. Get 3 cup idli batter
  2. Prepare 2 Onions
  3. Get 1 mix chopped vegetables(cabbage capsicum carrots beet tomatoes coriander)
  4. Prepare 2 Green chillies
  5. Get 1 piece small ginger
  6. Prepare 1 tsp Mustard seeds
  7. Get 1 tsp chana dal
  8. Prepare 1 tsp urad dal
  9. Prepare 10 curry leaves
  10. Prepare 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  11. Prepare To taste Salt
