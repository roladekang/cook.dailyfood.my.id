The ingredients needed to make Veg Manchurian:
  1. Get 1/2 cabbage
  2. Get 2 carrot
  3. Get 2 capsicum
  4. Take 2 spoon all purpose flour
  5. Prepare 4+2 tsp corn flour
  6. Make ready to taste Salt And red chilli powder
  7. Take 2 big tomato
  8. Make ready 5-6 pieces garlic
  9. Take As needed Oil
  10. Get As needed Water
Instructions to make Veg Manchurian:
  1. Firstly peeled all vegetables and grated it now add 1 spoon salt half spoon red chilli powder And 4 spoon corn flour And 2 spoon all purpose flour mix it and make small balls
  2. Now heat oil fry all balls,make tomato And garlic paste
  3. Now take another pan heat 2 spoon oil add tomato And garlic paste fry it now add corn flour mix well add 2 spoon corn flour fand salt and red chilli powder as required now add water as u like boil it
  4. Lastly add all balls in it mix well garnish it with tomato cover flower
