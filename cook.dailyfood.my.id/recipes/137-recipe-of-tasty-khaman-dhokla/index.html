The ingredients needed to make Khaman Dhokla:
  1. Prepare 5 cup Besan
  2. Get 3 cup Curd
  3. Make ready 1 cup Water
  4. Prepare 1 tsp Turmeric
  5. Make ready 7 tsp Sugar
  6. Get 2 Lemon
  7. Make ready 3 tbsp Refind oil
  8. Prepare 2 tsp Soda
  9. Prepare 1 tsp Rai
  10. Take to taste Salt
  11. Get 30 Curry leaves
  12. Take 5-6 Whole chilli

Instructions to make Khaman Dhokla:
  1. Make a paste of besan curd and water keep it for fermentation for 6 to 8 hours
  2. Add salt sugar turmeric 1 lemon juice 2 tsp oil and soda.
  3. Grease the dhokla plate and pour the batter in it.
  4. Now boil 2 glass water in dhokla cooker and steam dhokla for 15 mins.
