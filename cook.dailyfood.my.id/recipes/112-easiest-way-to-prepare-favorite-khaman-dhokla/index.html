The ingredients needed to make Khaman Dhokla:
  1. Make ready 1 cup besan
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup curd
  3. Get 1/8 tsp turmeric
  4. Take 1/2 tsp green chilli paste
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp fruit salt
  6. Take 1 tsp salt
  7. Take 1 tbsp lemon juice
  8. Get 2 tbsp water
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp sugar
  10. Prepare For tempering
  11. Make ready 2 tbsp oil
  12. Get 1 tsp mustard seeds
  13. Get 2 green chillies slit
  14. Get 1/2 cup water
  15. Make ready 1 pinch sugar
  16. Prepare 1 pinch salt
  17. Get as needed Fresh coconut & coriander for garnish

Steps to make Khaman Dhokla:
  1. In a mixing bowl take Besant, add curd, water, green chilli paste, salt, turmeric and sugar and mix it nicely.
  2. Then add fruit salt, lemon juice mix it nicely and pour the batter in a greased plate and steam the dhokla for 15-20min on medium high flame.
  3. For tempering heat oil in a pan, then add mustard seeds when the oil becomes hot, add green chillies, salt, sugar and pour water on it, give it one boil and switch off the flame.
  4. Once the dhokla is out of the steamer, cool it for 5min, cut it into pieces and pour the tempered mixture on it, garnish with fresh coconut & chopped coriander.
