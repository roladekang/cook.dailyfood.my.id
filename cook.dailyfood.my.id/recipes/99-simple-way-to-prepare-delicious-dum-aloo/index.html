Simple Way to Prepare Delicious Dum Aloo

The ingredients needed to make Dum Aloo:
  1. Get 8 medium sized potatoes boiled
  2. Make ready 1 cup curd
  3. Get 2 tsp fennel seeds powder
  4. Get 2 tsp coriander powder
  5. Get 1 tbsp sonth/dry ginger powder
  6. Get 1 and 1/2 tsp chilli powder
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp chilli paste
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp ginger paste
  9. Make ready 1 tsp garam masala
  10. Get 2 bay leaves
  11. Prepare 4 peppercorns
  12. Prepare 3 cloves
  13. Get 1 tsp jeera /cumin seeds
  14. Get 1 stick cinnamon
  15. Make ready 2 coriander leaves finely chopped
  16. Make ready As per taste Salt
  17. Make ready as required Oil
Steps to make Dum Aloo:
  1. Peel the potatoes and shallow fry them.
  2. Make paste by adding chilli powder, coriander powder, fennel seed powder, ginger to curd and blend it well.
  3. Heat oil in kadai, add jeera, bay leaf, cloves, cinnamon stick peppercorns saute for 20 secs add chilli paste, curd mixture and mix it well. Cook on sim till done.
  4. Add potatoes mix it well, cover cook for sometime or till oil seperates, add salt, garam masala give it a good mixer.
  5. Sprinkle coriander leaves on top and serve with rice and roti.
