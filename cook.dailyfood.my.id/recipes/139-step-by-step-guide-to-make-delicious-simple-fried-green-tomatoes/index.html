The ingredients needed to make simple fried green tomatoes:
  1. Prepare 5 green tomatoes
  2. Get 2 eggs
  3. Get 1/3 cup milk
  4. Prepare 2/3 cup jiffy baking mix (or flour)
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp parsley
  6. Take 2 tbsp creole seasoning
  7. Get 1 oil

Steps to make simple fried green tomatoes:
  1. I don't usually measure my ingredients. so this is a round about guess of measurements. I hope you enjoy it!
  2. heat oil in skillet
  3. slice tomatoes 1/2 in thick.
  4. in mixing bowl mix 2 eggs and milk. add in the jiffy mix and stir until clumps are gone. if the batter is too thin add more mix, if its too thick add more milk. next add parsley, Creole seasoning and stir.
  5. dip sliced tomatoes into batter. be sure to cover them completely. place in hot oil. let cook until golden brown and flip. (I find it easier to use tongs.)
  6. set out a plate with a few layers of paper towel. let cool and enjoy!
