The ingredients needed to make Vickys Quick Microwave Banana Cake, GF DF EF SF NF:
  1. Take 100 grams sunflower spread / butter
  2. Get 100 grams sugar, white or brown
  3. Take 2 medium over ripe bananas, well mashed
  4. Prepare 3 tbsp milk of choice - gluten free flour will need a little extra
  5. Make ready 100 grams plain / gluten-free flour
  6. Make ready 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  7. Get 3/4 tsp baking powder
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking soda / bicarb

Steps to make Vickys Quick Microwave Banana Cake, GF DF EF SF NF:
  1. Put the butter into a microwave safe glass dish or bowl, litre sized, and heat on high until melted, 30 seconds or so
  2. Mix the milk, sugar and mashed bananas into the melted butter, then mix in the flour, cinnamon, baking powder and soda
  3. Heat on high (my microwave is 800W) for 8 - 10 minutes or until risen and cooked right through. It'll pull away from the sides of the bowl when done
  4. Serve immediately with custard, ice cream or caramel sauce
