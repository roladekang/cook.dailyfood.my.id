The ingredients needed to make Bajra Methi paratha:
  1. Make ready 1 cup Bajra flour
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup Wheat flour
  3. Prepare 1 cup cleaned and chopped Methi leaves
  4. Take 1 tbsp curd
  5. Take 1 Green chilli, chopped(optional)
  6. Get 1/4 tsp Haldi powder
  7. Get 1/4 tsp Red chilli powder
  8. Prepare to taste Salt
  9. Prepare as required Oil to tawa fry
Instructions to make Bajra Methi paratha:
  1. Take both flours and rest of the ingredients in a bowl and knead a firm dough with lukewarm water.
  2. Pinch the dough and roll them like balls.
  3. Now Roll them out into parantha and roast on a tawa applying oil till they are nicely browned.
  4. Serve hot with any sabzi or Any chutney.
