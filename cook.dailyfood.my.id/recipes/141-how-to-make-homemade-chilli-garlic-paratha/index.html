The ingredients needed to make Chilli Garlic paratha:
  1. Prepare 1 cup wheat flour
  2. Take 1 cup maida/All-purpose Flour
  3. Prepare 2 cup water
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp sugar
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp salt
  6. Prepare 1 full sized chopped garlic
  7. Take 1 tbsp chilli flakes
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp amul butter
Instructions to make Chilli Garlic paratha:
  1. Melt 2 tbsp amul butter and add 2 tbsp chilli flakes in it and mix it well
  2. Now add chopped garlic in butter mixture and mix well
  3. Now take another bowl and add 1 cup of wheat flour and 1 cup maida in it
  4. Take 2 cups of water and add slowly in the bowl while mixing.mix it well leaving no lumps behind
  5. Now add butter mixture in it and again mix well also add 1 tbsp sugar and 1 tbsp salt in flour mixture
  6. Garnish it with kasoori methi and let it rest for 5 mins
  7. Now take a nonstick pan and heat it on low flame
  8. Now take a small amount of mixture and spread it in a circular shape.as shown.don't spread neither thick nor thin
  9. Cook it on medium flame and flip it upside down once it is half cooked from one side
  10. Then spread butter over both the sides like you do for parathas
  11. Chilli garlic paranthas are ready without rolling with just spreading the mixture
  12. Niw serve it with sauce and enjoy.
