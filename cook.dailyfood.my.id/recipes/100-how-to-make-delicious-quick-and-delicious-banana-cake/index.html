The ingredients needed to make Quick and Delicious Banana Cake:
  1. Make ready 280 g plain wholemeal flour
  2. Get 280 g plain sugar
  3. Get 200 g mashed banana
  4. Get 2 eggs
  5. Make ready 1 tsp baking powder
  6. Make ready 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  7. Get 140 g butter

Steps to make Quick and Delicious Banana Cake:
  1. Gather your ingredients together. This is going to be quick!
  2. Melt the butter. I put the butter in a microwave for 30 seconds and leave it out to melt completely.
  3. Add the eggs and sugar together in a mixing bowl and mix them together. I like to use an electric whisk.
  4. Add the mashed bananas to the bowl and blend it into your mixture
  5. Finally, add the flour, baking soda, baking powder and cinnamon. Blend these into the mixture thoroughly.
  6. Pour the mixture into a loaf tray lined with some baking parchment.
  7. Put into an oven at 170°/gas mark 3 and leave to bake for about an hour
  8. Take the cake out of the oven and leave it to cool. Make sure it's done by poking through it with a skewer.
  9. Slice up and enjoy on it's own or served with some ice cream
