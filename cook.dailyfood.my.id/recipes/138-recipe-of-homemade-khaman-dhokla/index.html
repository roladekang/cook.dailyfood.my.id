The ingredients needed to make Khaman Dhokla:
  1. Take 1 cup Gram flour
  2. Get 2 tablespoon Semolina
  3. Prepare To taste Salt
  4. Get 1 tablespoon Eno
  5. Make ready 4 tablespoon Curd
  6. Make ready 1 tablespoon Lime juice
  7. Take 1 tablespoon Coriander leaves (finely chopped)
  8. Prepare 5-6 Green chilli
  9. Take 1 pinch Asafoetida
  10. Take 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds
  11. Get 2 tablespoon Dry coconut (grated)
  12. Make ready 1 tablespoon Sugar
  13. Prepare 1/3 cup Water
  14. Get as needed Mustard Oil

Instructions to make Khaman Dhokla:
  1. First of all collect all the ingredients for khaman dhokla batter.
  2. Then collect all the ingredients for tempering.
  3. In a bowl, mix all the ingredients for khaman dhokla and make a batter of it.
  4. Then grease any plate or any vessel of your choice and pour khaman dhokla's batter into it.
  5. Meanwhile, heat your steamer by adding water in it and then place that vessel in which you've poured your batter.
  6. Let it cook for around 20 minutes then switch off the flame and allow it to cool down.
  7. For tempering, heat a tablespoon of oil in a pan, into it add mustard seeds, asafoetida, green chilli (slited) and let it splatter.
  8. Then add water and sugar into it and cook it for one boil and switch off the flame.
  9. Cut your khaman dhokla into square pieces or as per your choice and then drizzle tempering onto it.
  10. Garnish your khaman dhokla with grated coconut and coriander leaves.
