The ingredients needed to make Choco Apple smoothie:
  1. Get 1 cup peeled apples
  2. Make ready 2 cups milk
  3. Prepare 2 tbls sweet yogurt
  4. Prepare 1 tbls condensed milk
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup coconut milk
  6. Get 1/4 cup whipped cream
  7. Take 1 tbls Nutella
  8. Prepare Ice
Steps to make Choco Apple smoothie:
  1. Wash and peel the apple then cut into cubes.
  2. In a jug of blender add apples, condensed milk and coconut milk blend till apple turn in puree.
  3. Add sweet greek yogurt and milk with ice and blend for a minute.
  4. Mix Nutella into whipped cream and put into piping bag.
  5. Pipe the chocolate cream on top of the choco Apple smoothie and serve.
