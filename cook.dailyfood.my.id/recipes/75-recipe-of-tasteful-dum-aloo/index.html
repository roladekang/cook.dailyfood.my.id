The ingredients needed to make Dum aloo:
  1. Get 250 grams baby potatoes
  2. Prepare 4 chopped onions
  3. Prepare 4 chopped tomatoes
  4. Make ready 1 tablespoon ginger -garlic -green chilli paste
  5. Get 2 tbsp curd
  6. Prepare to taste Salt
  7. Make ready 2 tbsp cashew
  8. Get 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  9. Prepare 1 tablespoon kitchen King masala
  10. Prepare 2 tbsp coriander seed powder
  11. Make ready 4 tbsp oil
  12. Get as per need Coriander for garnish
Steps to make Dum aloo:
  1. First halfly boiled potatoes. Then peel it. Make hole all in potatoes with knife. Add curd,red chilli powder,garam masala. coriander seed powder and salt.
  2. Mix all ingredients well then cover it and keep it aside for a while
  3. Now take a pan add oil keep it on a medium flame. When oil is heated add the chopped onions and ginger garlic Green chili paste..
  4. Onion till it turns pinkish brown then add the tomatoes and cashew. Cook it for few minutes
  5. Now grind the mixture
  6. Not same pan add oil fry the potatoes that keeped side. Cook till all ingredients mix with potatoes
  7. Then add gravy in potatoes and masale according to gravy. Mix well. Cook it for few minutes
  8. Then add water in gravy. Cover the pan let the gravy and potato mix with each other.
  9. After few minutes when gravy get thick. Garnish it with coriander. dum aloo is ready to serve.
