The ingredients needed to make Khaman dhokla:
  1. Get 1 cup chik pea flour (besan)
  2. Get 3/4 cup warm water
  3. Make ready 2 medium size lemon juice
  4. Get 1 tbsp sugar
  5. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  6. Make ready 1 tsp eno
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking powder
  8. Get 2 tbsp oil
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  10. Prepare For tempering
  11. Get 2-3 green chilli cut long piece
  12. Make ready 7-8 curry leaves
  13. Make ready 1 tsp Mustard seed
  14. Prepare 1 tbsp sugar
  15. Prepare 1/2 cup water
  16. Prepare Coriander leaves for garnish
  17. Take Grated coconut for garnish (optional)

Instructions to make Khaman dhokla:
  1. Take a bowl add oil, sugar, warm water,turmeric powder, lemon juice mix it well then add besan whisk till smooth texture at least 5 min with whisker rest it for 10 min aside
  2. Then turn on the gas get ready for steam preheat the steamer
  3. Then take a mixture of besan add baking powder and eno whisk it
  4. Then grease the dhokla plate or thali with oil pour the mixture
  5. Then place plate or thali in a steamer steam for 20 min in medium flame
  6. After dhokla is ready cool for 5 min cut the dhokla with knife
  7. Then make a tempering heat the oil in a tadka pan add mustard seed after crackle of seed then add curry leaves and green chilli water then sugar boil for 1 min
  8. Then pour and spread the tadka in a dhokla plate wait for 5 min then garnish with spread of chopped coriander leaves and grated coconut then dhokla is ready to serve
  9. Texture of dhokla prefect spongy and soft
