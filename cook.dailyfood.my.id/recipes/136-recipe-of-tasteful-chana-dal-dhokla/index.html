The ingredients needed to make Chana Dal Dhokla:
  1. Take 1 cup Channa dal soaked overnight
  2. Take 1 tbsp sooji
  3. Make ready 3-4 green chilli
  4. Get 2 tbsp Dahi
  5. Take 1 tsp salt
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp Baking soda
  7. Make ready 1 tsp Haldi
  8. Take 1 tsp Eno

Instructions to make Chana Dal Dhokla:
  1. Soak dal over night. Grind it in a mixer with salt
  2. Take out the paste in a bowl and add, haldi, sooji, green chilli, soda n mix well.
  3. Grease dhokla pan and pour in paste in the pan - Keep a big pan on the gas and keep dhokla pan in that - Let it steam for 15-20 mins or till its done - Check with a knife if it‚Äôs done and then take it out.
  4. For tadka - Take oil in a pan, add mustard seeds - Let them crackle - Add white sea same, whole green chillies n coriander leaves - 1tsp lemon juice
  5. Garnish dhokla with tadka and serve with chutney of your choice
