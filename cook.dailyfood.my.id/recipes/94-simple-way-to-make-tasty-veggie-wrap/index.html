Simple Way to Make Tasty Veggie wrap

by Jennie Sparks

The ingredients needed to make Veggie wrap:
  1. Get 4-6 Wheat rotis
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup chopped colour peppers
  3. Take 1/4 cup chopped cauliflower
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup chopped mushrooms
  5. Get 1/4 cup chopped carrots
  6. Take 1/4 cup onion dices/slices
  7. Take 1 tsp chopped garlic
  8. Prepare 1 tsp mix herbs
  9. Make ready 1 tsp red chilli flakes
  10. Get to taste Salt
  11. Take as required tomato ketchup
  12. Make ready as required Chopped fresh coriander leaves
  13. Take as required Mayonnaise
Instructions to make Veggie wrap:
  1. In a kadhai take butter or oil add chopped garlic and saute then add veggies and saute later add mushrooms to avoid making it soft and chewy.
  2. Add mixed herbs, chilli flakes, salt and saute.dont make them soft and don't overcook
  3. Take a roti apply mayo at centre. Fill the veggie mix on mayo add ketchup, coriander leaves and fold as wrap
  4. Roast on tawa with little butter or oil till the crust becomes crisp
