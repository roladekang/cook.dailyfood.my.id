The ingredients needed to make Veg Manchurian:
  1. Prepare 2 cups maida
  2. Make ready .5 cup corn flour
  3. Prepare 3 cup cabbage
  4. Take as needed Strips chopped capsicum onion cabbage carrot
  5. Get as required Oil to fry
  6. Get As needed Oil to saute
  7. Get as per taste Salt
  8. Prepare 1/2 tbsp Black pepper
  9. Prepare 2 tsp Soy sauce
  10. Prepare as needed Green onion
  11. Make ready as per need Ginger garlic chilli crushed
Instructions to make Veg Manchurian:
  1. First grate the cabbage and grate it. Add salt to it. Keep for 5 mins as tje water comes out squeeze all the water from it
  2. Now add maida corn flour and salt add ginger garlic chilli to it.
  3. Make balls and fry it till brown in colour
  4. Take a wok add oil add ginger garlic chilli to it. Add onion add saute on full.gas add other veggies to it add salt And saute
  5. Add black pepper msg soY sauce add manchurian and serve it hot
