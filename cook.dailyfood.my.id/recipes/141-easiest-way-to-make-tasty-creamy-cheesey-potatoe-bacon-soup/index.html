The ingredients needed to make Creamy cheesey potatoe bacon soup:
  1. Make ready 2 containers chicken soup broth
  2. Prepare 4 medium size potatoes
  3. Get 2 celery sticks
  4. Make ready 1 small onion
  5. Get 1 large carrot
  6. Take 1 small green pepper
  7. Take 4 white mushrooms
  8. Get 1 cup kernel corn
  9. Make ready 2 cloves garlic
  10. Take to taste salt and pepper
  11. Take 1 tbs herb due provence
  12. Prepare 1 package bacon
  13. Get 1/2 cup butter
  14. Get approx. 1/2 cup flour
  15. Make ready 3 cups cream
  16. Get 1 package Velveeta cheese or 2 cups shredded cheddar
  17. Get fresh green onion parsley and/or cilantro
Instructions to make Creamy cheesey potatoe bacon soup:
  1. Chop potato, celery, onion, carrots, pepper, mushrooms, and garlic and combine with soup broth corn and seasonings in large pot. Bring to boil and simmer for 30-40 minutes
  2. Cook bacon until crispy
  3. In a separate pot melt butter on medium heat and then slowly add flour until the liquid is absorbed and you have a thick paste. Slowly add cream and bring to a boil while whisking constantly. Once at a boil add cheese and stir until melted.
  4. Mix cream cheese sauce into soup and add crumbled bacon.
  5. Top with fresh chopped green onion, parsley, and/or cilantro.
  6. Enjoy! Soup gets thicker once cooled and reheated the next day
