The ingredients needed to make Instant sooji meduvada:
  1. Make ready 500 grams sooji
  2. Get 250 grams curd
  3. Make ready 2 tablespoons chilli ginger garlic paste
  4. Prepare 2 onion (finely.chopped)
  5. Prepare 2 tablespoon green coriander leaves (finely chopped)
  6. Make ready as per taste Salt
  7. Get 1/2 tablespoon baking soda
  8. Prepare as required Oil for frying
Instructions to make Instant sooji meduvada:
  1. Take a bowl pour sooji add salt, curd, ginger chilli garlic paste, coriander leaves onion and baking soda mix all the ingredients very well to make a soft dough
  2. Now take a small portion from the dough and roll it and make whole in the middle.
  3. Now in a Kadai heat up oil then deep fry this meduvada on low flame until it's colour changes to golden brown.
  4. Instant sooji meduvada is ready to serve.Serve meduvada with sambhar and coconut
