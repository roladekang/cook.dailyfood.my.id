How to Prepare Tasteful Fried green tomatoes

by Harold Obrien

The ingredients needed to make Fried green tomatoes:
  1. Make ready 1 cup all-purpose flour
  2. Get 2 cup cornmeal
  3. Take 3 eggs
  4. Take 1 salt and pepper to taste
  5. Get 1 hot frying oil
Steps to make Fried green tomatoes:
  1. Cut and slice green tomatoes
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl
  3. Scramble eggs in another bowl
  4. Dip tomatoes in egg mixture and bread in dry ingredients
  5. Fry til golden on both sides and tomatoes float on top of the oil
