Steps to make Dates biscuit delight:
  1. Dates and figs soaked in a water.
  2. After soaking mashed.
  3. Then take a pan add ghee.
  4. Ghee hot add all dry fruits,milk,and dates puree cook for 10 minutes after 10 minutes add milk powder cook for 2 minutes then gas off.
  5. Take a Marie biscuits one biscuit cover with dates batter then cover with second biscuit.
  6. Dark chocolate melt in microwave then biscuit dip in chocolate and set in fridge.
  7. After set then white chocolate melt and dip in white chocolate.
  8. Then set in fridge and enjoy Marie biscuit delight.
