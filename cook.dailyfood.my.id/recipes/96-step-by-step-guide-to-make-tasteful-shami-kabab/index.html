The ingredients needed to make Shami Kabab:
  1. Take 1 kg Boneless beef
  2. Take half kg Chana daal
  3. Make ready 1 big size Aloo
  4. Take 12 pieces Garlic
  5. Prepare 2 inch Ginger
  6. Make ready 2 medium size Onion
  7. Prepare 10-15 pieces Whole chilli
  8. Prepare Salt as required
  9. Get 1 tsp Garam masala powder
  10. Make ready 2 Egg
Steps to make Shami Kabab:
  1. In pot, add beef, chana daal, onion, garlic, ginger, salt and whole chilli in water.and allow to cook till tender.
  2. Mix in blender, into mixture. Now add garam masala powder. Make small pattise.
  3. Whisk egg in bowl. Dip kabab in egg and place on hot pan. Cook from sides. Serve with salad and chutney.
