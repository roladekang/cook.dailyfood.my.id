The ingredients needed to make Sausage, Hotdogs, and Fruit:
  1. Get Sausage and hotdogs
  2. Take 20 ounces mild Italian sausage
  3. Get 14 ounces good quality hotdogs
  4. Get Fruit
  5. Prepare 3 medium Fuji apples
  6. Make ready 1 cup dried currants
  7. Make ready 1-1/2 cup diced peaches
  8. Get 1 pound red grapes
  9. Get 1/2 cup cherry preserves
  10. Make ready Other ingredients
  11. Take 1 large shallots
  12. Make ready 2 cups shredded carrots
  13. Take 3 cloves garlic sliced thin
  14. Prepare To taste salt
  15. Get To taste ground white pepper
  16. Make ready 1/4 cup sugar if to tart optional
Steps to make Sausage, Hotdogs, and Fruit:
  1. Preheat oven to 400° Fahrenheit. Gather the fruits. Slice and core the apples
  2. Brown the hotdogs and sausage in an oven safe skillet.
  3. Remove the sausage and hotdogs. Add the carrots and sauté for 7 minutes. Gather the fruits, garlic, and shallots.
  4. Add the fruit mixture to the carrots and sauté 12 minutes. Slice the sausage and hotdogs into bite size pieces. Put into oven for 25-30 minutes. Add the sugar here and stir at the last 10 minutes in the oven.
  5. Give it a good stir let rest 12 minutes and serve. I hope you enjoy!!!
