The ingredients needed to make Garlic chilli paratha:
  1. Take 3 cups wheat flour
  2. Get 1 tsp salt
  3. Take 1 tsp mixed herbs
  4. Prepare 1 tsp garlic paste
  5. Take 2 tbsp spring onion chopped
  6. Take 2 tbsp coriander leaves chopped
  7. Get 4 tbsp oil
  8. Take 15 garlic cloves
  9. Prepare 6 green chillies
  10. Get 1/2 tsp asafoetida
  11. Prepare 1 tbsp amul butter
  12. Get as needed Water
Instructions to make Garlic chilli paratha:
  1. Take wheat flour in bowl add salt, coariander leaves, spring onion,mixed herbs,garlic paste, 1 tbspoon oil.Knead semi soft dough. Rest for 20 minutes
  2. Chop garlic cloves and green chillies.add salt, asefoetida and melted butter in it.mix well and keep aside.
  3. With the help of wooden masher crush garlic mixture.take out dough again Knead it.and divide it in equal sized balls.
  4. Roll out paratha spread chilli garlic mixture on it.fold it like flaps.and make round shape.
  5. Again roll out paratha.heat Pan put paratha from both sides till golden brown by adding some oil. serve hot with onion raita, boondi raita, chutney anything goes well with it.my family prefers thick dal with this paratha so served with dal and onion raita.do try this recipe.
