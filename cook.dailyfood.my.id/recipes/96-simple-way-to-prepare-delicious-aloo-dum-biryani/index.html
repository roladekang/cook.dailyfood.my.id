The ingredients needed to make Aloo dum biryani:
  1. Make ready 2 glasses rice
  2. Prepare 4 glasses water
  3. Get 2-3 cloves
  4. Get 2 elachi
  5. Make ready 2-3 biryani leaves
  6. Take 1 table spoon ginger garlic paste
  7. Prepare 1 table spoon biryani masala
  8. Get to taste Salt
  9. Prepare Pinch turmeric
  10. Take 1 onion
  11. Prepare 1 carrot
  12. Make ready 2 potatoes
  13. Get 2table spoons oil
  14. Make ready 1/2 table spoon jeera seeds
  15. Take Handful curry leaves
  16. Prepare Handful coriander leaves

Steps to make Aloo dum biryani:
  1. Soak rice for 30 mins. Take pressure cooker. Keep it on the stove, once it heats pour oil, add biryani leaves, jeera, cloves, elachi, fry it in low flame
  2. Add onions, curry leaves, fry it in low flame. Add salt, ginger garlic paste, turmeric, roast it in a low flame. Add potatoes, carrot and close the lid, open it add biryani masala, mixed well.
  3. Add rice, mix well, add water, mix well. Close the pressure cooker. Cook for 3 whistles.
  4. Once it cooked take it into a plate and serve hot.
