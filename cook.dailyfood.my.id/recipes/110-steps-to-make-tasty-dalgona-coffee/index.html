The ingredients needed to make Dalgona Coffee:
  1. Prepare 3 cups cold milk
  2. Take 4 tsp instant coffee
  3. Take 4 tsp sugar
  4. Get Pinch Cocoa powder
  5. Prepare 1 tsp water for whisking
  6. Prepare as required Ice
Steps to make Dalgona Coffee:
  1. Mix coffee, water and suvarn whisk it nicely so that the coffee gets a good fluffiness.you can use whisker. With hand, it will take 10 mins
  2. Mix milk and ice.
  3. Pour milk into a glass. On to that top add the whisked coffee. Sprinkle cocoa powder.
  4. Enjoy delicious dalgona coffee.
