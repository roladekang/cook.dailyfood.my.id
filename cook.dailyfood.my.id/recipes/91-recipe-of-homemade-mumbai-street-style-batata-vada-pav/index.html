The ingredients needed to make Mumbai street style batata vada pav!:
  1. Make ready 4 boiled mashed potatoes
  2. Get 1-2 green chillies, finely chopped
  3. Make ready 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste
  4. Get 4-5 curry leaves
  5. Make ready 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder
  6. Get 1 teaspoon cumin
  7. Get 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  8. Get 1-2 tablespoons chopped coriander leaves (Cilantro leaves)
  9. Get as per taste Salt
  10. Prepare For batter-
  11. Prepare 1-2 cups gram flour (besan)
  12. Take 1 pinch asafoetida (hing)
  13. Make ready 1/2 cup water or add as required
  14. Get As per taste Salt
  15. Get For green chutney-
  16. Get 1 cup chopped coriander leaves
  17. Get 3-4 garlic cloves chopped
  18. Take 2-3 green chillies
  19. Get as per taste Salt
  20. Get Other ingredients-
  21. Get as required Oil for deep frying
  22. Make ready 4 pav
Steps to make Mumbai street style batata vada pav!:
  1. Boil or steam potatoes in cooker Peel and then mash them with a fork in a bowl.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add tumermic, cumin seed mustard seeds and crackle them.then add ginger garlic paste Then add curry leaves and Stir and fry for about 5 seconds
  3. Pour this tempering in the mashed potatoes. - - Add chopped coriander leaves and salt. - - Mix everything well and then make small to medium balls from the mashed potato mixture. Flatten these balls a bit. Cover and set aside
  4. Making of batter
  5. In another bowl, make a smooth and thick yet flowing batter from the besan, turmeric powder, salt and water.
  6. Medium hot oil. - - Deep fry these batata vadas till golden evenly. - - Drain them on kitchen paper towels.
  7. Making Green Chutney - - Grind all the green chutney ingredients with little water till smooth. Avoid making a watery chutney.
  8. Our yummy Vada pav are ready to serve now.
  9. Serve vada pav immediately You can also serve some fried green chillies with it
