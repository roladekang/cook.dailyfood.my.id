The ingredients needed to make Chana Dal fry:
  1. Make ready For tarka
  2. Prepare 1 cup onion chopped
  3. Make ready 1 tbs cumin
  4. Take 3 tbsp oil
  5. Make ready 1 and 1/2 cup tomato finally chopped
  6. Get Salt as ur taste
  7. Take 2 pitch hing
  8. Prepare 1/2 tbs haldi power
  9. Prepare 2 tbsp kasuri methi
  10. Make ready 1 tbs Chili powder
  11. Get 1 tbs garam masala
  12. Take 4-5 kari patta finally chopped
  13. Get For daal
  14. Get 1 cup chana daal
  15. Prepare 1 tbs haldi
  16. Take Salt as tadte
Steps to make Chana Dal fry:
  1. Firstly, take chana dal and wash well. Take it in a pressure cooker. Add in salt and turmeric powder. Add water and cook for 5 to 6 whistle. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a karahi, add hing, cumin, lal mirch, and tejpatta and let it sizzle.now add ginger-garlic paste, chopped onions andcook till golden.then add in tomatoes, chillies powder, kari patta, and mix well.Cook till tomatoes get mushy.
  3. Add salt and spice powders and mix well.Add in cooked dal, and mix well.Simmer for 8 mins.
  4. Add in kasoori methi and cook 1 minutes
  5. Serve hot with pulka and enjoy
