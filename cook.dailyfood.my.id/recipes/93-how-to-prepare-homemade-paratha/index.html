How to Prepare Homemade Paratha

The ingredients needed to make Paratha:
  1. Get 200 gm wheat flour
  2. Take 500 gm cauliflower
  3. Prepare as needed Oil
  4. Take to taste Salt
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp ginger
  7. Take 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  8. Make ready 2 green chilies
  9. Prepare 3 spoon green coriander leaves
  10. Make ready 1 pinch hing/asafoetdia
  11. Take 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  12. Get 1/2 tsp dry mango powder
Steps to make Paratha:
  1. Clean and chop the cauliflower, chilli and ginger. - cut the coriander leaves
  2. Heat a pan and add 2 spoons of oil. - Add cumin seeds, hing, ginger, and chilli mix it.
  3. Add all spice and cauliflower mix it 5 minutes and add green coriander leaves.
  4. Take a wheat flour and add some water and make a soft dough, heat a pan. - take a little dough and make small circle. - fill the stuffings and make a round paratha
  5. Heat the pan along with the paratha then apply oil on both sides
  6. Paratha it's ready to serve.
