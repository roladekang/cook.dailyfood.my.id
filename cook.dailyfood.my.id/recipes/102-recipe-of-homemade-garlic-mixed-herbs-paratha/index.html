The ingredients needed to make Garlic Mixed Herbs Paratha:
  1. Make ready 2 tsp salted butter
  2. Prepare 10-12 Small garlic cloves
  3. Take 1 tsp mixed herbs
  4. Take 1/2 tsp red chilli flakes
  5. Get 1 bowl flour dough
  6. Take As needed oil for frying
Steps to make Garlic Mixed Herbs Paratha:
  1. First peel off garlic & crushed roughly it.
  2. Now take butter in a bowl & add 1 tsp mixed herbs & 1/2 tsp Chilli flakes in it.Finally add crushed garlic to it
  3. Mix them properly.
  4. Now take a ball of dough & roll it.Then apply pre prepared garlic mixture on it.
  5. Now cook it from both sides by applying cooking oil on medium flame.
  6. It is ready.Serve it with butter & curd.
