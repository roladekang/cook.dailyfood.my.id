The ingredients needed to make #dussehra

Khaman Dhokla Post 2:

  1. Take 1 cup gram flour
  2. Take 2 cup semolina
  3. Prepare 1 tsp Sugar
  4. Prepare Salt as requited
  5. Take End fruit salt 1 pkt
  6. Take 1 tsp vegetable oil
  7. Get 1 pinch citric acid dissolved in water OR
  8. Take 1/2 cup curd
  9. Prepare For Tadka
  10. Make ready 1 tsp Oil
  11. Take 1 tsp Mustard seeds
  12. Take 10/12 leaves Curry patta
  13. Take 1 tsp Sesame seeds
  14. Prepare 2 tsp lemon juice
  15. Take 2-3 Green chilli
  16. Take For garnish
  17. Make ready Fresh grated coconut
  18. Prepare Chopped coriander
Instructions to make #dussehra

Khaman Dhokla Post 2:

  1. Add gram flour, semolina, salt, sugar and curd and oil.
  2. Mix till all lumps are gone. Add water if required for the consistency of batter. Add Eno just before u pour the mixture in vessel.
  3. Add this batter in a oil greased vessel and cook in cooker without whistle for 5-10 min. Check with a tooth pick and if it comes out clean, the dhokla is ready.
  4. Prepare more until all batter is finished.
  5. For Tadka Add mustard seed, sesame seed, green chillies, Curry patta in hot oil and pour this into the vessel. Add lemon juice also mixed in water. U can add sugar too in this mixture if u like sweet taste.
  6. Garnish with Fresh grated coconut and coriander leaves.
  7. Cut in desired shape and serve hot..
