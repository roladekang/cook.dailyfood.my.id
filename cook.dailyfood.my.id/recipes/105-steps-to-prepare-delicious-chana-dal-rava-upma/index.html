The ingredients needed to make Chana dal Rava Upma:
  1. Get 1 cup Rava-
  2. Get 1/2 cup Chana dal- soaked in water
  3. Get 1 Onion finely chopped
  4. Prepare 1 tsp Mustard seeds
  5. Prepare as per taste Salt-
  6. Take -5-6 Curry leaves
  7. Make ready 3-4 spoons Oil -
  8. Take 1 tsp Coriander powder
  9. Prepare 1 tsp Garam masala
  10. Prepare 1tsp Red chilli powder
Instructions to make Chana dal Rava Upma:
  1. Wash and soak Chana dal in water for 20 mints then boil in pressure cooker till tender. Strain and Keep aside.
  2. Take a pan add oil, mustard seeds and let it crackle. Add curry leaves and semolina and cook till the colour changes and aroma or roasting rava comes.
  3. Add above-mentioned spices and mix well and add Chana dal and mix well. Add water as required and keep stirring till the batter gathers and the pan leaves the sides.
  4. Transfer to a bowl and garnish with curry leaves. Serve hot with coconut chutney.
