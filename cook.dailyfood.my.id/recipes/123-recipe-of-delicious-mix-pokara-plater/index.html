Steps to make Mix pokara plater:
  1. First of all make onion and patato pokora,cut the vegetables amd make a batter of baison, keep the batter of baison in fridge for 10 minutes
  2. Now marinated chicken and keep in fridge for about 30 minutes
  3. Now make a batter of baison and take out the chicken, dip into batter and fry it until than it's colours will golden brown
  4. Now take the batter of pakora mix well dip the green chilli in batter then fry it
  5. Now take the batter of baison mix onion and patato ingredients mix well
  6. Heat the oil and fry pokora until golden brown in colour, sprinkle with chaat masala
  7. Now 3 types of pokora are ready to serve with ketchup, chilli sauce and green mint chutney.
