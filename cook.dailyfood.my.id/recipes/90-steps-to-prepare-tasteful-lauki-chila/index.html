The ingredients needed to make Lauki Chila:
  1. Get 3/4 cup Lauki grated
  2. Prepare 3/4 cup Besan
  3. Take 3/4 cup Sooji
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup yogurt
  5. Take 1 tsp red chilli powder
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  7. Take Pinch asafoetida
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp oil
  9. Prepare as per taste Salt
  10. Get 1/2 cup water to mix the batter
Instructions to make Lauki Chila:
  1. Grate the Lauki
  2. Add to the bowl and add besan, sooji, yoghurt, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, asafoetida, salt and mix well
  3. Add 1/2 cup water and mix well to make a good batter. Add 1/2 tsp oil.
  4. In a griddle grease with oil and spread all across. Take a ladle of the batter and add to the oil, spread evenly. Fry for 1-2 mins each side and flip. Set aside. Complete the batch
  5. Plate with Tomato & Coriander-Mint Chutney. Enjoy!
