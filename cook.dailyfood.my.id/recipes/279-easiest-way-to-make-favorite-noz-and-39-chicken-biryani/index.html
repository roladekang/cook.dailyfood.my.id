Easiest Way to Make Favorite Noz&amp;#39; Chicken Biryani

The ingredients needed to make Noz' Chicken Biryani:
  1. Make ready Rice
  2. Take soaked Lentils
  3. Get salt
  4. Take Spice for Rice
  5. Take chicken
  6. Get sour cream
  7. Take olive oil
  8. Take Cumin powder
  9. Make ready Steak Spice
  10. Take Coriander powder
  11. Make ready Chicken spice
  12. Take cinnamon stick
  13. Take cumin seeds
  14. Take curry leaves
  15. Take curry powder
  16. Make ready paprika
  17. Take mustard seeds
  18. Prepare garlic and ginger paste
  19. Take potatoe
  20. Prepare Onions chopped
  21. Get Green pepper
Instructions to make Noz' Chicken Biryani:
  1. Soak Lentils at least for 4 hours
  2. Wash chicken pieces and then add these spices and marinade with sour cream. Set aside for 4 hours minimum
  3. To cook: Wash rice and drain your lentils and add to rice and boil with seasoned water. When boiling, add spice for rice and let the rice cook.
  4. Once the rice and lentils are cooked drain water and set aside. NB, do not over cook rice
  5. Heat olive oil, add mustard seeds, garlic and ginger, cumin seeds, curry leaves and a little bit of coriander powder. Make sure it does not burn so reduce heat once the olive oil is hot.
  6. Once the garlic is tender, not burned, add chicken mixture in
  7. Let it cook until onoins are soft and changed colour
  8. Add curry powder and papprika, let it cook 5 min, then cut potatoe into half and chuck it in.
  9. Simmer until chicken and potatoe is cooked and to maintain the juice keep it on the low medium heat.
  10. Once cooked mix rice in and simmer on a low heat for 10 min then move it from the stove and set aside for at least 5 minutes before serving
  11. ENJOY!
