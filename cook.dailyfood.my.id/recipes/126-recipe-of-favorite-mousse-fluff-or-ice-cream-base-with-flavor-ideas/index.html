The ingredients needed to make Mousse Fluff or Ice cream base with flavor ideas:
  1. Take 250 gm mascarpone
  2. Take 250 cc heavy whipping cream. (May double to 500 cc)
  3. Make ready 3-4 tbsp sweetener (monkfruit, swerve, etc)
  4. Prepare drops Vanilla
  5. Make ready Optional: chocolate, instant coffee, vanilla, PB, berry
Steps to make Mousse Fluff or Ice cream base with flavor ideas:
  1. Decide flavor: - 1. Chocolate (prepare ganache 80 gms chocolate bits, 85% and cream to cover. Put in small bowl and micro by short pulses. - 2. Vanilla, drops - 3. Instant coffe, 1 or 2 tsp - 4. PB, 1/2 to 1 cup. - 5. Berry of choice 1/2 to 1 cup, mashed
  2. Place all ingredients in bowl and blend until firm.
  3. Place in small mason jars. - 250 cc cream gives btw 6 - 8 small mason jars. - 500 cc cream gives 10 - 12 jars
  4. Place in fridge. Lasts a week or more. - If freezing, take out 1 hour before serving.
