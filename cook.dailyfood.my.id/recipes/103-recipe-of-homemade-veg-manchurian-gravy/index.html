The ingredients needed to make Veg manchurian gravy:
  1. Make ready for veg manchurian balls
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup grated carrot
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup finely chopped cabbage
  4. Get 1/4 cup finely chopped capsicum
  5. Get 1/4 cup spring onion
  6. Get 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  7. Get 2 tablespoon maida
  8. Prepare 2 tablespoon cornflour
  9. Get to taste Salt
  10. Make ready 500 ml oil to deep fry
  11. Prepare for gravy
  12. Get 1 tablespoon oil
  13. Prepare 1 cup freshly chopped onions
  14. Get 1/2 tablespoon ginger garlic paste
  15. Get 2 finely chopped green chillies
  16. Prepare 1/2 tablespoon soya sauce
  17. Prepare 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  18. Get 2 tablespoon chilli sauce
  19. Prepare 1 tablespoon vinegar
  20. Make ready 1 cup water
  21. Get 1/2 spoon black pepper powder
  22. Make ready to taste Salt
  23. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  24. Make ready 1/4 cup spring onion for garnishing

Steps to make Veg manchurian gravy:
  1. Making veg manchurian balls…..
  2. Take all the vegetables and add cornflour and maida and mix them well.
  3. Now add salt to it and mix properly so that the vegetables leave water
  4. Now Knead this mixture like dough and divide it into small portions.
  5. Now make small balls by rolling them into your palm and deep fry them into warm oil.
  6. Making Gravy……heat 1 tablespoon oil in pan and add ginger garlic paste,green chilli and onion into it and stir fry till it becomes little brown
  7. Now add the sauces and mix well.
  8. Add the water and bring it to boil
  9. Mix 1 tablespoon of cornflour into 2 tablspoons of water and add it into the pan.
  10. Continue to stir so that no lumps are formed.wait till the gravy thickens
  11. Now add salt and black pepper into it and mix well.
  12. Now add sugar and vinegar to it and mix.
  13. Now add manchurian balls to the gravy.Garnish it with spring onions.your veg gravy manchurian is ready to eat.
