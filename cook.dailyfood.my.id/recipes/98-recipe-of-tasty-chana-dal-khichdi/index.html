Recipe of Tasty Chana dal khichdi

The ingredients needed to make Chana dal khichdi:
  1. Take 1 1/2 bowl basmati rice
  2. Get 1/2 bowl soaked chana dal
  3. Prepare 1 bay leaf
  4. Make ready 1 cumin seeds
  5. Make ready 4-5 cloves
  6. Take 1 black cardamom
  7. Take 1 long chopped onion
  8. Make ready 1 chopped onion into small
  9. Get 2 tomatoes
  10. Make ready 2 green chillies
  11. Prepare 1 tsp Red chilli powder
  12. Take to taste Salt
  13. Take 1 tsp Garam masala
  14. Take 2 tsp Coriander leaves to garnish
  15. Get 2 tablespoon ghee
Steps to make Chana dal khichdi:
  1. Wash and soak chanadal for 2-3 hours.
  2. Heat ghee in a cooker. Fry long chopped onion until dark brown. Separate brown onion in a bowl.
  3. Now add black cardomom, cloves, cumin seed, bay leaf into leftover oil saute until fragrant.
  4. Add chopped onion, fry until light pink, add chopped tomatoes, green chilli add salt, red chilli powder, garam masala and saute for 1 minute.
  5. Separate 1/2 the mixture into a bowl. Now it's time to add layers
  6. Add a layer of chana dal use 1/2 only to make another layer, then add a layer of washed rice, one layer of dark brown onion, another layer of saute mixture, chana dal and rice.
  7. Add 3 cup water for rice and extra 1/2 cup water for chana dal. Cover the lid until 2 whistle. Let the khichdi cook on high flame.
  8. After 2 whistle open the the lid and serve khichdi with raita, achaar, salad. - Sneh Saini
