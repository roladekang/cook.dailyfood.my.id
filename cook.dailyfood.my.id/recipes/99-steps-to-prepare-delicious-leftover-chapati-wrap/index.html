The ingredients needed to make Leftover chapati wrap:
  1. Take 6 chapatis
  2. Take 4 Boiled Potatoes
  3. Take 1 Onion
  4. Prepare 2 tsp Garlic paste
  5. Get 1 tsp Ginger paste
  6. Make ready 1 tsp turmeric powder
  7. Get 2 tsp Chaat masala

Steps to make Leftover chapati wrap:
  1. Take boiled potatoes peel them. Add salt and chaat masala and mix it nicely.
  2. Take a pan heat 1tsp of oil add ginger and garlic paste and saute. Add chopped onion and fry. Add boiled potatoes mixed with turmeric and chaat masala.
  3. Let it cool down. Add 2 tbsp of potatoes mixture in the chapati. Fold it nicely and again fry in a pan.
  4. Chapati wrap is ready..
