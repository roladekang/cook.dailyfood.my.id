The ingredients needed to make Eggless banana cake:
  1. Get 2 cupa All-purpose flour
  2. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  3. Take 1/2 tsp baking soda
  4. Take 3 bananas
  5. Make ready 100 gm sugar
  6. Get 1 tsp vanilla essence
  7. Make ready 3 tbsp cocoa
  8. Get 1/2 cup milk
  9. Get 1/2 cup oil or melted butter

Steps to make Eggless banana cake:
  1. Put 3 bananas n sugar in a food processor or a blender n blend until well combined
  2. Pour in a bowl n add oil n stir
  3. Add the vanilla essence
  4. Sieve the dry ingredients I.e All-purpose flour,cocoa,baking powder n baking soda n combine them well.
  5. Add to the wet ingredients n fold until well combined
  6. Add milk to the milk n continue folding until it is well combined.Grease your baking tray and pour your batter in.
  7. Preheat your oven for 10mins.Put your batter thereafter and bake at 170-160⁰c for 40-45 mins.
