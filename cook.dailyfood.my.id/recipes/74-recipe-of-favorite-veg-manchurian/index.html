Recipe of Favorite Veg Manchurian

The ingredients needed to make Veg Manchurian:
  1. Take Manchurian balls-
  2. Get cauliflower grated
  3. Prepare carrots grated
  4. Take capsicum chopped
  5. Make ready cornflour
  6. Get maida
  7. Take salt
  8. Get spoon red chilli powder
  9. Take baking soda
  10. Prepare Oil for frying
  11. Make ready Gravy-
  12. Make ready capsicum julienne
  13. Take carrot julienne
  14. Prepare ginger chopped
  15. Get spring onion chopped
  16. Get garlic cloves chopped
  17. Get green chillies, chopped
  18. Prepare Water
  19. Take Salt
  20. Get black pepper powder
  21. Take soya sauce
  22. Take ketchup
  23. Take green chilli sause
  24. Take vinegar
  25. Get cornflour
  26. Make ready water
  27. Take Green onion to garnish
Instructions to make Veg Manchurian:
  1. Grate and chop the veggies then mix in a mixing bowl. Add salt and leave it covered for 10 minutes. After 10 minutes strain all the water from veggies using a strainer or a cloth. In a bowl add maida, cornflour, chilli powder and baking soda. Mix well and knead a smooth dough. Divide this dough into equal parts.
  2. Deep fry these dough balls in oil on medium flame till they acquire a golden hue. Now get them out and fry once more on high flame for a few seconds till they get a crust around them.
  3. For gravy take another pan and add 2 spoons oil in it. once heated add ginger, garlic and green chillies. Once sauted add green onions. Cook for a minute on high flame then add carrot Julian saute them for a minute then add capsicum julienne.
  4. Cook veggies for a minute then add salt, black pepper powder, soya sauce, ketchup, chilli sauce and mix really well. Add water as required to make gravy. Add fried Manchurian balls and cook till they absorb all the flavor in them. Now in a bowl add 1 spoon cornflour and add water to it. Mix well to form a slurry. Add this slurry to Manchurian to help thicken the gravy.
  5. Garnish the manchurian with green onions and serve them with rice, chapati or noodles or can enjoy them as a snack.
