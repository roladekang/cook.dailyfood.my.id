Step-by-Step Guide to Make Tasteful Dalgona Coffee

by Kate Weaver

The ingredients needed to make Dalgona Coffee:
  1. Make ready 2 tsp coffee powder
  2. Prepare 2 tsp sugar(you can add more sugar if you like more sweet taste)
  3. Take 2-3 tsp water
  4. Take 1 1/2 cup milk (hot Or cold it's your choice)
Steps to make Dalgona Coffee:
  1. First mix coffee powder, sugar and water. Blend them nicely. You can use blender or spoon.
  2. Keep blending, it will take almost 10-12 minutes.
  3. To know coffee is ready, you need to lift the blender or spoon and you should get this texture like mountain. It means your coffee batter is ready.
  4. Now depends on your choice you can add hot Or cold milk in cup and on top add coffee mixture. Enjoy your coffee!!!
