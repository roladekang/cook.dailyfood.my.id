Steps to make Mix Vegetable Handvo:
  1. Combine all the ingredients with 3-tbsp water and mix well. Keep aside.
  2. Heat 2-tbsp oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, hing, sesame seeds, curry leaves, red chilli powder, and saute on a medium flame for 15-seconds.
  3. Pour a handva better and spread it evenly, cover with a lid and cook on a medium flame for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Turn and again cover with a lid and cook on medium flame for 3-4minites. Golden brown colour from the other side as well.
  5. Remove on a board and cut into 4-equal pieces.
  6. Serve the mix vegetable handvo immediately with tea and oil.
  7. Enjoy!
