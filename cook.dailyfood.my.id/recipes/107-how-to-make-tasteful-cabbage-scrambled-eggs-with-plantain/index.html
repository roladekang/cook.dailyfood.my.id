The ingredients needed to make Cabbage Scrambled eggs with plantain:
  1. Take 3 eggs
  2. Take Cabbage
  3. Make ready Maggie
  4. Prepare Curry
  5. Take Salt
  6. Make ready 3 Plantain
  7. Take G/nut oil
Instructions to make Cabbage Scrambled eggs with plantain:
  1. Break the eggs add in Maggie,curry, salt and sliced cabbage
  2. Add 1 teaspoon spoon of oil and fry the eggs
  3. Peel off the plantain slice and deep fry in oil
