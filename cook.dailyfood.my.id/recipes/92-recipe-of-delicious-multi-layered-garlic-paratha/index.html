Recipe of Delicious Multi layered Garlic Paratha

by John McCarthy

The ingredients needed to make Multi layered Garlic Paratha:
  1. Take 1 cup Wheat flour
  2. Take 10-12 garlic pods chopped finely
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp chopped green coriander
  4. Take 1/2 tbsp chilli flakes
  5. Take 2 tbsp melted butter
  6. Get to taste Salt
  7. Prepare as needed Water
Instructions to make Multi layered Garlic Paratha:
  1. First, take a bowl. Add 1 cup of wheat flour and salt as per taste and mix well. Now knead a soft dough for paratha. Apply some oil or ghee and rest for 15-20 mins.
  2. In a bowl, add finely chopped garlic and coriander.
  3. Add chilli flakes, salt (if required) and butter. Mix all the ingredients well.
  4. Now on a rolling pin, take a dough and roll into a thin round chapati. Apply the prepared garlic mixture on it.
  5. Now using knife or pizza cutter cut the vertical strips by leaving around 1 inch from top. Once done start rolling the chapati from one side. (as shown in the pic)
  6. Now roll and tuck in the end in the center. Slightly press the dough.
  7. Again roll the dough with a soft hand. Heat the tawa. Place the rolled paratha on tawa. Cook from both the side by apply ghee or butter.
  8. Once cooked properly from both the sides you may see the layers. Transfer the paratha on a serving plate. Serve hot with chutney, ketchup or tea.
