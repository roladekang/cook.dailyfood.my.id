The ingredients needed to make Waffle with Ice cream:
  1. Make ready 1 cup All-purpose Flour
  2. Take 4 Tbsp sugar
  3. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt/1/4 tsp (as ur taste)
  4. Prepare 1 egg yolk
  5. Prepare 1 egg white
  6. Take 1/2 cup milk
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup water
  8. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  9. Prepare 1 tsp oil
  10. Take 1 tbsp butter
  11. Take Ice cream/nutella/Fruits
