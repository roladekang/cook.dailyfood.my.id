The ingredients needed to make Stuffed Dum Aloo:
  1. Get 8 small potatoes
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup grated paneer
  3. Prepare 8-10 cashews
  4. Prepare 8-10 raisins
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp coriander powder
  6. Make ready 1/4 tsp kitchen king masala
  7. Take 1/4 tsp chaat masala
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp black pepper powder
  9. Prepare For gravy
  10. Take 4 tomatoes
  11. Make ready 10-12 cashews
  12. Make ready 1/4 tsp kashmiri chilli powder
  13. Prepare 1/4 tsp coriander powder
  14. Get 1/4 tsp red chilli powder
  15. Get 1/4 tsp garam masala
  16. Prepare 1 inch ginger
  17. Prepare 2 green chillies
  18. Prepare 1 pinch hing
  19. Prepare as needed Oil
  20. Make ready to taste Salt
Instructions to make Stuffed Dum Aloo:
  1. First wash and peel the potatoes. Cut upper cap of the potato and hollow it from center. Heat oil in a pan and deep fry the potatoes till it turns golden brown. Keep it aside.
  2. For stuffiing take grated paneer, cashews and raisins in a bowl. Add all spices as mentioned and mix well. Now stuff this filling in the hollow portion of potatoes. Now join the cap of potato with a toothpick so that stuffing don't come outside.
  3. For gravy soak cashews in water for 15 min. Now take chopped tomatoes, soaked cashews, ginger and green chilli in a blender and make puree of it. Heat oil in a pan and add hing and cumin to it. Now add puree and all spices to it and cook for 5 min. Once it's cooked add potatoes to it and cook further 5 min. Stuffed dum aloo is ready. Serve it with any paratha or poori.
