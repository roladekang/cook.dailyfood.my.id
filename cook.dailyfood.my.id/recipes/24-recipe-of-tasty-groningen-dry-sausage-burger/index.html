Recipe of Tasty Groningen Dry Sausage Burger

by Jay Collier

The ingredients needed to make Groningen Dry Sausage Burger:
  1. Get Burger
  2. Take 1 piece (Groningen) dry sausage
  3. Get 250 grams ground beef
  4. Make ready 1 egg yolk
  5. Take bread crumbs
  6. Get salt and pepper
  7. Get Sandwich and topping
  8. Take 2 piece whole wheat buns
  9. Take 1 piece onion
  10. Take 2 tsp (Groningen) mustard
  11. Take (rucola) lettuce
  12. Take 1 piece tomato
Instructions to make Groningen Dry Sausage Burger:
  1. Chop the dry sausage in very small pieces. Mix it with the ground beef and a little bit of egg yolk and bread crumbs, such that it becomes a sturdy mass.
  2. Divide it in two burgers and add salt and pepper to taste. Cook the meat for a few minutes on both side. If you would like to you can melt some (Gouda) cheese on them.
  3. Chop the onions and (rucola) lettuce and cut the tomato in slices. Cut the buns in half, smear a teaspoon of mustard on both buns, add the burger, and top of with the vegetables.
  4. Enjoy!
