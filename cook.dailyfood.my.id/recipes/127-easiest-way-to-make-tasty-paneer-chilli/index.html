The ingredients needed to make Paneer chilli:
  1. Take 250 gm paneer cubed cuted
  2. Prepare 1 cuted capsicum
  3. Prepare 1 cuted onion
  4. Get 2 tsp garlic ginger green chilli fined chopped
  5. Prepare 1 tsp red chilli sauce
  6. Get 1 tsp green chilli sauce
  7. Get 1/2 cup cornflour water add pinc of orange food colour
  8. Get 3-4 tsp schezwan sauce
  9. Take 2 tsp oil
  10. Prepare to taste Salt
  11. Make ready As needed Spring onion
  12. Prepare As needed Water
  13. Take Paneer cot batter:-
  14. Take 1 cup Maida /All purpose flour
  15. Get Pinch salt
  16. Make ready Pinch orange food colour
  17. Take As needed Water
  18. Get As needed Oil for deep paneer

Steps to make Paneer chilli:
  1. 1st make batter. Add all ingredients of cot paneer then add paneer and deep fry.
  2. Take kadai heat oil add garlic ginger green chilli saute till colour change then add onion capsicum saute for few seconds
  3. Then mix green and red chilli sauce add in capsicum add schezwan sauce salt cornflour water saute well.(lumbs free).
  4. Then add water as required thick gravy boil water then add fried paneer. Cook for 5mins.
  5. Garnish with spring onion. Ready to eat PANEER Chilli
