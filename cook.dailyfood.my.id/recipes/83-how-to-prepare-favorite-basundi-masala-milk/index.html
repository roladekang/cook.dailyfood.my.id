How to Prepare Favorite Basundi (Masala milk)

by Allen Harmon

Hello everybody, hope you are having an incredible day today. Today, we’re going to make a distinctive dish, basundi (masala milk). One of my favorites. For mine, I am going to make it a bit tasty. This is gonna smell and look delicious.

To begin with this particular recipe, we must first prepare a few components. You can have basundi (masala milk) using 6 ingredients and 5 steps. Here is how you cook it.

The ingredients needed to make Basundi (Masala milk):
  1. Take full cream milk
  2. Get khoya
  3. Prepare sugar
  4. Prepare Dry fruits powder
  5. Take Pista for garnishing
  6. Get cardamom and nutmeg powder
Instructions to make Basundi (Masala milk):
  1. Take a thick bottom pan and add milk let it boil till it becones thick. Keep stirring in between so that milk will not stick to the bottom and will not come out.
  2. After 45 min or so milk will start becoming thick. Take a bowl and mix khoya with 2-3 tbs milk, mix till it completely dissolved. Add this mixture in the milk.
  3. Add sugar as per taste, add dry fruits powder and cardamom and nutmeg powder mix well the milk will become thick quickly after adding khoya.
  4. Tip: Cool the basundi and keep it in the fridge for 5-6 hours to becomes thick. A layer of cream will form and basundi will become thick and more tasty.
  5. Basundi is ready to serve hot or cold, garnish it with pista.Its delicious with Puri as well.
