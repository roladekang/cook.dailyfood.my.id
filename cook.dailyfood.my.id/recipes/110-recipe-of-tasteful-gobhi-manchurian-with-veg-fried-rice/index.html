The ingredients needed to make Gobhi Manchurian with Veg Fried Rice:
  1. Prepare 2 Cups boiled rice
  2. Get 1/2 cup Springonins
  3. Prepare 3 tbsp diced carrots
  4. Prepare 3 tbsp Green bell pepper grated
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp Beans finely chopped
  6. Take 2 tbsp Soya Sauce
  7. Make ready 2 tsp Garlic chopped
  8. Make ready 1 tsp green chillies
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp Black pepper powder
  10. Prepare 1 tsp MSG optional
  11. Take 3 tbsp oil
  12. Make ready to taste Salt
  13. Make ready Gobhi Manchurian ingredients!
  14. Make ready 2 Cups boiled Cauliflower with little salt and turmeric
  15. Take 1 cup Cornflour
  16. Get 1 cup Plain flour
  17. Get 2 tbsp Soya Sauce
  18. Take 2 tbsp Red Chilli garlic Sauce
  19. Take 1/2 tsp Black Pepper
  20. Get 1 tbsp chopped garlic
  21. Make ready 3 tbsp springonion
  22. Get Oil for deep frying
Steps to make Gobhi Manchurian with Veg Fried Rice:
  1. First take a wok.Add oil to it.
  2. Heat on medium flame.
  3. Add garlic,chopped chillies, veggies and saute well.
  4. Now add the boiled rice to it.
  5. Season it with Salt,pepper,Soya sauce and mix well.
  6. Simmer it on low flame covered for 5 minutes
  7. For Gobhi Manchurian
  8. First add Cornflour,flour to the cauliflower.
  9. Deep fry it till it turns crispy and taje out in a paper towel.
  10. For more crispiness again deep fry it.
  11. Now in another wok add 2tbsp of oil.
  12. Add garlic,green chillies, pepper,sauces and mix well.
  13. Make a slurry with 2 tbsp of cornflour and 1/4cup of water.
  14. Add slurry in wok, saute little.
  15. Then add fried gobhi to it and mix well.
  16. Immediately switch of the flame.
  17. Serve hot with Veg Fried Rice and enjoy!
