The ingredients needed to make Bottle gourd and chana Dal ke cutlets:
  1. Make ready 500 gm bottle gourd
  2. Make ready 1 cup chana dal soaked
  3. Get 1 cup bread crumbs
  4. Take 2 Green chilli chopped
  5. Prepare 1 tsp ginger chopped
  6. Get to taste salt
  7. Take 1 tsp turmeric powder
  8. Prepare 1 tsp garam masala
  9. Get 1 tsp dry coriander powder
  10. Get 1 tsp amchur(dry mango) powder
  11. Take 1 bay leaves
  12. Make ready 1 tsp chopped coriander leaves
  13. Get 1 tsp kasuri methi
  14. Prepare As needed Oil to shallow fry
  15. Prepare 2 tsp lemon juice
  16. Get 1 onion rings for garnish

Instructions to make Bottle gourd and chana Dal ke cutlets:
  1. Prepare all the ingredients. Chop the bottle gourd.
  2. Cook both the dal and bottle gourd with bay leaves till soft… Stir it properly. Mash
  3. Mix all spices, bread crumbs.. Methi with mashed batter.. Add lemon juice. Make like a dough.
  4. Now make shape of cutlets with this dough. Now shallow fry all cutlets on nonstick pan.
  5. Now healthy cutlets are ready to serve with chutney and sauce and onion rings.
