The ingredients needed to make Dal paratha:
  1. Take Wheat flour
  2. Take Water
  3. Get Left over dal
  4. Prepare Salt
  5. Take Chilly powder
  6. Take Cooking oil
Steps to make Dal paratha:
  1. Mix flour, left over dal, salt and Chilly powder as per taste with little oil.
  2. Cover the dough for 10 to 15 minutes at room temperature.
  3. Make parathas of it using oil. Serve hot with chatni or ketchup.
