The ingredients needed to make Dum aloo:
  1. Prepare 2-4 Potato
  2. Get 2 Onion
  3. Take 1 Bay leaves
  4. Make ready -1/2 tsp Red chili powder
  5. Get 2 Red chili
  6. Take as per taste Salt
  7. Take 1 tbsp Sugar
  8. Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp Ginger and garlic paste
  9. Get 1/2 spoon Turmeric powder
  10. Prepare 1/2 tbsp Panch Phoron
  11. Make ready 1/2 tsp or 1tsp Kitchen king
  12. Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp Garam masala
  13. Prepare 2 Green chillies
Instructions to make Dum aloo:
  1. Peel the potatoes, onion, take green chillies, all ingredients, cut the potatoes, onion, green chillies
  2. Cut it, all put the kadhai in a gas add oil, oil heat it add all ingredients fry it add onion fry when onion is fried till golden brown now add potatoes
  3. Fry it till brown add water and cook it will when it done potato curry done serve it with puri
