The ingredients needed to make Quick Chana Dal:
  1. Make ready 1 1/2 cup chana dal soaked for 30 minutes in warm water
  2. Prepare 1 medium potato chopped
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp grated coconut (optional)
  4. Get 1 medium tomatoes chopped
  5. Make ready 1 green chilli chopped
  6. Get 1 tsp ginger grated
  7. Make ready 1 tsp ghee
  8. Prepare 1 tsp oil
  9. Prepare 1 bay leaf cut into half
  10. Prepare 1/3 tsp turmeric powder
  11. Take 1/2 tsp Kashmiri chilli powder
  12. Make ready 1/3 tsp cumin powder
  13. Get 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  14. Make ready to taste Salt
  15. Prepare For fresh garam masala
  16. Make ready 1 inch cinnamon stick
  17. Take 1 green cardamom
  18. Prepare 1/2 black cardamom
  19. Make ready 2 cloves
  20. Take 4 black pepper corns
Steps to make Quick Chana Dal:
  1. Pressure cook soaked chana dal and potato, with turmeric,salt and water.
  2. Grind tomatoes, coconut, grate ginger, green chilli, cumin and coriander powder to a fine paste.
  3. Light fry the ingredients of garam masala and grind to powder.
  4. Heat ghee and oil. Add panch phutan and bay leaves. Let them splutter.
  5. Add red chilli powder. Mix the masala paste and saute on low-medium flame, till oil separate.
  6. Add and mix boiled dal, potato and fresh, garam masala powder prepared. Let it boil for 2 minutes. - - Serve hot with roti/rice. - Thanks
