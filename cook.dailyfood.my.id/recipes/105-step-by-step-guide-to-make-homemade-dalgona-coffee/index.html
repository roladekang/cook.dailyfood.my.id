Step-by-Step Guide to Make Homemade Dalgona Coffee

by Nelle Watson

To begin with this recipe, we must prepare a few ingredients. You can have dalgona coffee using 4 ingredients and 4 steps. Here is how you can achieve it.

The ingredients needed to make Dalgona Coffee:
  1. Take 4-5 bru packets (small)
  2. Take 3-4 tsp sugar
  3. Get 2 cups milk
  4. Prepare 1 tsp water
Instructions to make Dalgona Coffee:
  1. Take bru packets and put in any glass or cup add sugar
  2. And add water beat it nicely till it turns fluffy
  3. Take milk and boil it and pour the hot milk in the glass and put the fluffy coffee on the top of the milk
  4. Mix the coffee with the help of spoon and enjoyy the dalgona coffe
