The ingredients needed to make Chicken With Bacon Mini Burger In Hoisin Sauce:
  1. Get 5 slice bacon
  2. Get 2 cup mince chicken
  3. Make ready 1 cup grated carrot
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  5. Get 1 tbsp light soy sauce
  6. Make ready 1 dash Salt and pepper
  7. Take 1 tbsp sesame oil
  8. Prepare 2 clove garlic
  9. Get 3 small dice pearl onion
  10. Take GARNISH
  11. Make ready 1 cup grated carrot
  12. Make ready 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
  13. Take basting
  14. Prepare 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
Instructions to make Chicken With Bacon Mini Burger In Hoisin Sauce:
  1. MAKE MINI PATTIES
  2. mix mince chicken and fine dice bacon with dice garlic and onion with light soy sauce
  3. add carrot and bread crumbs and seasoning with sesame oil mix well
  4. shape 6 mini ball then lightly flatten it then brush it with hoisin sauce
  5. in pan with little oil pan grill both side of the mini burger
  6. put on parchment paper and bake at 200 C / 400°F for 20 to 25 minutes or longer depending on the size of the burger
  7. serve with fresh grated carrot and top additional hoisin sauce
