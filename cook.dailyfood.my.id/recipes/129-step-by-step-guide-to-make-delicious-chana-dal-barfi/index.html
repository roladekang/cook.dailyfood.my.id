Step-by-Step Guide to Make Delicious Chana dal barfi

The ingredients needed to make Chana dal barfi:
  1. Prepare 1 cup chana dal
  2. Take 3 cups milk
  3. Make ready to taste Sugar
  4. Get 4 tsp ghee
  5. Take 1 cardamom
Instructions to make Chana dal barfi:
  1. Wash chana dal and make it dry with a cloth
  2. Now take a pan,add two table spoon ghee and add chana dal,cook it till it becomes crispy and changes colour,ghee will left here
  3. Let it cool down and grind it in grinder by adding cardamom into it
  4. Now take a wok or pan, add sugar and milk into it and let the milk boil and mix the sugar into it
  5. After bringing it to one boil, add dal paste that is grinded in grinder and add remaining ghee that was left add that in milk now and 1 tsp ghee more
  6. Cook it untill it becomes thick It's done
  7. Take a big plate and apply ghee on the pan and spread the mixture on plate and cut it into desired shape
  8. Now garnish it with almonds
