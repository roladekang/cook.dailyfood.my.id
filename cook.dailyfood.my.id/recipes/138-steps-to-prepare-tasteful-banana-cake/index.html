  3. Get 1/4 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  4. Make ready 1 tsp baking powder
  5. Get 3 eggs
  6. Prepare I tsp vanilla essence
  7. Get I tsp cinnamon
  8. Get 1 /2 cup butter
