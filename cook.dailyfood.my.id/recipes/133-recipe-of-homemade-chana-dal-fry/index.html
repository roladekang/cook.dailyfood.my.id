The ingredients needed to make Chana dal fry:
  1. Get 100 gm chana dal
  2. Prepare 1 tablespoon ghee
  3. Take 2 tomato
  4. Get 1/2 Capsicum
  5. Get 1/2 tsp jeera( cumin)seeds
  6. Prepare pinch hing/asafoetida
  7. Take 1/2 tsp haldi/ Turmeric Powder
  8. Prepare 2 Green chilli
  9. Take 1 tsp Ginger
Steps to make Chana dal fry:
  1. Boil the daal.with salt and termaric.
  2. Grind tomato ginger and chilli
  3. Heat the oil in pan.put cumin seed hing. Add the tomato paste cook untill oil came out put boiled daal. Cook untill they boil. Serve it with boiled rice
