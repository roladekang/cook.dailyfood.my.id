The ingredients needed to make Dalgona coffee:
  1. Prepare 2 tsp- Instant Coffee
  2. Make ready 2 tsp or more- Sugar
  3. Take 3 tbsp- Hot Water
  4. Prepare as needed -Cold Milk
  5. Take Ice Cubes -as needed
  6. Make ready Sweetener for milk if needed (Optional)
Instructions to make Dalgona coffee:
  1. Take coffee, sugar, hot water in a bowl. Mix well.
  2. Use a hand blender to whip this till it gets light and fluffy. It will take around 3 to 5 mins.
  3. Now take a serving glass. Fill with ice. Add in sweetened milk.
  4. Top with the whipped coffee.
  5. Sprinkles some coffee and serve.
  6. Mix well and enjoy.
