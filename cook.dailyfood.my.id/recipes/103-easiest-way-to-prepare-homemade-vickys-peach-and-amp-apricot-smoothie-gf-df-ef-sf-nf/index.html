The ingredients needed to make Vickys Peach & Apricot Smoothie, GF DF EF SF NF:
  1. Make ready 2 medium bananas, cut into chunks and frozen overnight
  2. Prepare 1 peach, sliced and frozen overnight
  3. Make ready 2 apricots
  4. Make ready 300 ml light coconut milk (or milk of choice)
  5. Take 2 tbsp milled flaxseed (linseed)
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  7. Prepare to taste maple syrup or agave nectar
Steps to make Vickys Peach & Apricot Smoothie, GF DF EF SF NF:
  1. Put all the ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth. Pour into a glass and enjoy!
