The ingredients needed to make Khaman Dhokla:
  1. Take 1 cup besan
  2. Get 1 tsp suji
  3. Make ready 1 packet Eno
  4. Make ready Salt as per taste
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup Curd
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup water
  7. Get Haldi little
  8. Prepare 2 tsp Oil for tadka
  9. Get 2 tsp sugar
  10. Get For tadka
  11. Make ready 1 tsp Mustard seeds
  12. Take 1 cup grated coconut

Steps to make Khaman Dhokla:
  1. First in bowl add besan,suji curd and mix and keep for half hour then add eno along salt, sugar give a nice mix
  2. Grease an plate with oil and put this batter in the plate or any bowl and steam for at least 20 minutes check in between with knife
  3. As your khaman is ready take oil heat it nicely and add mustard seeds, curry leave and put this tadka on khaman garnish it with coconut and coriander leaves
