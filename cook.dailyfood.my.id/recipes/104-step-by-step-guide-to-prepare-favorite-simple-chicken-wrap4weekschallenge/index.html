The ingredients needed to make Simple chicken wrap#4weekschallenge:
  1. Get 1 chapati
  2. Prepare 100 gram pre-cooked chicken cuts
  3. Get 1 large onion sliced
  4. Make ready 1 green capsicum sliced
  5. Take 1 large tomato sliced
  6. Make ready Lettuce
  7. Get Oil
  8. Make ready Salt
  9. Make ready Light soy sauce
Instructions to make Simple chicken wrap#4weekschallenge:
  1. Set all your ingredients together.
  2. Saute the chicken and vegetables in low heat. Add soy sauce, salt and stir.
  3. Once ready put them in a separate bowl and let them cool.
  4. Place the lettuce on top of the chapati, add the saluted vegetables and wrap.
  5. Serve when hot. Enjoy with a cup of ginger tea
