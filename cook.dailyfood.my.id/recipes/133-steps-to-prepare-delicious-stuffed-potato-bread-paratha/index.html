The ingredients needed to make Stuffed potato bread üçû paratha !:
  1. Make ready 3 boiled potatoes
  2. Make ready 8-10 bread slices
  3. Prepare half tsp red chilli powder
  4. Take half tsp salt
  5. Make ready 1/4 tsp chaat masala
  6. Take 1-2 chopped green chillies
  7. Take Few leaves chopped green coriander
  8. Prepare Oil or ghee for Cooking
Steps to make Stuffed potato bread üçû paratha !:
  1. First of all in a bowl prepare potato mixture
  2. Add all the spices red chilli powder, salt, green coriander leaves and green chilli
  3. Now take a bread slice cut the corners and roll each slice with the help of rolling pin make it flat
  4. Now put potato mixture in one slice and cover with another slice and stick with little bit water and press all the corners sealed it
  5. Now in slow flame cook it on tawa make it golden brown from both sides put ghee on the both sides
  6. Now serve hot and enjoy with your favourite chutney or as it is
