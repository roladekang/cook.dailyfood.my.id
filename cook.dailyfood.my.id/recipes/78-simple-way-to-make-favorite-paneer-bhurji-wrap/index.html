The ingredients needed to make Paneer bhurji wrap:
  1. Prepare 100 grams Crumble paneer
  2. Prepare 1 Capsicum roughly chopped
  3. Get 1 Tomato roughly chopped
  4. Take 1 Onion roughly chopped
  5. Get 1 tbsp Oil
  6. Get 1/2 tsp haldi( Turmeric) powder
  7. Get 1 tsp Red chilli powder
  8. Get 1 tsp Coriander cumin powder
  9. Make ready To taste Salt
  10. Make ready 1 tsp Garam masala
  11. Get 1 tbsp tomato sauce
  12. Make ready 1 Cheese cube
  13. Make ready 1 tbsp Butter
  14. Prepare 1 tbsp Shredded cabbage
  15. Take 4 Ready wheat roti

Instructions to make Paneer bhurji wrap:
  1. Take a pan. - Heat oil in it. - Add onion and capsicum and saute it translucent. - Then add tomatoes to it. - Add turmeric,red chilli powder, coriander and cumin powder,garam masala and salt.
  2. Mix it well and saute for few seconds. - Add paneer and cook for 1 min on high flame. - Off the gas. - Take a dough and make thin roti of it on rolling board. - Roast it both the side on tawa by sprinkling little oil on it.
  3. Then after take a roti. - Spread butter and sauce on it. - Add paneer bhurji in a center and some shredded cabbage on it. - Grated cheese on it. - Then after fold it from the bottom horizontally and vertically from both the side and pack it with aluminum foil. - And enjoy this scrumptious wrap for breakfast.
