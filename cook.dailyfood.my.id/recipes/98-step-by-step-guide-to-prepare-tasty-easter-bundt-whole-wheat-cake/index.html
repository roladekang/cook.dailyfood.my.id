  1. Prepare 200 gm whole wheat flour
  2. Get 2 Eggs if not egg then take one ripened banana and onecup curd
  3. Prepare 1 cup vegetable oil
  4. Prepare 1 tablespoon baking powder
  5. Take 1/2 tablespoon baking soda
  6. Take 1 cup powdered sugar
  7. Take 2 tablespoon cocoa powder
  8. Get Few drops vanilla essence
  9. Make ready Red raspberries for garnishing

Steps to make Easter Bundt whole wheat cake:
  1. Sieve whole wheat flour in a container
  2. Add cocoa powder and powdered sugar.
  3. Add baking powder and baking soda.
  4. Take wet ingredients in a bowl
  5. Add 2 eggs full and add butter or oil and mix together and whisk properly
  6. Add few drops of vanilla into it
  7. Now mix both the ingrdients properly and go on mixing it by cut and fold method until the paste attains a desired consistency.
  8. Grease the Bundt cake mould and pour the paste into it..
  9. Keep the.mould in the OTG preheated at 180°C for 25 minutes
  10. After 25 minutes check the cake and if the needle comes out clean that means the cake is ready
  11. Demould once it gets cooled
  12. Sprinkle powdered sugar over it and garnish with red raspberries..
  13. Enjoy
