The ingredients needed to make Dum aloo (without onion and Garlic):
  1. Prepare 200 gm potato
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp coconut chopped
  3. Get 1 tomato paste
  4. Make ready 4 green chillies paste
  5. Prepare 1inch " ginger paste
  6. Make ready 1 tsp Jeera whole
  7. Get 3 tbsp mustard oil
  8. Make ready 1 tsp garam masala powder
  9. Get 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
Steps to make Dum aloo (without onion and Garlic):
  1. Boil the potato peel it off. Add whole jeera coconut chopped and fry it.
  2. Grind green chillies, tomato, and ginger and add this to pan saute it the potato and coconut.
  3. Now the dish is ready to serve.serve with Paratha.
