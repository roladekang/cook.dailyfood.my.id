The ingredients needed to make Khaman Dhokla:
  1. Take 2 cups besan
  2. Make ready 1 cup sooji
  3. Take 1 tsp baking soda
  4. Make ready 1 cup curd
  5. Prepare 1 tsp lemon juice
  6. Make ready 1 tsp hot oil
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  8. Take For tempering
  9. Take 1/2 glass water
  10. Get 1/2 tsp sugar
  11. Take 1 tsp mustard seed
  12. Take 1 tsp oil
  13. Take 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  14. Get 5-7 curry leaves
  15. Take 2 green chilies
  16. Make ready 2 tsp chopped coriander leaves

Instructions to make Khaman Dhokla:
  1. Keep soda and hot oil aside and make the mixture of all for dhokhla batter with 1 cup water
  2. In pan or cooker keep water for boiling. Now keep ready oil greased plate or dhokla stand plates
  3. Now add soda and hot oil and mix fast and pour the mixture in greased plate
  4. Cook it till toothpick comes clean
  5. For tempering- keep small pan on medium flame, add oil and give tadka with seeds, curry leaves and chilies
  6. Now add water, sugar and salt and heat little
  7. Pour this mixture on dhokala, garnish with coriander
