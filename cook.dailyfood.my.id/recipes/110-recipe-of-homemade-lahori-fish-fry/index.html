The ingredients needed to make Lahori Fish fry:
  1. Make ready For Marination:
  2. Prepare 400 gms fish cut in cubes
  3. Take 1 tsp minced garlic
  4. Prepare 1/2 lemon squeezed
  5. Get 1 tsp soya sauce
  6. Take For Spice mix:
  7. Take 1/4 tsp ajwain
  8. Make ready 1/4 tsp coriander seeds
  9. Prepare 1/4 tsp fennel seeds
  10. Take 1/4 tsp cumin
  11. Get For fish batter:
  12. Prepare 1 tsp spice mix
  13. Get to taste Salt
  14. Take 1 tsp chilli powder
  15. Take 4 tbsp gram flour
  16. Take 2 tsp rice flour

Instructions to make Lahori Fish fry:
  1. Mix all the ingredients for marinate and let it ait for 1 hour.
  2. Make the spice mix using all the ingredients given in spice mix.
  3. Mix all the powders for batter together.
  4. Pat the fish in the powder mix. Do not add water. Just pat it and drop in hot kadai at medium flame.
  5. Deep fry it and cook properly both sides.
  6. Serve it with hreen chutney chutney or tomato ketchup.
