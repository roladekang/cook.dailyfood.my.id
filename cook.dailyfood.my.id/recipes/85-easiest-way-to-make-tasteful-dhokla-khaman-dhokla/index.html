The ingredients needed to make Dhokla / Khaman Dhokla:
  1. Get 2 cups Gram flour (baisan)
  2. Prepare 1 tsp Turmeric powder (haldi)
  3. Make ready 1-1/4 cup water or as required
  4. Make ready 1 tsp Icing Sugar
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp Semolina/Rava/Suji
  6. Get 2 tbsp Yogurt
  7. Take 1/2 tsp Citric acid (nimbo Salt/ tatri)
  8. Take to taste Salt
  9. Take 1 tsp Baking soda
  10. Get Soaking ingredients
  11. Take 1/4 cup water
  12. Make ready 1/2 tsp Sugar
  13. Prepare 1/4 tsp Salt
  14. Make ready 1 tbsp Lemon juice
  15. Prepare Bhagar ingredients
  16. Get 2 tbsp Oil
  17. Take 2 tbsp Mustard seed (rai dana)
  18. Get 2 tbsp Cumin seeds (Zeera)
  19. Make ready 15-20 Curry leaves

Steps to make Dhokla / Khaman Dhokla:
  1. In a bowl mix all ingredients except baking soda and rest for 15-30 minutes - Meanwhile ready ur steamer 1. Grease ur cake pan & transfer this mixture in it - Add baking soda and top with few drops of lemon juice, mix this foamy texture in all ur batter (do it quickly) & place it on ready (preheated)steamer
  2. Check it after 10-15 minutes, steam it until toothpick comes out clean 1. Meanwhile in a saucepan add all soaking ingredients cook until sugar dissolved, strain & set aside 1. When dhokla is ready add this mixture on top lil by lil with spoon (don’t need too soggy)
  3. When cool Cut in ur desired shape spread it on ur serving dish 1. In a pan heat oil add all bhagar ingredients,when it’s sizzling add on top of ur dhokla
  4. Decorate with chilli flakes, desiccated coconut & fresh coriander - Serve with raita, coconut chutney, lehsan chutney - Tadda - Enjoy
