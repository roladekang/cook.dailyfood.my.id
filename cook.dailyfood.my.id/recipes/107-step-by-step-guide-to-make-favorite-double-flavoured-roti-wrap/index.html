The ingredients needed to make #Double Flavoured Roti Wrap:
  1. Take 2 Rotis (prepared with spinach purée dough and plain dough)
  2. Make ready 1 carrot medium, shredded
  3. Make ready 1 onion thinly sliced
  4. Take 1 tomato deseeded and thinly sliced
  5. Prepare as needed Lettuce or cabbage
  6. Prepare 1 green chilli chopped
  7. Prepare 100 grams Paneer
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup cream cheese
  9. Get 1-2 garlic cloves minced
  10. Take as needed Lemon juice
  11. Get as needed Pepper powder
  12. Take 1/4-1/2 tsp Mustard powder or ground mustard
  13. Get as needed Salt
  14. Take 1-2 tbsp Olive oil
Instructions to make #Double Flavoured Roti Wrap:
  1. Wash and shreded the veggies as desired.
  2. Mix together cheese or mayo with garlic, mustard, pepper and lemon juice. The spread is ready.
  3. Prepare two rotis by mixing spinach purée dough and plain dough it gives good taste.Take some plain dough and some dough prepared with spinach purée,then combine them and make rotis.
  4. Spread the mix evenly over the roti, including the edges.
  5. Place the lettuce and then the shredded veggies,paneer cubes over the rotis.
  6. Begin to roll and just stick up the edge.
  7. Make a thin stripe with dough to show knotted the wrap.Garnish the knot with Paneer cube and sauce.
  8. Serve immediately.
