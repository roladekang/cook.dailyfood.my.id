The ingredients needed to make Oreo Monster Shake:
  1. Take 1 Cup Milk
  2. Prepare 1 Cup vanilla ice cream
  3. Prepare 1 Cup oreo
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp melted chocolate
  5. Get For garnishing
  6. Prepare 1 piece brownie
  7. Prepare 3-4 mini oreos
  8. Take Some Chocolate chips
  9. Prepare 1 Perk chocolate
Instructions to make Oreo Monster Shake:
  1. Set all the ingredients. I use mini chocolate filled oreos in the shake. Melted chocolate and vanilla ice cream
  2. Grind all the ingredients with 1 cup of milk. I use olpers.
  3. Now garnish it with a piece of brownie ice cream and chocolate chips.
