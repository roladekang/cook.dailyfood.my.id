The ingredients needed to make Biscuit cake:
  1. Make ready 5 Britannia marie biscuit pieces
  2. Get 5 Oreo biscuit pieces
  3. Prepare 5 bourbon pieces
  4. Prepare 100 ml boiled milk
  5. Get 4 tablespoons sugar
  6. Make ready 1/2 tablespoon baking soda
  7. Get Little butter or ghee for grease
Instructions to make Biscuit cake:
  1. Powder all the biscuits in a mixer jar and then add milk,sugar, baking soda again run mixer jar for a second
  2. Take a pressure cooker add 250 gms salt and heat it in a sim manner for 5 minutes
  3. Take a bowl (steel) grease it with ghee and pour the mixture in it
  4. In a pressure cooker keep a small stand or pressure cooker plate, then keep the bowl on it and then close the lid.No need to put whistle, let it be for half an hour.
  5. After half an hour transfer it to a plate and cut into pieces,if we want we can add chocolate sauce on top.
  6. Delicious biscuit cake is ready.
