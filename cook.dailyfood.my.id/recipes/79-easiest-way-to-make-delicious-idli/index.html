Easiest Way to Make Delicious Idli

by Leila McGuire

Hey everyone, I hope you’re having an amazing day today. Today, I will show you a way to prepare a special dish, idli. It is one of my favorites. For mine, I am going to make it a bit unique. This will be really delicious.

Idli is one of the most well liked of recent trending foods in the world. It’s simple, it is fast, it tastes delicious. It is appreciated by millions daily. Idli is something which I have loved my whole life. They’re fine and they look wonderful.

To begin with this recipe, we have to first prepare a few components. You can have idli using 21 ingredients and 3 steps. Here is how you cook it.

The ingredients needed to make Idli:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup Urad dal
  2. Take 2.5 cup Idli rice/White rice
  3. Take to taste Salt
  4. Make ready Poha (optional) (1/4 cup)
  5. Make ready Fenugreek seeds 1/2 tspn
  6. Get Dry yeast (needed in cold country)
  7. Get Fermenting and steaming steps
  8. Prepare Wash and soak urad dal and rice
  9. Make ready (6 hours)
  10. Take Grind dal,rice,fenugreek seeds (add yeast and salt) mix well
  11. Make ready Set the grinded/blended smooth batter aside in warm place
  12. Make ready Let it ferment and rise for 8 to 14 hours(depending the weather)
  13. Make ready (The batter need to ferment untill it rises)
  14. Get On a high flame, bring water to boil in an idli steamer pot
  15. Take Mix the idli batter gently
  16. Prepare Pour into the batter into greased tray
  17. Prepare Cover the pot with lid
  18. Prepare Let the idly steam for 10 minutes
  19. Prepare Once done, Remove the idly trays
  20. Prepare Let it cool down for 1 minutes
  21. Get With a help of a spoon remove idli from the tray
Steps to make Idli:
  1. Soak & Grind urad dal & rice into a smooth batter
  2. Ferment for 8 to 14 hours. Let it rise. Steam idlis. (8 minutes per tray), (i used steamer sheets)
  3. Idli can be served with any curry or chutney
