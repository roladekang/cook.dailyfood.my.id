The ingredients needed to make 'V' easy banana cake:
  1. Take 125 grams butter
  2. Prepare 150 grams caster sugar
  3. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract
  4. Prepare 1 egg beaten
  5. Take 2 very ripe bananas mashed
  6. Make ready 190 grams self raising flour
  7. Take 60 ml milk

Steps to make 'V' easy banana cake:
  1. grease and line a 2 lb loaf tin, melt butter , sugar and vanilla in a saucepan over a medium heat
  2. remove from heat and add the mashed bananas, and mix well.
  3. add the egg and mix well
  4. stir in flour and milk
  5. pour into prepared tin, sprinkle with a tablespoon of demerara sugar to give a crunchy topping if liked
  6. bake at 150 c fan oven (or 170 c regular, gas mark 3 for 35 mins, until a skewer comes out clean. leave to cool and enjoy
