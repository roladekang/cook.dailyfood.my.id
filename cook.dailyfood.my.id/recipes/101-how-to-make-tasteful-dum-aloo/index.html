The ingredients needed to make Dum Aloo:
  1. Take 4-6 medium potato cut in halves
  2. Take 2 onion chopped
  3. Get 1 onion chopped
  4. Prepare 1 green chilli slitted
  5. Take 1/2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  6. Get 1 tbsp cumin powder
  7. Get 1 tbsp coriander powder
  8. Make ready 1/2 tbsp chilli powder
  9. Take 1/2 tbsp Garam masala
  10. Get 2 tbsp sunflower oil
  11. Take 1 tbsp Cumin seeds
  12. Get 1 cup green peas
Steps to make Dum Aloo:
  1. First you have to cut the potatoes and veggies..
  2. Now turn on the gas, and take a bowl boil the potato cubes..Now keep aside…Take a pan fry them in oil.
  3. Now in the same pan, add cumin seeds, and onion,tomatoes, green chili…fry them.Now add peas in it and fry them.
  4. Now add the masalas,give lil water and cook them for a min till the veggies and masalas well mixed..Now add the fried potato cubes..Mix them well and cover the lid cook it for 5 mins more till the potatoes soft.
