Recipe of Delicious Fruit Chaat

The ingredients needed to make Fruit Chaat:
  1. Get 2 Apples
  2. Make ready 2 Bananas
  3. Make ready 1 bunch grapes
  4. Take 4-5 Dates
  5. Get 1 Peach
  6. Take 1 tbsp Sugar
  7. Take 1 tsp Chaat Masala
  8. Prepare 1 lemon
Steps to make Fruit Chaat:
  1. Cut all the fruits according to your shape in a bowl.
  2. Add sugar,lemon juice and chat masala in a small bowl and mix it untill sugar gets dissolved.
  3. Now add this syrup in the bowl of fruits..Mix well.
  4. Keep the fruit chaat in fridge for a while and then serve chilled.
