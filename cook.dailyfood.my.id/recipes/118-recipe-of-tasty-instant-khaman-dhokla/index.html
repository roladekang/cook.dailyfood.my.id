The ingredients needed to make Instant khaman dhokla:
  1. Take 1 1/2 cup gram flour
  2. Make ready 1 cup curd
  3. Take 1 tsp salt
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  5. Prepare as needed Water
  6. Make ready 1 packet Eno
  7. Prepare For tempering
  8. Take 2 tsp oil
  9. Prepare 1 tsp mustard seeds
  10. Make ready 2 tsp water
  11. Prepare 1/2 tsp lemon juice
  12. Make ready as needed Coriander leaves for garnishing

Steps to make Instant khaman dhokla:
  1. Mix all the ingredients except for tempering and water.
  2. Mix well to form a semi thick batter. Add little water if required. Keep aside for 5-10mins.
  3. Heat a steamer. Grease the tin.
  4. Add Eno to the batter and give a quick stir. Pour in tin and steam it for 15-20mins
  5. After cooling, de mould it and cut it in required shape.
  6. For tempering heat oil, add mustard seeds. Let them splutter. Till that time mix water and lemon juice. Add this to tempering and spread this mixture on dhokla.
  7. Your dhokla is ready to serve.
