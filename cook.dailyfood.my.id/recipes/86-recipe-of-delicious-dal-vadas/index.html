Recipe of Delicious Dal Vadas

The ingredients needed to make Dal Vadas:
  1. Take 1/2 kg Bengal Gram or Chana Daal
  2. Make ready 2 tsp Salt
  3. Prepare 1 tsp Turmeric powder
  4. Take 1 tbsp Red chilli powder
  5. Get 1 " Ginger shredded
  6. Make ready 7-8 Green Chillies finely chopped
  7. Make ready 2 Sprigs Curry Leaves torn
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp Coriander Seeds partially crushed
  9. Get 1/8 tsp Asafoetida
  10. Take 1 small bunch Coriander Leaves finely chopped
  11. Make ready 1 Onion finely chopped
  12. Get 1 bunch Spring Onions finely chopped
  13. Make ready 4-5 tbsp Bengal Gram or Chana Daal reserved
Instructions to make Dal Vadas:
  1. Grind dal with all the masalas except the, Onions, Coriander Leaves and Spring Onions.
  2. Later on mix the other ingredients. After mixing well, keep the dal batter aside to let the flavours infuse well. Shape them into small vadas and flatten them a little with your hands. Deep fry these vadas in hot Oil on a low to medium high heat. (Care should be taken for the first 2 minutes and the vadas should be left undisturbed.)
  3. After a minute or two, slowly turn them to the other side. Serve them hot with Green Chutney or any dipping sauce of your choice. Enjoy!
