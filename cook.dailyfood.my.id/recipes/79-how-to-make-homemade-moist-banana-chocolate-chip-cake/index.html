The ingredients needed to make Moist Banana Chocolate Chip Cake:
  1. Make ready a
  2. Prepare 6 medium size mashed banana
  3. Get b
  4. Take 2 C flour
  5. Get 1 tsp baking powder
  6. Make ready 1 tsp bicarb soda
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp cinnamon powder
  8. Make ready (Optional)
  9. Get c
  10. Make ready 180 ml melted butter
  11. Prepare d
  12. Take 4 eggs
  13. Make ready e
  14. Make ready 1/2 c dark brown sugar
  15. Take 1/2 c castor sugar
  16. Get Pinch salt
  17. Take f
  18. Take Banana essence (optional)
  19. Make ready Chocolate chips

Instructions to make Moist Banana Chocolate Chip Cake:
  1. Prepare A (mashed), B (sift together) & C (melt butter). Set aside.
  2. Beat Eggs & Castor sugar till light & fluffy. Add C. Keep beating. Add in F, pinch of salt. Keep mixing.
  3. Next, combine A with the batter. Mix well again. Lastly, add B (sifted flour mixture).
  4. Bake at 160 c for 45 mins. Adjust your oven accordingly.
