The ingredients needed to make Mendu wada with sambhar:
  1. Take 250 gm White udad dal
  2. Take 1 finally chopped onion
  3. Take 1 finally chopped green chilli
  4. Get 1 Tea spoon jeera
  5. Prepare 1 pinch soda
  6. Take Salt as per taste
  7. Prepare Oil for deep fry
  8. Get For Sambhar
  9. Prepare 250 gm mix veg (Pumpkin, brinjal, gourd bottle, tomato)
  10. Make ready 2 Drum sticks cuted in 3inch
  11. Prepare 1 Bowl Arahar and moong dal mix
  12. Make ready 2 Chopped onion
  13. Take 1 Chopped tomato
  14. Make ready 5-6 Curry patta
  15. Take 4-5 garlic cloves chopped
  16. Make ready 1/2 Tea spoon haldi powder
  17. Take 1/2 Tea spoon garam Masala
  18. Make ready 1/2 Tea spoon Dhaniya powder
  19. Make ready 1/4 Tea spoon red chilli powder
  20. Take 1 Tea spoon Sambhar masala
  21. Prepare 2 Tb. Spoon. Oil
  22. Make ready Salt as per taste
  23. Take 1/4 Tea spoon Rai
  24. Prepare 1/4 Tea spoon jeera
  25. Make ready 1 pinch Hing
  26. Take 2 whole dried red chilli
  27. Prepare 2 tb.spoon. jegary
  28. Get 3 Tb. Spoon. imli pulp
  29. Make ready 2 Tb fresh green coriander
Instructions to make Mendu wada with sambhar:
  1. For Mendu wada:—— - Soak udad daal for 6 hr. Grind it without water. Then again keep it farmantaion for 8hr.
  2. Now take udad daal add salt, jeera, soda, onion, green chilli. Mix it well.
  3. Apply some water from the bowl on both your hands. take some batter in your right hand from the bowl. give it a round shape.with your thumb make a hole in the center.
  4. Heat oil in pan. Once the oil becomes hot, pour the medu vada into the hot oil.
  5. Cook on medium flame.drain medu vadas on kitchen tissue to remove excess oil.
  6. For Sambhar:—– - Wash dal, vegetables and cook in pressure cooker with3 cups water for 3 Whistles.
  7. Take pan. Heat oil. add hing, jeera, and Rai. Add Curry patta and dry red chilli. add Ginger garlic.
  8. Add chopped onion then add Tomatoes and drum sticks.
  9. Add all masala powder.Sprinkle some water on masala. fry it nicely on low flame.
  10. Add boil daal with vegetables. - Mix it. Add 1glass water. Add salt.
  11. Add jegary and Imli pulp. Cook it for 10 min.
  12. Garnish with fresh coriander. Serve it with Mendu Wada.
