The ingredients needed to make Fried rice cake | Char koay kak:
  1. Get 2 cups rice flour
  2. Prepare 1 cup leftover cooked rice (need blend to smooth paste)
  3. Make ready 1 cup shredded white radish
  4. Prepare Mix all in pan and stir on medium flame. Once batter start to get thicken, move from flame and transfer to any steaming container, steam at least for 45 min. And chill in fridge at least for 1 day
