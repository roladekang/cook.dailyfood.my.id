The ingredients needed to make Homemade Kashmiri Dum Aloo Curry:
  1. Make ready 2 potatoes (boiled)
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp cumin powder
  3. Get 1 tbsp coriander powder
  4. Make ready 1 tsp fennel powder
  5. Make ready 1 tsp red chilli powder
  6. Take 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  7. Take 2 cardamom
  8. Get 1 cloves
  9. Prepare 1 " cinnamon stick
  10. Make ready 1 " ginger (grated)
  11. Take 1/2 cup curd
  12. Prepare As needed Coriander leaves
  13. Prepare to taste Salt
  14. Get 2 tbsp oil
Steps to make Homemade Kashmiri Dum Aloo Curry:
  1. Washed properly 2 potatoes. Cut into two pieces and boiled in cooker for two whistles. After two whistles. Cool it and open the lid. Peel the potatoes and keep aside. Heat oil in Kadai. Add 1 bay leaf, 2 cardamom, 1 cloves, 1" cinnamon stick. Let it crackle. Then fry 1/2 tsp cumin seeds. Fry for few minutes.
  2. In a bowl add 1 tbsp cumin powder, 1 tbsp coriander powder, 1 tbsp fennel powder, grated ginger, 1/2 tsp haldi powder, 1 tbsp red chilli powder. Mix…properly. Add the mixture and saute it for few minutes. Add 1/2 cup curd. Stir it continuously. Till it become gravy
  3. Add 1/2 cup curd. Stir it continuously. Till it become gravy. Add boiled potatoes. Mix till the potatoes coated with masala..
  4. Add water as needed. Cover and boil it. Till the curry is gravy.
  5. Once it is done. Transfer to a serving dish. Ready to serve…with…nan..
