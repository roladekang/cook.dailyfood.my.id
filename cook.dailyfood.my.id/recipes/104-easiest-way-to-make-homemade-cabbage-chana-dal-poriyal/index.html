The ingredients needed to make Cabbage Chana dal Poriyal:
  1. Make ready 500 gm cabbage (sliced)
  2. Get 100 gm chana dal
  3. Prepare 2 onion (sliced)
  4. Prepare 4 green chillies (slit)
  5. Take 2 Spring curry leaves
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  7. Get to taste salt
  8. Take 3 tbsp oil
  9. Get 1 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  10. Take 1/2 tsp urad dal
  11. Make ready 1/8 tsp cumin/jeera seeds
  12. Make ready 4 tbsp grated coconut
Instructions to make Cabbage Chana dal Poriyal:
  1. Soak chanadhal for ½ hour and pressure cook with ½ cup water for 3-4 whistles
  2. Heat oil in a pan add mustard seeds after it cracks add urad dal and cumin seeds fry till dal is golden.
  3. Add green chillies, curry leaves and onion. Saute till translucent. Add the cabbage.
  4. Next add salt and turmeric powder. Mix well. Add the water from chanadhal and cook the cabbage.
  5. Now add the cooked dal and give a quick stir. Add grated coconut and mix well.
