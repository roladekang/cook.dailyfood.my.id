The ingredients needed to make Dahi Fulki Chaat:
  1. Prepare For Dahi mix (curd mix)
  2. Get 500 ml homemade curd
  3. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Take 1/4 tsp Black Salt
  5. Take 3-4 tbsp sugar (as per taste)
  6. Get 1/2 tsp Chilli flakes
  7. Take 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  8. Get 1/2 tsp Garlic paste
  9. Prepare 2 cups water (approx 300 ml)
  10. Make ready For Fulkis
  11. Take 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  12. Get 2-3 green chillies (finely chopped)
  13. Prepare 1/4 tsp asafoetida
  14. Make ready 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  15. Take 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  16. Get 1 medium size Chopped onion
  17. Make ready Salt as per taste
  18. Make ready 1/4 tsp baking soda
  19. Prepare 1/3 cup water (for batter)
  20. Take Oil for frying
  21. Take For serving
  22. Take Dahi Fulki
  23. Make ready Spicy Green Chutney
  24. Get Sweet and tangy dates Chutney
  25. Get Black Salt
  26. Prepare Roasted cumin powder
  27. Make ready Red chilli powder

Instructions to make Dahi Fulki Chaat:
  1. For Curd Mix: Take big and deep vessel pour curd in it. Add sugar, salt.
  2. Along with black salt, corse Crush cumin seeds and Chilli flakes.
  3. Add garlic paste and water mix it all well. Keep in refrigerator.
  4. Now Fulkis: Take a pan for frying add oil and keep on low flame. Now take a mixing bowl add gram flour, Chopped Chillies, asafoetida, Cumin seeds.
  5. Along with red chilli powder, salt, Chopped onion and water.
  6. Mix without lumps. Add baking soda 1/2 tsp water mix it well.
  7. Oil is hot by now drop very small small pakodis with Ur hands or help of spoon. Fry golden. Now all Fulkis r ready, curd is also ready.
  8. Drop all Fulkis in curd mix. Soak them for atleast 1/2 hr. I soak it for hr.
  9. Now serve: take serving bowls take dahifulkis in it, spread green chutney, sweet dates chutney, some roasted cumin powder, black salt and red chilli powder.
  10. Some coriandor leaves, Chopped onions and Sev Garnishing is done and serve super spicy, tangy, sweet Dahi Fulki Chaat…. It is very delicious and won't resist in one bowl….. Enjoyyyyyyyyyyyy
