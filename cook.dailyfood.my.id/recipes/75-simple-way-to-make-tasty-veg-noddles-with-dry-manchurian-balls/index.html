Steps to make Veg Noddles with dry manchurian balls:
  1. Direction for veg noddles:: First of all boil 6 cups of water with a spoon oil to a rolling boil..add Noddles and boil them for 2-3 minutes without making them mushy…drain to a colander and rinse them well under running cold water.
  2. Sprinkle 1 tsp oil and smear it to the noddles to prevent them from turning sticky..set aside
  3. Add oil to a hot pan and put on to a high flame..Saute garlic and green chillies for a minute..add carrot capsicum onion…toss and saute for 2 minutes until they are half done yet crunchy
  4. Pour soya sauce,vinegar,hot sauce..salt as per taste…mix well…now add cooked noddles…black pepper…
  5. Toss well…and the noddles is readyðŸ˜Š
  6. Direction for dry manchurian balls:: Put the cut pieces of cabbage in the grinder to make it crushed (this is the easy way to save your time)
  7. Same you have to do with carrot
  8. Keep this crushed veggies in a bowl and add salt as per taste, cornflour 1/2 cup and 3 spoon maida..hlf spoon red chilli powder..1/2 spoon black pepper powder and make soft dough without water(salt hlp to release water from veggies..so u don't have to add any water)
  9. Make round balls from the dough…and deep fry it…(don't over fry the balls as u have re- fry them again…semi fried balls are ready
  10. Re -deep fry balls till it changes golden in colour (as this process gives more crispiness to the balls)
  11. In a pan heat oil and add chopped garlic onion capsicum…then add soya tomato sauce..sezwaan sauce…little bit sugar salt..then add 1 spoon cornflour paste(hlf spoon cornflour mix with hlf spoon water)..saute it for 2 minutes…add balls and add 2 spoon water…fry it for 2..3 minutes
  12. Dry manchurian balls are ready to serve..garnish it with Coriander leaves
