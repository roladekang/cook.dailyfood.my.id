The ingredients needed to make Banana walnut chocolate milkshake:
  1. Get 2 banana
  2. Take 1 teaspoon sugar
  3. Take 25 grams walnut
  4. Prepare 1 teaspoon milk powder
  5. Take 1/2 teaspoon chocolate or chocolate powder
  6. Get 250 ml milk
  7. Take as required Chocolate syrup for garnish
Instructions to make Banana walnut chocolate milkshake:
  1. Firstly arrange all ingredients as shown
  2. Take milk, banana, sugar, milk powder, chocolate, and walnuts in a mixer grinder and grind it completely amd make a thick shake from it.
  3. Now your walnut banana chocolate milkshake is ready to serve. Decorate a glass mug with choccolate syrup and then add in the milkshake. Enjoyy
