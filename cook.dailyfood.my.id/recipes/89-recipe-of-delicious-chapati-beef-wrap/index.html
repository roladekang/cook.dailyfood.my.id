Recipe of Delicious Chapati Beef Wrap

by Don Mason

To get started with this recipe, we must prepare a few ingredients. You can have chapati beef wrap using 5 ingredients and 3 steps. Here is how you can achieve it.

The ingredients needed to make Chapati Beef Wrap:
  1. Make ready 3 eggs
  2. Make ready 1 cup beef samosa filling(check previous recipe)
  3. Prepare Salt
  4. Get Oil
  5. Make ready handful Spring onions
Steps to make Chapati Beef Wrap:
  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add onion cook for a min then add the beef filling. Cook till a bit crispy.
  2. Beat eggs separately add salt then add to beef. Let it cook on one side then flip to the other.
  3. Once cooked place the omelette on top of your chapati and roll into a log. Serve with tea/juice/milk.
