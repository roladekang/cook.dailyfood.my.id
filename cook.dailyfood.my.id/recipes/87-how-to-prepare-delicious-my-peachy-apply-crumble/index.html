The ingredients needed to make My Peachy Apply Crumble:
  1. Make ready 4 Apples peeled and chopped
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp brown sugar
  3. Get 4 peaches sliced
  4. Make ready 1 pinch Nutmeg
  5. Make ready 1 pints cinnamon
Instructions to make My Peachy Apply Crumble:
  1. Peel and cut into wedges the apple
  2. Cut up peaches
  3. Add apples and sugar with a small amount of water 1 tbsp water and add to a saucepan and boil and simmer for 5 minutes
  4. When apples are softened add the peach turn low and leave for a few minutes
  5. Add a little nutmeg and cinnamon and stir in..when peaches are softened turn off and let it cool .
  6. Break big pieces up with a fork.
  7. Spread the apple out in a oven dish and cover with crumble flour mix on top.
  8. Make the crumble and spread all over the top
  9. Cook in preheated oven 175c / gas 6 for 10 - 15 minutesQ
  10. Make your custard with almond milk it tastes yummy
