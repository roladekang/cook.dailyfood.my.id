The ingredients needed to make Vickys Slow Cooked Sausage & Bacon Stew, GF DF EF SF NF:
  1. Get gluten-free pork sausages
  2. Make ready bacon
  3. Get tin of chopped tomatoes
  4. Make ready frozen peas
  5. Get carrots, diced
  6. Take leeks, sliced
  7. Get garlic, finely chopped
  8. Get tomato puree/paste
  9. Prepare mixed herbs
  10. Get smoked paprika
  11. Make ready cornstarch mixed with a little cold water
  12. Take chicken bouillon
  13. Take water
Instructions to make Vickys Slow Cooked Sausage & Bacon Stew, GF DF EF SF NF:
  1. Brown off the sausages and bacon under the grill/broiler
  2. Meanwhile add all the vegetables, the tomatoes, garlic, paprika and herbs to a slow cooker or casserole dish
  3. Chop the bacon but leave the sausages whole. Add them to the casserole dish
  4. Add the water and chicken bouillon to the cornstarch and mix. Pour over the sausages etc and give a gentle stir
  5. Cook on low for 8 hours, high for 4 hours or in a preheated oven for 2 hours at gas 6 / 200C /400F
  6. Serve with potatoes and some greens
