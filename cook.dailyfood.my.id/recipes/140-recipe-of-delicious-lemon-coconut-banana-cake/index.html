The ingredients needed to make Lemon coconut Banana cake:
  1. Get 200 g melted margarine
  2. Make ready 200 g sugar
  3. Take 400 g flour
  4. Get 1 egg
  5. Prepare 4 bananas
  6. Get 1/2 TSP baking soda
  7. Make ready 2 TSP baking powder
  8. Get 1 handful dessicated coconut (optional)
  9. Take Lemon essence
  10. Take Half cup milk

Steps to make Lemon coconut Banana cake:
  1. Measure the ingredients and keep aside
  2. Melt the margarine and mash 3 bananas. You can also blend the bananas.
  3. Mix melted margarine with sugar and mix using a spatula
  4. Add an egg and lemon essence then add banana puree and mix
  5. Add dessicated coconut and mix
  6. Add sieved flour mixed with baking soda and baking powder then mix while cutting and folding. Then add milk then continue mixing till consistency is right
  7. Pour in a grease baking tin
  8. Bake for 40mins in an oven or jiko. Oven should be preheated to 180degrees
  9. Yummy
