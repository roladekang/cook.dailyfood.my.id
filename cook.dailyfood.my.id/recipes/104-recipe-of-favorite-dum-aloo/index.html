The ingredients needed to make Dum Aloo:
  1. Get 1/2 kg baby potatoes
  2. Make ready 1 and 1/2 cups water in pressure cooker
  3. Get 1 tsp salt in cooker
  4. Prepare as required Oil for frying
  5. Get 2 tbsp Oil for tadka
  6. Prepare 4-5 tbsp Kashmiri red chilli powder
  7. Make ready 2 cups yoghurt or curd
  8. Get 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  9. Prepare as per taste Salt
  10. Take 1 cup water
  11. Make ready 1-2 tsp garam masala
  12. Get 1 tsp kasuri methi
Instructions to make Dum Aloo:
  1. Put baby potatoes, salt & water in a pressure Cooker. Make 1 whistle.
  2. Cool the cooker. Drain the water, peel the potatoes & poke it with toothpick.
  3. Fry the potatoes on medium flame until golden brown.
  4. Take 2 cups yoghurt or curd in a bowl. Add 4 tbsp kashmiri red chilli powder & salt into it. Mix it well with whisker.
  5. Take 2 tbsp oil in a pan or kadhai add hing, coariander powder, red chilli powder, kashmiri red chilli powder, ginger garlic paste & water mix it well. Now add curd mixture, salt mix it well.
  6. Cook 10 minutes while continuously stirring. Add fried potatoes & garam masala into it. Mix it well.
  7. Cook & cover it for 10 minutes.sprinkle some kasuri methi.
