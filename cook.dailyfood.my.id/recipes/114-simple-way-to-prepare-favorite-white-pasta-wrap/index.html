The ingredients needed to make White Pasta wrap:
  1. Make ready 3-4 chapati
  2. Prepare 1 cup pasta (boiled)
  3. Prepare 2-3 spoon chopped onion
  4. Get 2 spoon chopped tomato
  5. Get 2 spoon chopped capsicums
  6. Get 2 spoon maida
  7. Take 2 tsp pizza sauce
  8. Make ready 1 cup milk
  9. Get 2-3 tsp butter
  10. Prepare 1 tsp black pepper
  11. Prepare 1 tsp mix herbs
  12. Take to taste Salt
Steps to make White Pasta wrap:
  1. Take a pan add 1 tsp butter heat up add 2 spoon maida roasted it till light brown. Add litt bit salt
  2. Now take other pan add some butter add onion sauté it then add capsicums stirring it now add tomatoes.. it becomes soft switch off the flame.
  3. Now it mixture mix it other pan and add the milk boiled pasta and salt, all spices mix it well.
  4. Now take a chapati and spread the pizza sauce and put some pasta,and make a wrap of it with tha help of toothpick
  5. Now serve hot with green çoriander chutney…
