Simple Way to Make Homemade Ghana light chicken soup

by Ruby Mills

The ingredients needed to make Ghana light chicken soup:
  1. Take big garden egg
  2. Take medium sized white onions
  3. Make ready Few dices of red onions
  4. Make ready fresh chicken(please be sure to use live chicken and native one if possible)
  5. Prepare Salt
  6. Make ready medium sized fresh tomatoes
  7. Make ready seeds yellow scotch bonnet pepper
  8. Take seeds green u ripe scotch bonnet pepper
  9. Get tomato paste
  10. Take deseeded uda spice
  11. Take garlic(optional)
  12. Prepare Big cut of ginger
  13. Take Seasoning
  14. Take For the swallow
  15. Prepare Boil cassava and unripe plantain and pound in a mortar just like pounded yam
Instructions to make Ghana light chicken soup:
  1. Wash and place cleaned pieced chicken in a pot and add the grinded ginger and garlic with seasoning, salt and the diced onions.
  2. First stir and cover for the chicken to cook in it’s own juice after which you add more water and continue cooking until the chicken is half way cooked. Then drop in the whole garden egg, yellow scotch bonnet pepper, whole white onions and whole tomatoes and cover to continue cooking
  3. After like 7-8 minutes, remove the garden egg, tomatoes, onions and pepper you added and blend with little water in a blender to a smooth paste and set aside (or pound in a mortar)
  4. Now add the tomato paste to the chicken and stock boiling.
  5. Stir and add the uda spice, green unripe pepper and some more water and cover to cook for about 3minutes
  6. Now use a tiny mesh sieve to sieve in the blended mix and watch as the soup thickens to a light thick consistency
  7. Taste for seasoning and allow to cook for some 4 minutes
  8. Done!
