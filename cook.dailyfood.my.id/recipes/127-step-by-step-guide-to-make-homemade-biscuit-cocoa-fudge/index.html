The ingredients needed to make Biscuit cocoa fudge:
  1. Prepare Marie Biscuits
  2. Get cocoa powder
  3. Get Condensed milk
  4. Make ready Cashews or Almonds as a prinkle on top
  5. Get butter
Instructions to make Biscuit cocoa fudge:
  1. Crush each buscuit into 4 pieces
  2. Break the nuts to be used as a prinkle into pieces
  3. Melt the butter over low flame and add the condensed milk (100g is a small can)
  4. while stiring continously add the coacoa powder
  5. Stir well until the cocoa powder has fully blended
  6. Off the flame and add the biscuits into ( mix to coat well)
  7. When all the biscuits are nicely coated transfer the biscuits onto a tray and sprinkle nuts over it
  8. Keep it in freezer for 3 hours min
