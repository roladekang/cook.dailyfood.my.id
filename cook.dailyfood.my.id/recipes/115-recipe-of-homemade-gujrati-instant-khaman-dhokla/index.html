The ingredients needed to make Gujrati instant khaman dhokla:
  1. Get 1 cup gram flour
  2. Make ready 2 1/2 cup water
  3. Take 1 tablespoon sugar
  4. Take 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  5. Take 1 tablespoon salt
  6. Make ready 1 tablespoon baking soda
  7. Prepare 1 tablespoon refined oil
  8. Make ready 10-15 curry leaves
  9. Prepare 4 sliced Chillies green
  10. Get 1 tablespoon mustard seeds
  11. Get Some Fresh coriander leaves for garnishing
  12. Get As required coconut grated
  13. Prepare 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  14. Make ready 1 pinch hing

Instructions to make Gujrati instant khaman dhokla:
  1. To make khaman dhokla, take a bowl and add gram flour,water,lemon juice,and baking soda in it.mix all the ingredients. And allow the batter to make a balance fermant for 3 hrs. Mean while pour water in a steamer and greased them with the oil.
  2. Pour the dhokla batter in the utensil and cook on a low flame for 25 mins.Check with knife or spoon after 25 mins wheather it is finely cook or not.If it's fine cooked take out from the stove,allow them for 10 mins and cut into pieces.
  3. For tadka :Heat the pan with 3 tablespoon oil in a low medium flame. Add mustard seeds,curry leaves,and slit green chillies,add half cup of water in the pan and allow it to boil.squeeze lemon,2 tsp of sugar. And switch off the flame.
  4. Pour the tadka on the dhokla prepared and garnish it with fresh coriander leaves and grated coconut.
