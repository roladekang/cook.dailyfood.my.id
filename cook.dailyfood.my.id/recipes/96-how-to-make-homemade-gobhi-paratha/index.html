How to Make Homemade Gobhi Paratha

by Alice Sandoval

The ingredients needed to make Gobhi Paratha:
  1. Make ready 1 cup Wheat flour
  2. Get 1/2 cup Oil
  3. Get 2 cups Grated gobhi
  4. Take 1 tbsp Ginger
  5. Take 1 tbsp Garlic
  6. Get 2 tbsp Green chillies
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup Onions
  8. Take Salt as per your taste
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp Cumin powder
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup Warm water
Steps to make Gobhi Paratha:
  1. Firstly to prepare the dough take a bowl and add wheat flour.
  2. Add a pinch of salt and mix it well.
  3. Gradually add warm water to make a semi soft dough.
  4. Let the dough rest for sometime meanwhile prepare the gobhi masala for stuffing.
  5. Take a pan and heat a spoon of oil in it.Add ginger, garlic and green chillies to it.
  6. Now add onions and let it turns aromatic.
  7. Now add the grated gobhi into it by adding salt and cumin powder.
  8. Mix it well and cook it for 3-4 minutes.
  9. Now make small balls of the dough and stuff the gobhi masala into it.
  10. Roll it nicely and place it onto the heated tawa.
  11. Make it crisp from both the sides.
  12. Paratha is ready to eat with chutneys/ curd/ vegetables/ curry/ pickles.
