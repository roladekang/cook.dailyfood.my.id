##### The ingredients needed to make Egg Noodle Lasagne:

1. Get 12 oz egg noodles (x-wide)
1. Prepare to taste Salsa mix/salt/pepper/ seasonings
1. Get  Water
1. Take 1 lb ground beef
1. Take  Shredded queso blanco
1. Make ready 2 eggs
1. Take  Oil to fry
1. Make ready 13 ×9 in pan
1. Prepare 3 tbsp butter




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Egg Noodle Lasagne:

1. Boil noodles according to package directions. Strain and let it drip. Defrost meat. Shred queso blanco
1. Put the oil in a saucepan. Fry your salsa mix and add seasonings
1. Add defrosted meat to salsa/seasoning mix. Cook until brown. Return noodles to cooking pot. Whisk 2 eggs.
1. Oil the 13×9. Mix the meat and noodles in cooking pot. Add butter. Mix. Spoon half into the 13×9
1. Coat with half the queso blanco and half the egg mix. Spread evenly.
1. Spoon the rest of the noodles. Top with the rest of the queso blanco and eggs. Cover with foil and cook @ 400F for 20 min. Let stand until cool enough to eat
