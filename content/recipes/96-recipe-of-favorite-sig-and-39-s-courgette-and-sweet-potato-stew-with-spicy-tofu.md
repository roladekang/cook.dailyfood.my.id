##### The ingredients needed to make Sig&#39;s Courgette and Sweet Potato Stew with Spicy Tofu.:

1. Prepare  Stew
1. Make ready 3 tbsp olive oil
1. Make ready 1 medium onion, chopped
1. Get 5 clove garlic chopped
1. Make ready 2 1/2 inch piece of ginger chopped
1. Make ready 2 small courgette ( zucchini ) chopped into cubes
1. Get 1 1/2 lb sweet potatoes chopped into cubes
1. Take 1 tbsp smoked paprika
1. Get 1 can plum tomatoes
1. Get 250 ml pear, peach or pineapple juice
1. Take 150 grams smooth peanut butter.




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Sig&#39;s Courgette and Sweet Potato Stew with Spicy Tofu.:

1. Gently heat the oil,  add the onions and soften them.
1. Add the finely chopped ginger and then the finely chopped garlic. Cook until soft
1. Add the bite sized chopped chunks of courgette ( zucchini ) and sweet potato, cook until they are almost soft
1. Add the smoked paprika and the cayenne pepper, mix gently but well
1. Add the canned tomatoes , break them down gently
1. Add the smooth peanut butter
