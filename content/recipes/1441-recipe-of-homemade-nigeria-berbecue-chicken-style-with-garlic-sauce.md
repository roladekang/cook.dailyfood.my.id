##### Steps to make Nigeria Berbecue chicken style with garlic sauce:

1. In a bowl,Wash,remove chicken skin and cut the chicken to desirable sizes
1. Wash with water and lemon, blend garlic,ginger and cloves to paste and add to the chicken.add little oil,seasoning cubes,salt,and little oil with the spices into the bowl with the chicken and mix it very well.cover and put in the fridge to marinate for 2hours.for best taste keep it over night.
1. In a baking tray,spread foil paper and grease it with butter.arrange the chicken on the foil,and drizzle some oil on the chicken.
1. Grilled on a preheat oven,on medium heat until done
