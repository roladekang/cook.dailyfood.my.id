1. Prepare  unsalted vegetable stock or water

##### Steps to make Potato, Leek and Pea puree:

1. Warm butter in pan then sauté the leek for 5-6 minutes until just golden.
1. Add the potatoes and stock. Bring to the boil, reduce the heat, cover and simmer for 10 minutes.
1. Add the peas and continue cooking for 3-4 minutes or until the vegetables are tender
