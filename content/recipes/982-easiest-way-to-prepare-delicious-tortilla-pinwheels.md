##### Instructions to make Tortilla Pinwheels:

1. Soften cream cheese in microwave. 10 to 20 sec.
1. Chop green onions.
1. Mix green onions and ranch dressing mix in cream cheese.
1. Spread 2 to 3 Tbs. onto tortilla.
1. Top with cheese and turkey.
1. Roll, saran wrap, and refrigerate for 2 or more hours.
