##### The ingredients needed to make Beef &amp; Broccoli:

1. Get 1 tbsp olive oil
1. Make ready 1 lb round steak thinly sliced into 2 inch pieces
1. Prepare 1 large onion, thinly sliced
1. Make ready 2 cup fresh broccoli florets, chopped
1. Make ready 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of broccoli soup
1. Make ready 1/4 cup water
1. Prepare 3 tbsp soy sauce




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Beef &amp; Broccoli:

1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over high heat. Stir in the steak &amp; season w/ salt &amp; pepper. Cook until beef is almost completely browned.
1. Stir in onions and saute until tender. Stir in broccoli, condensed cream of broccoli soup, water &amp; soy sauce. Blend well.
1. Reduce heat &amp; cover. Cook to desired doneness
