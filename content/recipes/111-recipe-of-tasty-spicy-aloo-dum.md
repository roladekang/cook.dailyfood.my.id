##### Instructions to make Spicy Aloo Dum:

1. In a kadai add oil and fry Aloo.
1. In the same oil add cumin seeds.onces they splutter add Dalchini, elaichi, fennel,onion,ginger garlic and fry for 5-6 min.
1. Now add spices like red chilli powder,coriander powder, turmeric,salt.
1. Continuously fry till oil seperates.
1. Turn the flame low and add curd,give a good mix.
1. Add 1 cup of water as required to adjust consistency.
1. Finally add fried aloo and cook for 20 min.
1. At the end add kasuri meethe.
1. Finally garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
